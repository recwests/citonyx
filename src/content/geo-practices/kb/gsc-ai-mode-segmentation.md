## Summary
Google Search Console now has AI Mode and AI Overviews filters under Search Appearance. The gap between AIO impressions and clicks is the most direct traffic-loss indicator. Seer April 2026: cited brands get +120% more organic clicks per impression.

## Details
Seer Interactive tracked 3,119 keywords across 42 organizations (25.1M impressions): organic CTR for AIO keywords fell from 1.76% (Jun 2024) to 0.61% (Sep 2025), then stabilized at 2.4% (Feb 2026). Non-AIO queries: 2.74% to 1.62% over same period. Six-lever workflow: GSC segmentation, citation readiness, schema markup, E-E-A-T signals, brand search tracking, Search Everywhere optimization on Reddit/YouTube/Quora/LinkedIn.
