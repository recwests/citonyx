## Summary
Grok displays a unique citation pattern: 45% of sources come from X/Twitter (34% organic posts, 11% verified-only). Verified accounts receive a 2.8x citation multiplier. Posts with 5,000+ likes are 4.6x more likely to be cited; 50,000+ likes achieve 11.2x probability. Average 4.7 sources per answer.

## Details
Unlike other AI engines that primarily cite web pages, Grok's training data and real-time retrieval are heavily weighted toward X/Twitter content. This means brands need an active, authoritative X presence to appear in Grok responses. Social proof signals (likes, verified status) serve as Grok's equivalent of PageRank. For maximum Grok visibility: post original data/insights, build verified status, and engineer for engagement on X.
