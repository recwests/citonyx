## Summary

Averi's March 2026 analysis of 680 million AI citations found only 11% domain overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity. Passionfruit confirmed 12% across three engines. Slate HQ documented citation volume variance of up to 615x for the same brand between platforms.

## Details

- Source: Averi (680M citations), Passionfruit (cross-engine), Slate HQ (300K+ citations for 6 B2B brands, 90 days).
- Per-platform citation profiles looked like different brands — Claude had highest owned citation share (9.1%), ChatGPT the lowest.
- Implication: brands must track per-platform visibility, not an aggregate score.
