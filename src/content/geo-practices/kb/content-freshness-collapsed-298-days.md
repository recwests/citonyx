## Summary
Freshness signal sharply intensified in 2026. GitHub study (May 2026, 153K citations, 5K queries, 6 platforms) found median cited content age dropped from 2.2 years to 298 days. Ahrefs 17M URL study confirms AI engines cite content 25.7% fresher than Google organic top-10.

## Details
Ahrefs 17M citation study by platform: ChatGPT direct citations avg 2.6y, Google AI Overviews 3.9y — a 15-month gap. Perplexity avg 3.2y, Gemini 3.1y, Copilot 2.9y. Zyppy meta-analysis (54 studies) confirms the "25.7% fresher" claim is reliable across studies. However, the widely repeated "3.2x more citations within 30 days" stat does not replicate — it appears to be a single-vendor finding. Freshness helps but is not the dominant factor vendors claim; brand mentions and entity authority still rank higher. Content under 13 weeks old still earns disproportionate citations.
