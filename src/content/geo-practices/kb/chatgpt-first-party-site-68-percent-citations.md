## Summary
Foglift classified 1,430 citations from 375 buyer-intent responses across 5 engines. ChatGPT cites the vendor's first-party site 68% of the time (vs 46-52% for others). Perplexity is the only engine citing YouTube at scale (9.7%). Each engine runs on a visibly different slice of the web.

## Details
Gemini and AI Overviews cite from all 14 classified categories. Perplexity covers 13 (gap: community discussion). ChatGPT and Claude cover 9 each. ChatGPT skips: video, personal blog, review aggregator, community discussion, search-engine cache. Claude skips: video, tech press, community discussion, lifestyle media, developer platform. Only 55.4% of total citations were classifiable, suggesting significant long-tail citation diversity. Quarterly benchmark methodology frozen for honest cross-quarter comparison.
