## Summary
llms.txt adoption grew to 784+ implementations along tech diffusion curve, but faces existential question: no major LLM provider except Perplexity and Anthropic has confirmed using it in production retrieval. MCP (Model Context Protocol) emerging as more robust alternative.

## Details
Presenc AI annual report: early adopters in tech/cybersecurity/blockchain expanded to mainstream SaaS and publishing by Q1 2026. Financial services, healthcare, legal under 10% among top-100. Top-100 brands adopt at multiples of general population — remains a sophistication signal. Zyppy meta-analysis scored llms.txt lowest across all 23 factors (2.0/10). Perplexity publicly confirmed use; Anthropic confirmed for Claude Desktop. OpenAI, Google, Meta silent after 12+ months. GPTBot and ClaudeBot started consuming sitemap.xml in 2026 — sitemaps may matter more than llms.txt. Recommendation: implement as "nice to have" but priority remains robots.txt and quality sitemap.
