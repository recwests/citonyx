## Summary
Across 602 prompts / 21k citations: ChatGPT cites fewer sources but with 0.27 mean influence (high depth); Perplexity cites ~16/answer but only 0.065 influence. Q&A formatting alone did NOT improve absorption — evidence density (definitions, numbers, comparisons, steps) did.

## Details
"Evidence-container design": write modular passages, each carrying a distinct citable claim, with definitions/statistics/comparisons/procedural steps. A dashboard should track 5 metrics: selection rate, citation breadth, absorption score, support quality, coverage equity. Avoid a single visibility number.
