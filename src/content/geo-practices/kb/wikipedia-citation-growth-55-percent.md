## Summary
Wikipedia is becoming a shared trust layer across AI models, reaching 83,191 citations in May 2026 (+55.2% MoM). Visibility score rose 50% and citation rate improved 14.3%, making it one of the strongest multi-metric gainers in Meltwater's dataset.

## Details
NIH/NCBI entered the top 3 sources with 91,227 citations (+41.3% MoM), surpassing Wikipedia and Statista in raw volume. Together these movements suggest AI systems increasingly reward sources offering neutrality, authority, structure, and evidence. For GEO strategy: brands should ensure their Wikipedia entries (where applicable) are factual, neutral, current, and well-structured — not for promotion, but as a foundational trust layer AI models reference independently of brand-owned content.
