## Summary
CiteLens benchmarked 320 buyer queries across Google AI Mode, Perplexity, Claude, and ChatGPT (July 2026). Each engine follows a distinct citation rulebook: Google AI Mode (93% from top-10 organic), Perplexity (89%), Claude (53%, tracking brand search demand), ChatGPT (30% — 70% of sources didn't rank in top-10 of either Google or Bing).

## Details
The 4-rulebook finding confirms that a single GEO strategy cannot serve all platforms. Google AI Mode and Perplexity are accessible through traditional SEO with adjustments. Claude responds to brand authority and Wikipedia-backed sites. ChatGPT requires entirely different source cultivation — community presence, conversational content, original data, and Reddit/Wikipedia mentions. This is the most granular per-engine attribution study to date, with Claude's 58% Wikipedia correlation as a notable sub-finding.
