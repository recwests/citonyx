## Summary
Otterly AI analyzed 1,028,959 unique URLs cited across ChatGPT, Google AIO, Google AI Mode, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot (24-hour window). Average cited URL cited 1.9 times, median = 1. URL length correlation with citations: r = -0.025 (near-zero). Guide pages (/guide/) averaged 2.7 citations, 42% above 1.9 average. Clean URLs (no query strings) = 2.1 citations vs 1.6 with query strings. TLD choice irrelevant (.com, .org, .io, .ai all = 1.7). Path depth irrelevant (r = 0.002).

## Details
Most-cited page types: /guide/ = 2.7 citations, blog posts = 2.0, pricing pages = 1.5. URLs with digits averaged 1.6 vs 2.0 without. Over 50% of URLs cited only once. The most-cited URL was cited 965 times — extreme power-law distribution. AI search engines' citation patterns differ fundamentally from traditional SEO assumptions about URL structure. Single actionable takeaway: organize content under /guide/ paths and avoid query strings, but don't over-optimize URL length.
