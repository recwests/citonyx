## Summary
Presenc AI analyzed 3,800 Claude responses (Q1 2026): Claude weights peer-reviewed publications 3.4x baseline, government/regulatory sites 2.7x, Wikipedia 2.3x. Marketing content scores 0.2x (5x below baseline). Enterprise queries cite peer-reviewed sources at 4.2x consumer query rate. Claude under-indexes Reddit/forums (0.4x) and review aggregators (0.7x). Brands selling to enterprise buyers are evaluated against a higher source-quality bar than consumer brands.

## Details
Source selection probability (baseline 1.0): peer-reviewed 3.4, government/regulatory 2.7, Wikipedia 2.3, major news 1.9, editorial blogs 1.4, brand sites 0.8, review aggregators 0.7, Reddit/forums 0.4, marketing content 0.2. Claude does not expose inline citations in consumer chat by default (exposed via API/Enterprise). The path to Claude visibility runs through earned authority — white papers, research reports, technical documentation.
