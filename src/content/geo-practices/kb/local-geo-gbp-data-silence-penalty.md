## Summary
When AI crawlers cannot verify a business's Google Business Profile data — hours, phone number, reviews, attributes — the business faces a silence penalty in AI-generated local responses. Unverified or incomplete GBP entries are systematically skipped by AI systems.

## Details
This extends the local SEO principle of NAP consistency to AI visibility. AI local search engines cross-reference GBP data against multiple sources. Discrepancies or missing verification signals cause the AI to deprioritize the listing entirely. The penalty applies even if the business ranks well in traditional local pack results. For the Citonyx testbed: ensure GBP is fully verified, all fields populated, and data matches website and citation platforms exactly. Stock photos on GBP increase detection risk — use original photography. Review response rate signals authority to AI systems.
