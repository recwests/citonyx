## Summary
Muck Rack's Generative Pulse research (March 2026) found earned media accounts for ~25% of all LLM citations. Brand mentions are ~3x more influential than backlinks in AI citation decisions (Ahrefs 75,000-brand study). 95% of AI-cited links come from non-paid sources per Muck Rack's 1M+ citation analysis.

## Details
A separate Stacker and Scrunch study (March 2026) found third-party news distribution delivers 239% median lift in brand citations. The convergence of PR, SEO, and AI search means earned media is no longer optional for GEO. Brands earning AI citations in 2026 are placing earned media in publications AI engines trust, not chasing domain authority through traditional link acquisition.
