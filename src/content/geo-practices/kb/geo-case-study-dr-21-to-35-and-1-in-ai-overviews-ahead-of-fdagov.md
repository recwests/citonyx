## Summary
A multi-vertical B2B medical packaging supplier achieved #1 in Google AI Overview citations (1.69% market share) ahead of FDA.gov (1.65%), Fortune 500 competitors Cardinal Health, McKesson, Fisher Scientific. DR grew 21→35 in 7 months (+67%), referring domains 117→339 (+222). 283 AI citations across platforms, branded search +587%. 47% visibility on category queries. First-mover window (Q4 2024-Q2 2026) closing as competitors begin GEO in 2026.

## Details
Tactics: 100,000+ specialized content pieces distributed across industry sites, entity-rich schema linking to certifications, multi-tiered link building, brand mention campaigns on niche forums. Google AI Overview: 79 citations, #1 ahead of 2,000+ domains. 345 keywords improved positions during complete site redesign + Google Core Update. 12-18 month first-mover advantage window identified (Q4 2024-Q4 2026). Typical organic DR growth is 5-10% annually; client achieved 67% in 7 months.
