## Summary
Previsible's third AI Traffic Study (6.77M sessions, 166 websites, Nov 2024–May 2026) found ChatGPT carries 92.4% of trackable standalone LLM referral traffic. Gemini grew 3.2x, Claude 64x (passing Perplexity). E-commerce AI referral traffic rose 37x. AI discovery inside Google (AIO + AI Mode) exceeds all LLM assistants combined.

## Details
ChatGPT's 92.4% dominance in standalone LLM referrals dwarfs all others. Claude's 64x growth concentrated in developer/technical segments and overtook Perplexity in March 2026. Gemini's steady 3.2x growth reflects Google ecosystem integration. E-commerce AI referrals surged 37x, signaling a channel that didn't exist two years ago. Key finding: AI discovery inside Google (AIO + AI Mode) > all standalone LLM assistants combined. Multi-platform tracking is non-negotiable — each engine grows at different rates with different content preferences.
