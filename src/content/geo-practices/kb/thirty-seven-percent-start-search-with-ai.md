## Summary
The 2026 AI + Search Behavior Study (500 active AI users) reveals a structural reordering: 37% start with AI tools first, 60% find AI answers clearer, but 85% still verify via traditional search. AI is the discovery layer; traditional search is the verification layer.

## Details
Why users start with AI: 62% want quick summarized answers, 40% are tired of clicking through pages, 37% overwhelmed by ads and cluttered SERPs, 33% struggle to get straight answers. The journey is now two-step: AI for discovery, Google for verification. People still use search engines for: product reviews/prices (47%), news (44%), images/video (44%), factual info (43%), health (35%). Brands need both AI visibility AND SERP visibility to reinforce each other. Cited brands earn 35% higher organic CTR, creating a compounding effect.
