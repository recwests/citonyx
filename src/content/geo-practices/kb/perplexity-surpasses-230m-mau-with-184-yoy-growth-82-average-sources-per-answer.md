## Summary
Perplexity hit 230M MAU globally (Q1 2026), up 184% YoY, with 78M weekly and 22M daily active users. 18.4M Pro subscribers. Averages 8.2 cited sources per answer — highest citation density of any mainstream AI search engine. 64% of users are professionals using it for work. Perplexity-referred traffic converts at 3.1x Google organic rate.

## Details
UK: 5.8M MAU, 9.1% of adults 18–64 have used it. Average session length: 31 minutes (higher than ChatGPT). Perplexity Pro at $8/month average ARPU across paid tiers. 38% YoY UK MAU growth. Knowledge-economy regions show 2.1x higher adoption. 64% professional vs 36% consumer split. These figures from Perplexity platform reporting, Similarweb, SparkToro, Sensor Tower, and MarGen proprietary tracking.
