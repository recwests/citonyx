## Summary
Google I/O 2026 (May 19) transformed search: AI Mode default for 1B+ users, doubling quarterly. Traditional ten blue links no longer primary discovery surface. Search box accepts text, images, files, videos, and Chrome tabs.

## Details
Five major I/O 2026 announcements: (1) AI Mode powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash as default; (2) redesigned search box (first time in 25 years); (3) Information Agents scan web autonomously 24/7 across finance, shopping, news, services; (4) Generative UI (Antigravity) builds interactive dashboards, tables, mini-apps inside search results — removing the click entirely; (5) Personal Intelligence global in 200 countries, 98 languages. March 2026 Core Update: most volatile ever measured — 79.5% of top-3 positions moved. AI-paraphrased/generic content lost up to 71% traffic; sites with genuine E-E-A-T gained. Google's new AI Optimization Guide and Search Console AI Performance Reports (impressions in AI Overviews and AI Mode) released.
