## Summary
Analysis of ~1,000 pages over 3 months (Jul 2026): Google AI and Bing-powered AI (ChatGPT/Copilot) cite completely different language versions. Google AI = 85% English, Bing AI = 56% German. Same topic, same structure: German page got 776 Bing citations and 2 Google AI impressions; English equivalent got 0 Bing and 1,151 Google AI. Google AI favors structured Q&A and English listicles (55x Google-to-Bing ratio); Bing favors non-English how-to and commercial content.

## Details
Spearman correlation between Google AI and Bing AI rankings = 0.29 (very low). Only 50% overlap in top 50 cited pages. Google AI organic correlation = 0.61. Bing-favored formats: Dutch how-to (0.55x Google-to-Bing ratio), German best-of (0.12x). Google-favored formats: English top/best listicles (55x), definitional 'what is X' (37x), yes/no questions (29x). Content format determines which AI ecosystem cites you.
