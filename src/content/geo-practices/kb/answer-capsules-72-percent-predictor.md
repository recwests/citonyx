## Summary
Analysis of AI citation patterns found that 72.4% of cited content includes a standalone answer capsule immediately after the H2 heading. Placing a direct 120-150 character answer under each H2 is the single strongest content-level predictor of AI citation.

## Details
This finding reinforces the "answer-first" content architecture approach. AI engines extract answer capsules — concise, self-contained responses positioned directly after subheadings. The optimal length is 120-150 characters — enough for a complete answer, short enough for LLM extraction without truncation. Content structured with question-answer pairs under H2s (FAQ-style layout) significantly outperforms narrative-heavy or introduction-lazy formats. This aligns with the finding that 44.2% of LLM extractions come from the first 30% of page body.
