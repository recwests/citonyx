## Summary
Semrush survey found 81% of brands with integrated SEO+GEO strategies report measurable gains vs 36% with siloed approaches — 2.25x higher effectiveness. 60% of marketers are reallocating SEO budget to AI search optimization.

## Details
The integrated approach treats SEO and GEO as complementary: SEO builds traditional visibility while GEO builds AI citation presence. They reinforce each other — cited brands earn 35% higher organic CTR. The 60% budget reallocation trend signals the market shift. For brands still treating GEO as a separate initiative from SEO, the data suggests merging teams and strategies for best results. Effective integration includes unified keyword/prompt research, shared content calendars, combined schema implementation, and joint performance dashboards tracking both organic and AI citation metrics.
