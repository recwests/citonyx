## Summary
GEO monitoring market barely existed in 2024; by mid-2026 dozens of platforms compete. Peec AI scores highest for operators (4.10/5) at ~$89-95/mo with clean daily tracking. Otterly.AI Lite at $29/mo is cheapest entry point. Presenc AI leads multi-platform coverage with 7+ platforms and 6-factor visibility scoring. RankScope covers ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Google AI Mode with real browser automation.

## Details
Critical evaluation criteria: which engines monitored (minimum 2026 set = ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Google AI Mode), statistical sampling vs single-run checks, competitor tracking, sentiment analysis, historical trends. Dedicated AI visibility tools outperform bolt-on modules from traditional SEO suites by average 31 points in scoring. GEOAhead uniquely covers Chinese AI ecosystem (Doubao, Kimi, DeepSeek) plus ChatGPT and Gemini. Traditional SEO tools (Semrush Enterprise AIO, Ahrefs) now offer basic AI visibility features but lack sampling depth. For practitioners: choose depth over breadth — tracking 4 engines reliably beats 7 engines with spotty coverage.
