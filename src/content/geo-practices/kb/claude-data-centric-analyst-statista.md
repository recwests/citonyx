## Summary
Claude behaves as the data-centric analyst among AI models. Statista leads Claude citations at 21,620 — the highest single model-source pairing in Meltwater's May 2026 dataset. NIH follows at 18,640, confirming Claude's preference for structured, authority-led, data-rich sources over social/UGC.

## Details
Per-model breakdown reveals distinct citation personalities: ChatGPT remains institutionally anchored (Wikipedia 9,010, NIH 8,053) with no meaningful YouTube/Reddit citations in top sources. Copilot's B2B profile favors Forbes (12,176). Google AI Mode has highest raw volumes across YouTube, Reddit, Yahoo. Gemini is the most balanced model. Perplexity remains research-engine-like with YouTube top. Grok shows broader mix including Reddit, LinkedIn, Wikipedia. Per-model GEO strategies are now essential — data-rich pages with Statista-style structured data perform best on Claude.
