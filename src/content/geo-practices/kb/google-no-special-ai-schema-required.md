## Summary
Google stated plainly in 2026 that no AI-specific markup, special schema, or llms.txt is required for AI Overviews or AI Mode. In Google's own words, 'GEO is still SEO at the core.' Standard structured data helps parsability but proprietary AI markup solutions are unnecessary.

## Details
This official Google statement (2026) debunks the GEO myth that special AI markup files are required. Google explicitly said no AI-specific requirements exist. However, this does not mean structured data is irrelevant — it still helps parsability and citation accuracy. GPT-4 accuracy jumps from 16% to 54% when content has standard structured data (Data World study). The takeaway: invest in clean semantic HTML, standard schema.org types, and server-side rendering rather than chasing proprietary AI markup solutions.
