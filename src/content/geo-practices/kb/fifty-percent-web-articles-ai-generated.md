## Summary
Graphite analyzed 55.4k Common Crawl articles using 3 AI detectors (Pangram, GPTZero, Copyleaks). AI-generated articles jumped to 36% within 12 months of ChatGPT launch, reached 50% by Q1 2025, plateaued since. False positive <2%, false negative <2% across all detectors.
## Details
Multiple detector averaging used to avoid single-detector bias. Pre-ChatGPT baseline (Jan 2020 - Nov 2022): ~0% AI-generated. 12 months post-ChatGPT (Nov 2023): 35.9%. 24 months: 48%. Q1 2025: 49.6%. Q4 2025: 50.9%. Q1 2026: 49.9%. Plateau hypothesis: practitioners found AI-generated articles don't perform well in search. Implication for GEO: AI models retrieving AI-generated content creates feedback loop that compounds search collapse.
