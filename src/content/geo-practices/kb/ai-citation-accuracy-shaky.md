## Summary
Citation quality is unreliable (best ~66% accuracy, worst <50% per Venkit et al.). Optimize for being the cleanest extractable source to survive verification. AuthorityTech's "AI Citation Accuracy 2026" analyzed 2.2M citations.

## Details
Implication for GEO: evidence placed adjacent to claims, clear entity naming, single-topic focus, and current dated facts help pages survive pipeline stages. Brands should monitor not just whether they're cited but whether citations are accurate. Tow Center: incorrect citations in 60%+ of tests.
