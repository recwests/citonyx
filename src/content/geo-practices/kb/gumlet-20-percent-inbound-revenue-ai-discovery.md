## Summary
DerivateX ran GEO for Gumlet (B2B video platform, 16 months). April 2026: 27% of signups self-reported AI discovery (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude), but GA4 tracked only 0.5%. 83% of AI-aware users arrived via Google or direct URL. 3-layer measurement: onboarding survey, GA4 referrer filter, weekly prompt tracker across 500+ queries (DerivateX, May 2026).

## Details
The 27% vs 0.5% gap shows most AI-discovered users close the AI conversation and arrive via branded search. AI direct clicks convert at ~9x site average. AI's signup influence (27%) over-indexes tracked traffic share (0.5%) by ~54x. ~80% of what determines citation share lives off-domain (G2, Reddit, directories, listicles). Monthly attribution: ~14% (Feb), ~22% (Mar), ~27% (Apr), ~21% (May). Source: derivatex.agency/blog/real-roi-of-geo.
