## Summary
Perplexity cites 13.8 sources per query on average but uses each shallowly; ChatGPT cites 7-8 sources but absorbs them 2-3x more deeply. A single ChatGPT citation carries more influence. 80% of Perplexity-cited content does NOT rank in Google's top 10.

## Details
The geo-citation-lab dataset (Zhang, He, Yao, Apr 2026) analyzed 602 controlled prompts across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview — 21,143 search-layer citations and 23,745 citation-level feature records. High-influence pages: longer, modular, semantically aligned, containing extractable evidence (definitions, numerical facts, comparisons, procedural steps). Q&A formatting alone does NOT improve absorption — evidence must be substantive. Perplexity weighs freshness at ~40% of ranking signal. Reddit ~46.7% of Perplexity's top citations, YouTube ~14%. For B2B brands: ChatGPT rewards depth and institutional authority per page; Perplexity rewards breadth of coverage across multiple pages.
