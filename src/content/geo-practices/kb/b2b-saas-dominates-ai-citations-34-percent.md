## Summary
Xpand Media tracked 50 paid client engagements + 200 control sites (Jan-Mar 2026). Sector breakdown: B2B SaaS 34% (Perplexity leads), DTC 19% (ChatGPT leads), Fintech 14%, AI infra 11%, Hospitality 8%, Healthcare 7%. ChatGPT + Perplexity = 68% of all citations.

## Details
Gemini grew fastest, doubling month-over-month. Claude and Copilot remain niche but disproportionately cite long-form primary research — a signal for in-depth content publishers. Sector-specific engine preferences: B2B SaaS → optimize for Perplexity first; DTC/e-commerce → ChatGPT; Hospitality/Travel → Gemini (local-search heavy). Recommendation: start with ChatGPT + Perplexity for most clients, then layer in Gemini for regulated or local-search-heavy verticals. Median baseline visibility across brands: 8-12% citation rate; category leaders sit at 40%+.
