# State of GEO

## 1. Thesis

The defining reality of GEO in mid-2026 is structural divergence meeting signal convergence: five major AI platforms share only [11% of cited domains](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-11-percent-ai-citation-overlap), yet brands that invest in integrated SEO+GEO strategies report [81% measurable gains](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-integrated-seo-ai-strategy-81-percent-gains) vs 36% for siloed approaches — because every platform converges on entity clarity, third-party proof, and extractable evidence. The question has shifted from "how do I rank for this keyword?" to "does every AI platform know who I am — and can they prove it from my content?"

## 2. The State Now

[AI Overviews trigger on 86.7% of US searches](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-ai-overviews-86-percent-us-searches) (commercial-intent 88.5%), up from 56.9% YoY, reaching 2.5B monthly users — outpacing ChatGPT's 800–900M weekly. On 48–50% of Google US queries, AIOs now appear; healthcare triggers at [88%, education at 83%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-ai-overviews-48-percent-ctr-drop-61). Google shipped a Search Console AI visibility report — [impressions only, no clicks, no query-level data](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-search-console-ai-visibility-report) — and [confirmed no AI-specific schema is required](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-google-no-special-ai-schema-required): "GEO is still SEO at the core." [37% of consumers now start search with AI](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-thirty-seven-percent-start-search-with-ai). Content with author attribution is [4x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-google-ai-overviews-author-name-bio-required); the freshness window was cut from 365 to 180 days.

But aggregating platforms conceals more than it reveals.

**ChatGPT** has the narrowest aperture: it cites the vendor's own first-party site [68% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-first-party-site-68-percent-citations), relies on [Wikipedia for 29.7% of its top cited pages and brand homepages for 23.8%](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency). Citation follows steep position decay — top retrieval result cited [58% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-position-citation-decay-58-14), position 10 only 14%. [GPT-5.4 shows a trend toward own-site rewards: vendor first-party content cited at 74.6%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gpt-5-4-vendor-citations-74-percent). [Hallucination dropped sharply](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gpt-5-citation-hallucination-halved) — GPT-5-mini at 11.4% vs GPT-4o-mini's 45.3%.

**Perplexity** is the freshness engine: content updated within 30 days earns an [82% citation rate vs 37% for content over a year old](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points) — a 45-point advantage. It responds to new content in [5–7 days vs 3–5 weeks for ChatGPT](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points), and [46.7% of its top-10 sources are Reddit](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-46-percent-reddit-sources). Perplexity cites at [17.7% vs ChatGPT's 8.8% and Claude's 9.1%](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-perplexity-cites-2x-more-than-chatgpt-claude) and [converts B2B sign-ups at 1.66% vs 0.15% from traditional search — 11x](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-perplexity-converts-11x-b2b).

**Claude** biases toward premium editorial — NYT, Atlantic, New Yorker at [roughly 2x the rate of other platforms](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-claude-premium-editorial-bias), with the longest freshness window. Claude's AI visibility [grew 64x over its tracking period](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-previsible-claude-64x-growth), overtaking Perplexity. Its strongest model-source pairing is [Statista at 21,620 citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-claude-data-centric-analyst-statista); Claude prefers structured, authority-led, data-rich sources.

**Gemini** is the most schema-responsive engine, citing brand-owned sites at [52% — highest of any platform](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) — and shares only [13.7% of its citations with Google's own AI Overviews](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. Gemini is the fastest-growing, [doubling month-over-month](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent). The Gemini 3 update collapsed the AI Overview–organic top-10 overlap from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gemini-3-collapsed-top-10-overlap); fan-out sub-query mapping is now dominant.

**Cross-platform landscape.** Median cited-source half-life is ~[4.5 weeks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-citation-half-life-4-5-weeks); 40–60% of cited domains rotate month-to-month. [Community platforms capture 52.5% of all AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-community-platforms-52-percent-citations) vs 47.5% brand domains. [YouTube generated 188,863 AI citations in May 2026](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-youtube-dominates-ai-citations-188k), growing 56.4% MoM — the most-cited single source. [Wikipedia reached 83,191 citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-wikipedia-citation-growth-55-percent), growing 55.2% MoM. [B2B SaaS dominates at 34% of tracked citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent); Forrester finds [AI tools are the #1 cited information source in B2B buying](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-forrester-ai-tools-number-one-b2b-source). Top-3 Google pages are [34x more likely to be AI-cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-seo-floor-top-3-pages-34x-cited) than pages ranked 31–100 — traditional SEO ranking still gates AI visibility.

**Economics and measurement.** On queries with AI Overviews, organic CTR drops [61%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-ai-overviews-48-percent-ctr-drop-61); even queries without them saw 41% decline. [83% of AI Overview citations come from outside the organic top 10](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-search-converts-4-4x-better-than-organic). AI traffic converts at [14.2% average vs Google organic's 2.8%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-platform-specific-citation-conversion-rates) — 4.4x — with platform spreads (Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%). Brands cited in AI Overviews see [+35% higher organic CTR and +91% higher paid CTR](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-cited-brand-premium-35-91-ctr). Yet [27% of signups come from AI vs 0.5% tracked in Google Analytics](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-attribution-gap-54x-undercount) — a 54x undercount. [60% of AI-cited URLs never appear in Search Console or standard rank trackers](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-citation-reach-rate-33-percent-seo-tools-gap); Citation Reach Rate averages only 33%. [Unmonitored brands take 67 days to detect AI errors](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-error-monitoring-67-days-unmonitored) vs 14 days monitored.

**Technical barriers.** [73% of websites block AI crawlers](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-73-percent-websites-block-ai-crawlers); [JS-rendered content fails 77% of parser attempts](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-js-rendered-content-77-percent-fail) — static HTML with schema achieves 94% parse rate. From September 15, 2026, [Cloudflare's three-tier classification](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-cloudflare-three-tier-crawler-classification) blocks Training and Agent crawlers by default on ad-supported pages. [llms.txt support is confirmed for Anthropic and Perplexity](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-adoption-2026); adoption remains below 10% in finance and healthcare.

**The schema paradox persists.** One study shows [2.3x citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-2-3x-citation-lift) from FAQPage, HowTo, Article, and Product schema; another tracked 1,885 pages adding schema vs 4,000 controls and found [zero independent effect](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-near-zero-independent-effect). Both are valid: schema amplifies quality but does not create it. Attribute-rich Product/Review schema with populated ratings outperforms generic by [20pp (61.7% vs 41.6%)](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-attribute-rich-schema-20pp-citation-lift). [Comparison tables deliver +34%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift), llm.txt +32%, and FAQ schema +28% coverage lift within 14–21 days. [FAQPage was deprecated for rich result display in May 2026](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-faq-rich-results-deprecated-signal-remains), but the AI citation signal remains active.

## 3. What's Winning

**Entity** is the dominant GEO signal class. The SEO-to-GEO Divergence Index shows brand entity mentions at NIS [0.918 vs domain rating at 0.397](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-brand-entity-mentions-surpass-domain-rating) — more than twice as predictive. Across 75,000 brands, branded web mentions correlate at [r=0.664 vs backlinks r=0.218](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks); the top quartile averages 169 AI Overview mentions vs 14 for the next tier. The [Brand Stature Ladder](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-stature-ladder-73-44-11-percent) shows global brands at 73% citation rate, mid-market 44%, niche 11%. [Entity mass — declared, verified, cross-referenced identity signals — matters more than domain authority](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-entity-mass-architecture-aio-citation); [entity-optimized sites earn 40% more AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-entity-seo-40-percent-more-ai-citations). Google's [LLM patent](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-google-llm-patent-teaching-ai-who-you-are) confirms the SEO goal is teaching AI who you are.

**Review depth** is the single highest-correlated variable: [50+ reviews on G2/Capterra = 3.2x citation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-depth-strongest-geo-predictor). [Review velocity (r=0.72) outperforms star rating (r=0.31)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-velocity-vs-star-rating-citation-correlation). A [B2B SaaS case study](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-87-percent-citation-rate-reviews-pr) went from 12% to 87% citation rate in 4 months by scaling G2 reviews from 43 to 287 plus 14 PR placements.

**Wikidata QID with sameAs schema** lifts citations by [40% with zero ongoing cost](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent). Brands with Wikipedia earn [3x higher citation rates](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency); Wikipedia accounts for [29.7% of ChatGPT's top cited pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency) and [appears in ~47% of factual AI answers](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-wikipedia-47-percent-citations-factual-queries).

**Third-party proof** accounts for [68% of all AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-third-party-sources-68-percent-citations); one source yields 18% lift, five or more yield 78%. [Digital PR drives 25% of LLM citations but only 6% of practitioners use it](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-digital-pr-evidence-gap-25-percent-6-percent) — the widest evidence gap in GEO. An industrial lighting brand achieved [88.6% AI recommendation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-88-percent-ai-recommendation-pr-consensus) — own site contributed 4.5%, PR reprints drove 59.8%. [Press releases collapsed to 0.2% of citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-press-releases-citation-share-collapses); earned editorial at 37.6% is 188x more valuable.

**Content architecture.** [Named-source quotations with credentials](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-quotation-addition-strongest-geo-tactic): +42.6% lift (strongest Princeton GEO tactic). [19+ data points per page](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-data-rich-content-19-stats-5x-citations): 5.4 citations vs 2.8 — statistics plus sources boost visibility 40%. [Answer-first architecture](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-first-content-architecture) matters because [44.2% of LLM citations come from the first 30% of text](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-llm-citations-44-percent-front-loaded); claim-rich introductions get cited 2.1x more. [Multi-format content (guides + comparisons + how-to)](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-multi-format-content-2-3x-citations) earns 2.3x more citations. [Document structure alone drives 17.3% lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-document-structure-17-percent-citation-lift), headings accounting for 44.9% of the gain. [Prompt-content alignment (β=+0.37)](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-prompt-content-alignment-dominant-citation-predictor) is the strongest single citation predictor — stronger than domain authority. [Original datasets](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-original-datasets-highest-roi-tactic) are the highest-ROI GEO tactic: AI cannot synthesize data it cannot access. [Topic targeting replaces keyword targeting](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-topic-targeting-replaces-keyword-targeting).

**Case study convergence.** The [compiled benchmark](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-geo-case-study-benchmark-2026) shows 2,300% AI traffic growth across documented cases, 90–180 day inflection point. Fulton: [zero to $18,164/month in 12 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-fulton-35x-ai-revenue-case-study), 700% in 6 weeks, Google organic doubled as side effect. eCommerce brand: [+340% AVI in 8 weeks](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-ecommerce-geo-case-study-340-percent). B2B manufacturing: [50% citation rate in 112 days without dedicated pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-b2b-manufacturing-50-percent-citation-rate). Snack brand: [75% generative citation rate from zero in 4 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-snack-brand-75-percent-citation-rate-ahe-framework). [Rank Masters: 8,337% ChatGPT referral growth](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-rank-masters-8337-percent-chatgpt). One firm used [AI Discovery Files](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-ai-discovery-files-competitive) to score 99/100 against national brands. The [GainFrame playbook](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-gainframe-playbook-31-percent-chatgpt) — Quick Answer blocks, question H2s, FAQ schema, multi-schema JSON-LD, IndexNow — made ChatGPT the top acquisition channel at 31%. The [Zyppy meta-analysis](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-zyppy-evidence-weighted-citation-factors) scored 23 AI citation factors by evidence strength across 54 experiments.

## 4. Losing Signals

The KB currently flags no signals as formally deprecated or stale. Several contested findings and structural constraints are worth tracking.

**The schema paradox.** Schema produces [2.3x lift in observational studies](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-2-3x-citation-lift) and [zero independent effect in controlled testing](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-near-zero-independent-effect). Both are valid: schema amplifies quality but does not create it.

**Freshness is real but overclaimed.** The controlled advantage is [25.7%](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-25-percent-advantage), not the viral 4.3x figure. But the ~4.5-week half-life makes continuity discipline mandatory regardless.

**Google self-conflict.** Gemini and AI Overviews share only [13.7% of citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. AI Overview–organic overlap collapsed from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-aio-overlap-organic-collapse-76-to-38). Optimizing for one Google surface does not optimize for another.

**Only [36 global brands](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-semrush-universal-36-brands)** maintain top-100 visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews simultaneously. Those outside face structural disadvantages no single content fix resolves.

**Store-bought backlinks** show [near-zero transfer to AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-store-bought-backlinks-no-ai-citation-transfer). [Brand mentions correlate 0.664 vs backlinks 0.218](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks) — the architecture decouples from domain authority. Google's [listicle crackdown (January 2026)](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-google-listicle-crackdown-2026) caused 29–49% visibility losses, and AI Overviews [cite self-serving listicles but recommend competitors 69% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-google-llm-patent-teaching-ai-who-you-are).

**Hallucination trajectories diverge.** GPT-5-mini hallucinates at [11.4%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gpt-5-citation-hallucination-halved) (down from GPT-4o-mini's 45.3%), but Claude Haiku 4.5 increased 8%. Multi-model consensus achieves 95.6% accuracy, but relying on any single platform carries citation integrity risk.

**[Credibility paradox](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-credibility-paradox-ai-visibility-not-trust):** being cited in AI answers does not guarantee being believed (Burson/Profound, 85 companies, 55K+ forecasts). AI Overviews reduced [Wikipedia traffic by ~15%](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-ai-overviews-wikipedia-traffic-loss-15-percent) — causal evidence from 46.5M observations that AI answers cannibalize even authoritative sources.

## 6. What To Do

1. **Audit per platform, not aggregate.** Use [GSC's AI Mode and AI Overviews segmentation](https://citonyx.com/learn/tools#entry-gsc-ai-mode-segmentation). Add dedicated tracking for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini. [B2B SaaS dominates at 34% of tracked citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent) — apply the platform-specific lever.

2. **Build the entity foundation first.** Wikidata entry + sameAs Organization schema. Zero-cost, [40% citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent). Wikipedia presence for [3x higher citation rates](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency).

3. **Shift link budget to mention budget.** [Brand mentions are 3x stronger than backlinks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks). PR placements, G2/Trustpilot reviews, Reddit community. [Review depth (50+ = 3.2x)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-depth-strongest-geo-predictor) and [review velocity (r=0.72)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-velocity-vs-star-rating-citation-correlation) are the highest-leverage activities.

4. **Restructure for passage-level extraction.** Every H2 needs a direct answer block in the first 40–60 words. [44.9% of citation improvement comes from macro-structure](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-document-structure-17-percent-citation-lift). [Answer-first architecture](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-first-content-architecture) is the highest-return GEO content practice. [Original datasets](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-original-datasets-highest-roi-tactic), [named-source quotations](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-quotation-addition-strongest-geo-tactic), and [data-rich pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-data-rich-content-19-stats-5x-citations) compound.

5. **Adopt monthly publication cycles.** [Content <13 weeks is ~2x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-13-week-shelf-life). Perplexity rewards days-old content. [Comparison tables +34%, llm.txt +32%, FAQ schema +28%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift) coverage lift within 14–21 days.

6. **Audit AI crawler access — hard deadline.** [73% of websites block AI crawlers](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-73-percent-websites-block-ai-crawlers). [JS-rendered content fails 77% of parser attempts](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-js-rendered-content-77-percent-fail). From September 15, 2026, [Cloudflare's three-tier classification](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-cloudflare-three-tier-crawler-classification) blocks Training and Agent crawlers by default on ad-supported pages.

7. **Deploy AI Discovery Files.** llms.txt, llms-full.txt. [Anthropic and Perplexity confirmed support](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-adoption-2026). One firm used them to [score 99/100 against national brands](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-ai-discovery-files-competitive). [Google states no special AI schema is required](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-google-no-special-ai-schema-required), but the file suite has become a competitive differentiator.

8. **Budget for continuity, not campaigns.** [$35–60K/year](https://citonyx.com/learn/monetization#entry-b2b-geo-budget-guide-35-60k), 15–30% of marketing budget, 12–18 month payback. The [4.5-week half-life](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-citation-half-life-4-5-weeks) means GEO is a continuity discipline. [Integrated SEO+GEO strategies](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-integrated-seo-ai-strategy-81-percent-gains) report 81% gains vs 36% for siloed.
