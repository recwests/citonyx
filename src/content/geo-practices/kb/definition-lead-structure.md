## Summary
Every major section should start with a concise, self-contained definitional sentence that a model can quote without surrounding context. It is the most extraction-friendly format for AI answer engines.

## Details
GenOptima's stacked-schema framework and Google's fragment extraction (`#:~:text=`) favor semantically complete opening sentences. Definition-Lead sentences avoid marketing language ("premium", "revolutionary") and use factual categorization. Each entity's first mention should get its own Definition Lead. Place direct answers in the first 40–60 words of every section — Quattr found these in the majority of highly cited posts, and Erlin's data shows first-200-word quick-answer blocks correlate with higher citation rates.
