## Summary
ChatGPT grew from 200M weekly active users (late 2024) to 800-900M in 2026 — 4x growth in 18 months. Daily prompts reached ~2.5B, handling ~17% of global digital queries. AI-platform sessions worldwide reached 45 billion per month. Only 14% of marketers actively track AI search performance.

## Details
Data synthesized from OpenAI disclosures, Similarweb, Graphite, QuickSEO, and Conductor. Gemini app crossed 750M monthly users. Gartner predicted 25% traditional search volume drop by 2026. Eight Oh Two's 2026 study of 500 AI users found 37% of consumers start search with AI tools (not Google/Bing). 60% say AI gives clearer answers. 85% still double-check AI answers via traditional search. Search Engine Land's 2026 guide notes GEO conferences, agency specializations, and purpose-built tools ecosystem. The scale shift means AI visibility is no longer optional for most brands.
