## Summary
GEO case study (Sep 2025-Jan 2026): mid-size cybersecurity firm, DA 51, 280 pages. Baseline: 12% citation rate, 0% from own content. Phase 1: fixed robots.txt blocking AI crawlers → 18% in week 3. Phase 2: answer-first restructuring + original research data → 41% in week 8. Phase 3: 8 guest articles on DR 70+ pubs + CISO survey coverage → 71% in week 14. Phase 4: gap-filling → 87% in week 17.

## Details
Branded organic search +340%, direct sessions +28%, qualified inbound +19%. AI crawler blocking fix alone gave 50% relative lift. Three original research articles became highest-cited assets within 60 days. Journalist coverage of CISO survey drove the largest single citation jump. Off-site authority is non-negotiable above 40%: on-site improvements get to 40%, earned media gets to 87%. Three highest-impact tactics: answer-first structure, original research data, high-authority publication coverage.
