## Summary
Multiple sources converge on GEO budget for 50-300 employee firms: $300-700/month monitoring tools (Profound, Otterly, AthenaHQ, Geoperf Pro), $1-2K/month content creation, $15-25K/year PR/guest content on authority media, $5K one-shot Wikipedia article by experienced editor. Total: $35-60K/year or 15-30% of marketing budget. Target ROI: $0.8-1.2 per $1 at 12-18 months.

## Details
Primary KPIs: citation rate (fixed 30-100 prompt panel, re-run weekly), average rank in ordered lists, share-of-voice vs competitors, authority sources cited. Secondary KPIs: LLM referral traffic (chatgpt.com, perplexity.ai, claude.ai in GA4), lead-source attribution via form surveys. Strategic finding: cumulative earned media works for LLMs — brands with well-sourced Wikipedia articles are over-represented. Tools ecosystem maturing rapidly.
