## Summary
OtterlyAI compared GA4 last-touch attribution against post-signup surveys. GA4 credits Claude with 0.1% of signups, but self-reported data shows 10.6%. ChatGPT: 7% vs 11%. Google Search: 35% vs 15.7%. ChatGPT visitors convert at 25% (vs 15% organic) — 66% higher.
## Details
OtterlyAI tracked signups via two methods: GA4 last-touch attribution and a mandatory post-conversion survey asking 'where did you first hear about us?' For Claude, GA4 showed 0.1% while surveys showed 10.6% — a 100x difference. ChatGPT went from 7% (GA4) to 11% (survey). Google Search dropped from 35% to 15.7%. ChatGPT visitors converted at 25% vs 15% for organic search (66% higher). The key mechanism: ChatGPT hyperlinked brand mentions starting May 2026, while Claude shows few clickable citations. Recommendation: add self-reported attribution question at signup, compare both views, treat AI Search as brand-awareness channel.
