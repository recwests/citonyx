## Summary
First large-N multi-engine observational study of LLM citation drivers. Analyzed ~2M citations from ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI, Gemini joined to 10,000 B2B SaaS pages using 9-method consensus. Prompt-content alignment (Jaccard overlap) is dominant (β=+0.37, q≈10⁻⁷³). AEO checklist signals (FAQ, schema, Core Web Vitals) show positive effects that reverse or collapse with domain fixed effects — Simpson's paradox for the AEO literature.

## Details
Across 19 B2B SaaS workspaces over 6 months. Median citation age: 5.1 months (Claude) to 8.0 months (ChatGPT). Brand-controlled URL share: 13.8% (Gemini) to 39.3% (ChatGPT). Claude cites pricing content at median age 3.3 months, ChatGPT at 14.0 months. 96.6% of LinkedIn citations come from Google AI Overviews alone. Domain-level AI authority exceeds strongest non-alignment page feature by 6x in SHAP value.
