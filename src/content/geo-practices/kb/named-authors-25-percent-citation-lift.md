## Summary
Bylined articles from recognized experts are 25% more likely to be cited by AI than anonymous content. Entity authority is the top long-term AI citation factor but hardest to build. Sites with author schema markup 3x more likely to appear in AI answers.

## Details
Zyppy 2025 research: ChatGPT only cites ~15% of pages it retrieves. Named authors carry odds ratio of 1.40 vs 1.12 overall. 85% of brand mentions in AI responses originate from third-party pages. Schema markup accounts for ~10% of Perplexity's ranking algorithm. Sites with author schema markup 3x more likely to appear in AI answers (BrightEdge). Schema markup usage grew 35% from 2023 to 2026. Recommended optimization priority: FAQ sections → JSON-LD structured data → heading structure fixes → external citations → entity clarity → meta tags. For immediate results, focus on content structure and answer directness; entity authority is the long game.
