## Summary
Demand Local's 2026 analysis: cited brands see +35% organic CTR and +91% paid CTR vs non-cited competitors. Mechanism is brand-recall amplification — appearing in AI answers builds recognition before users reach SERPs or ads, lifting CTR on every channel.

## Details
Averi: AI-referred visitors convert 4.4x higher than traditional search and complete in 1/3 the sessions. Visionary Marketing: brands newly cited in AI Overview see +23% branded search lift within 30 days, +47% within 90 days. AIO citation duration averages 47 days before domain rotates. Asymmetry: +23% lift gained in 30 days vs 12% drop when citation lost. ChatGPT dominates AI referral traffic at 87.4% of all AI referrals. Median brand visibility: 8-12%; category leaders: 40%+.
