## Summary
Gemini chat citations skew heavily toward brand-owned domains at 52.15% — the highest first-party citation rate of any AI engine. AI Overviews + AI Mode cite brand domains at only ~13.7%, relying more on third-party and community sources.

## Details
QuickSEO.ai's 2026 cross-engine audit found stark differences in first-party citation rates: Gemini chat leads at 52.15%, significantly outpacing other engines. AI Overviews and AI Mode combine for only ~13.7% brand-owned citations on the same queries. AI Overview content shifts ~70% on repeated queries. Wikipedia accounts for 11.2% of AI Overview citations, YouTube 9.5%, blog.google 6.0%, Reddit 5.8%, google.com 5.6% — Google properties collectively represent 22.8% of AI Overview citations.
