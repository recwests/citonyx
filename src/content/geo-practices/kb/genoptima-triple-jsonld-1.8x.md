## Summary
GenOptima's complete 2026 GEO Playbook found deploying a triple JSON-LD stack (Article + ItemList + FAQPage) yields 1.8x more AI citations than single schema types. Combined with 7-14 day freshness cycles, this approach drove 11.8x citation growth (38 to 449 citations) in 14 days.

## Details
The triple stack works through complementary schema signals: Article schema defines the page's primary content entity; ItemList schema (for listicle content) explicitly structures comparative information; FAQPage schema provides direct Q&A extraction points for AI engines. GenOptima also found 74.2% of AI citations come from "Top N" listicles and 53.6% of ChatGPT responses return no web sources — indicating massive untapped opportunity for properly structured list-and-FAQ content.
