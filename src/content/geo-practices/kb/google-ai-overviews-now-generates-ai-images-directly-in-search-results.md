## Summary
Google launched AI image generation directly within AI Overviews (July 2026) using the latest Nano Banana model. Users can generate custom visuals from text prompts within the AI Overview response. Previously available in AI Mode, now expanding to standard AI Overviews. Rolling out in English for all regions supporting AI Mode image creation. Represents the deepening of multimodal AI search experiences.

## Details
Nano Banana model transforms text prompts into high-quality custom visuals made from scratch. Integration directly in AI Overview responses means brands' visual assets (product images, infographics, diagrams) may compete with AI-generated alternatives. This extends the citation challenge to visual content: even when a brand's text is cited, the AI may generate its own accompanying image instead of using the brand's visuals.
