## Summary
Perplexity pulled all advertising in 2026, staking its entire business on subscriptions. Anthropic drew a hard line: Claude stays ad-free with no sponsored links or product placements. Platform monetization strategies are diverging sharply.

## Details
Perplexity's subscription-only pivot means the platform has no paid placement inventory — all citations are earned organically. For GEO practitioners, this means Perplexity citations are purely merit-based, rewarding content quality, freshness, and community presence. ChatGPT, by contrast, is building an ad business that may eventually offer paid placement in responses. Claude remains entirely ad-free with no monetization path for third-party visibility. Strategic implication: as platforms diverge, Perplexity offers the cleanest organic citation signal, ChatGPT may develop a hybrid paid-organic model, and Claude remains purely editorial. Each requires a distinct approach to visibility investment.
