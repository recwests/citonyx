## Summary
Google I/O 2026 (May 19): AI Mode became the default search experience, powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash. Intelligent Search box redesigned with AI-powered suggestions and multimodal input. Information agents operate 24/7, monitoring web pages, news, social, finance, shopping, and sports — push synthesized updates with source links. Personal Intelligence expanded to 98 languages (connect Gmail, Photos, Google Calendar). AI Mode crossed 1B monthly active users.

## Details
AI Overviews now have 2.5B monthly active users. Search agents will launch first for Google AI Pro & Ultra subscribers. Agentic booking expanded to local services. Google Antigravity brings agentic coding to Search — custom dashboards, trackers, mini apps generated on the fly. Personal Intelligence: users choose which apps to connect, always in control. Seamless AI Search experience: AI Overview → follow-up in AI Mode with context retention.
