## Summary
The Loganix synthesis of six studies covering 680M citations found AI search traffic converts at 14.2% compared to Google organic 2.8% — a 5.1x advantage. By platform: Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%. ChatGPT-referred users spend avg 15 min vs 8 min from Google, with 12 vs 9 pageviews.

## Details
Caveat: Schulze-Kaiser 12-month study covering 973 websites and ~$20B revenue found ChatGPT referrals underperform Google organic on transactional purchases by ~13%. AI referrals overperform in research-heavy verticals (B2B SaaS, finance, electronics) and underperform in impulse categories (apparel, grocery). AI referral traffic = 1.08% of all site traffic, growing ~1% month over month.
