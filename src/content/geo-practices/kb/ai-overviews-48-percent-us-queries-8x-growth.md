## Summary
Google AI Overviews now appear on ~48-50% of all US search queries — up from 6.49% in January 2025, an ~8x expansion in 15 months. Google confirmed "roughly 50% of US queries" in February 2026. Healthcare triggers at 88%, Education 83%, Entertainment 37%.

## Details
BrightEdge and Generative Parser data documented the expansion from ~31% to ~48% YoY. Cited brands earn 35% higher organic CTR and 91% higher paid CTR. Ahrefs reported AIO visitors convert at 23x (12.1% of signups from 0.5% of traffic). However, AIO citations perform at roughly Position 6 click levels. Seer's April 2026 study of 53 brands across 5.47M queries found 120% more organic clicks per impression for cited brands. Organic CTR dropped 61% on queries with AIOs (Seer Interactive). Even queries without AIOs saw 41% CTR decline YoY, confirming structural shift.
