## Summary
AI-cited content is 25.7% fresher than organic top-10 (2.9 vs 3.9 years average). The viral "4.3x more cited for fresh content" claim traces to no primary study. Freshness is moderate evidence (7.0/10 Zyppy score), not a dominant factor.

## Details
Ahrefs measured content age across 16.975M cited URLs on 7 platforms. ChatGPT shows the strongest freshness bias (avg 958 days cited vs 1,432 organic). Content updated within 30 days is cited 3.2x more than stale equivalents (NinjaPromo). Zyppy scores freshness 7.0/10 — below accessibility (9.5), search rank (9.4), fan-out (9.3), and brand mentions. Fix crawlability and brand presence before refresh cadence.
