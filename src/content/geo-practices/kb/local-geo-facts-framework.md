## Summary
SOCi's FACTS framework structures local GEO optimization: Freshness (recent GBP posts and updates), Authority (review volume, response rate, response quality), Consistency (NAP alignment across all platforms), Trust (verified GBP, original photos), Semantic Relevance (primary/secondary category alignment).

## Details
Each FACTS component addresses a specific AI local search signal. Freshness: GBP posts within 7 days signal active management. Authority: responding to 100% of reviews with personalized replies outperforms automated responses. Consistency: every citation platform (Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing, Facebook, Foursquare) must show identical NAP. Trust: stock photos trigger AI detection algorithms — original photography required. Semantic Relevance: primary category must match the core service; secondary categories expand topical coverage. Stock photo detection is a growing issue — AI models can identify stock imagery and penalize local trust scores. For the Citonyx testbed: implement all five FACTS components.
