## Summary
Tempeh snack brand ranked #1 on Google but had 0% AI citation rate. Jet Digital Pro's AHE (Authority, Helpful, Engaging) Framework transformed visibility in 4 months: 60% AIO, 60% Perplexity, 30% ChatGPT citation rate.

## Details
Despite ranking #1 on Google for high-intent snack queries, the brand had 0% citation rate across Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT. AHE Framework applied: direct-answer blocks (1-3 sentences within first 120 words), embedded extractable numerical facts (ingredient count, cooking time), FAQ & HowTo Schema, contextual internal linking. Results after 4 months: 75% Generative Citation Rate across 20 target queries. Measurable lift in branded search interest. Backlinks from plant-based community blogs supplemented the strategy. Confirms that traditional Google ranking ≠ AI citation presence.
