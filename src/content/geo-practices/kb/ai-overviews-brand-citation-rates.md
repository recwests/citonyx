## Summary
OtterlyAI's analysis of 1+ million citations (Jan-Feb 2026) reveals stark platform-level brand citation differences: Google AI Overviews cites brands at 59.8%, ChatGPT at 44.7%, and Perplexity at only 28.9%. Google provides the highest number of clickable link citations. ChatGPT mentions brands frequently but links weakly — creating awareness without direct traffic.

## Details
Perplexity leans heavily on Reddit and community forums (16.9% of citations) and offers balanced mention-to-citation ratios, making it potentially more valuable for driving traffic. Google AI Overviews show the strongest brand preference but only appear on ~33% of queries. ChatGPT favors Reddit, Wikipedia, and news sites — brands get mentioned but receive weak link citations. Strategic implication: a monolithic AI SEO strategy fails because each platform's citation environment demands a separate approach. For Perplexity: community presence and press releases. For ChatGPT: Wikipedia and authoritative news. For AI Overviews: traditional SEO signals and broad content distribution.
