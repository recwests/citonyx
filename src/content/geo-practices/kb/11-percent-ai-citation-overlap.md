## Summary

ChatGPT and Perplexity share only 11% of cited sources per Averi's 680M citation audit. Superlines documented up to 615x citation volume variance for the same brand between platforms. Multi-platform tracking is essential for accurate GEO measurement.

## Details

Averi (March 2026) analyzed 680M AI citations — 11% domain overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity. Passionfruit found 12% across 3 engines. Slate HQ tracked 300K+ citations across 6 B2B SaaS brands for 90 days: Claude had highest owned citation share at 9.1%, ChatGPT consistently lowest. Superlines documented 615x citation volume variance for the same brand between platforms. Ahrefs found brand mentions correlate 0.664 with AI citation rates vs backlinks at 0.218 — 3x stronger signal.
