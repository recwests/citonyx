## Summary
Perplexity cites 13.8 sources per query on average but uses each shallowly; ChatGPT cites 7-8 sources but absorbs them 2-3x more deeply. A single ChatGPT citation carries more influence. 80% of Perplexity-cited content does NOT rank in Google's top 10. BrightEdge: 61.9% of queries have different brand recommendations across platforms. Citation volumes differ by up to 615x between Grok and Claude.

## Details
The geo-citation-lab dataset (Zhang, He, Yao, Apr 2026) analyzed 602 controlled prompts across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview — 21,143 citations. High-influence pages: longer, modular, containing extractable evidence. BrightEdge (2026) found ChatGPT, Google AIO, and Google AI Mode disagreed on brand recommendations for 61.9% of queries. Grok-3 had highest citation failure rate at 94%. Claude converts at 5.0% vs ChatGPT 15.9%. 37% of consumers now start searches with AI (up from 14%). Perplexity weighs freshness at ~40% of ranking signal. For B2B brands: ChatGPT rewards depth; Perplexity rewards breadth of coverage.
