## Summary
Formatting alone — heading hierarchy (macro), chunking (meso), emphasis (micro) — drives a 17.3% citation lift. No new content required.

## Details
Three auditable levels: macro (logical H1→H6 without skipping), meso (chunk content so each block stands alone), micro (use emphasis to guide citation). This compounds through earned media. Treat as a two-week audit before adding content.
