## Summary
At Google I/O 2026, AI Overviews and AI Mode were folded into a single unified AI Search experience running on Gemini 3.5 Flash. Previously separate surfaces now share one citation pool, one model, and reach over 1 billion monthly users globally. Separate citation strategies no longer needed.

## Details
The unified surface runs on Gemini 3.5 Flash by default — faster, longer, more citation-rich answers than prior Gemini 1.5 Pro. The competitive citation pool has expanded since AI Mode queries now compete with AI Overview queries. Citation behavior expected to favor structured, authoritative, frequently updated content. B2B software, news, e-commerce, healthcare, and travel each face different risk/opportunity profiles. Strategic implication: Google considers AI-first answers the default product.
