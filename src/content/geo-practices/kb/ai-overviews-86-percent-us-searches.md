## Summary
Peec AI analyzed 500K business-intent prompts across 6 markets in April 2026. AI Overviews appear in 86.7% of US searches (up from 56.9% in April 2025). Commercial-intent queries trigger AIO at 88.5%. Google I/O disclosed 2.5B monthly AIO users vs ~800M ChatGPT weekly active users.

## Details
Long-form queries (11-15 words) trigger AIO at 89.1%; two-word queries at 64.6%. AIO-related CTR collapse: Position 1 drops from 39.8% to 27.6% (-30.6%). Positions 6-10 lose 50% of clicks. Informational queries lost 61% organic clicks YoY; commercial queries -24%. Sites with AIO on >50% of impressions lost 34.5% organic clicks. Healthcare: -41%, Education: -38%, B2B SaaS: -34%. Semrush: AIO coverage rose from 6.49% (Jan 2025) to ~25% (early 2026).
