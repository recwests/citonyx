## Summary
AI-driven local search shifts from "near me" to "best for me" — context and intent override raw proximity. 65% of consumers click AI Overviews in local results; 84% use AI daily for local business discovery. AI prioritizes real customer experience, authentic reviews, and consistent third-party mentions over pure geographic proximity.

## Details
Conversational local queries ("find a plumber who handles emergency work") drive AI citation differently than traditional "plumber near me" searches. Key schema requirements: LocalBusiness with full address/geo/hours, FAQPage for service-specific queries, Review/AggregateRating for social proof. AI engines cross-reference GBP data, Yelp reviews, and third-party mentions. Unlike traditional local SEO where proximity dominated, AI considers review depth, response rates, and category alignment as strong signals.
