## Summary
Analysis of 680 million AI citations found only 11% domain overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity, confirmed at 12% by Passionfruit across three engines. Superlines documented citation volume variance of up to 615x for the same brand between platforms. Brands tracking only one platform miss 89% of their citation landscape.

## Details
Slate HQ tracked 300K+ citations across six B2B SaaS brands for 90 days. ChatGPT gave brands the lowest owned citation share. Claude gave 9.1%, Perplexity 6.8%, ChatGPT consistently worst. Ahrefs found 80% of ChatGPT-cited URLs don't rank in Google's top 100. Moz found 88% of AI Mode citations do not come from organic top 10.
