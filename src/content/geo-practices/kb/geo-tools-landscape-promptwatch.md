## Summary
2026 buyer's guide evaluates 12 platforms; most show where you're invisible but leave the fix to you. Promptwatch rated only full-loop Leader; Profound is enterprise-grade monitoring-only.

## Details
The GEO tooling category exploded in 18 months. Leaders: OtterlyAI (multi-engine, MCP server), Promptwatch (broadest stack: prompt/citation tracking, AI crawler logs, Reddit/YouTube/ChatGPT Shopping tracking, traffic attribution, built-in content generation — $99–$579/mo), Profound (enterprise, SOC2/HIPAA, crawler logs, autonomous Agents, but monitoring-only, $499+/mo). Challengers: Peec.ai (multilingual, ~€89/mo), Scrunch, Search Party. Niche: AthenaHQ, Ahrefs Brand Radar, Semrush (limited AI). Key: choose by actionability, not data volume — most tools report a number but don't close the gap between insight and published content.
