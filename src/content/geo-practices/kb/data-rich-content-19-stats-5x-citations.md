## Summary
SE Ranking's independent study of 129,000 domains: pages with 19+ statistical data points averaged 5.4 ChatGPT citations vs 2.8 for data-light pages. Princeton GEO framework confirms Statistics Addition and Cite Sources boost visibility up to 40%. Effect strongest for law, government, and opinion queries.

## Details
ZipTie.dev confirms data-rich pages earn nearly double AI citations. Data improvement of +37-41% on Position-Adjusted Word Count. Critical for challenger brands: lower-ranked sites (position 5+) improved visibility by 115% when optimized, while position-1 sites decreased by ~30%. GEO optimization disproportionately helps underdogs. Perplexity specifically: 19+ statistics, comparison tables, definitive answer capsules within first 400 words achieve 4.1x higher citation rates. Statistics Addition remains highest-impact single modification per the Princeton GEO paper.
