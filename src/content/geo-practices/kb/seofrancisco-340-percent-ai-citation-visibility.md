## Summary
B2B SaaS project management client (DA 62, 180K monthly organic) had zero AI citations despite strong SEO. After GEO: 340% growth in AI citation visibility, AI referral traffic from 2% to 44%. Strategy: entity graph (Organization + sameAs links to Wikidata/Crunchbase/LinkedIn/G2) + 120 pages rewritten citation-ready + full JSON-LD deployment (SEOFrancisco, Apr 2026).

## Details
Before GEO: top-10 rankings for 800+ keywords but never cited by ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity. Competitors with weaker SEO but stronger entity signals were cited. JSON-LD deployed: FAQPage for every guide, HowTo for tutorials, SoftwareApplication for product pages, Dataset for research, Claim/ClaimReview for comparisons. Cross-platform authority: expert content on G2, Capterra, Quora, GitHub, Stack Overflow. Front-loaded factual claims in first 100 words had highest impact.
