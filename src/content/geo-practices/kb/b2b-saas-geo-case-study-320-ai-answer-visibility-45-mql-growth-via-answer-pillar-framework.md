## Summary
Findably documented B2B cybersecurity SaaS GEO case study (2026): company had stalled organic growth despite strong SEO. Implemented structured GEO roadmap with Answer Pillars (not blog posts), hybrid SEO/GEO model, original benchmark data, and continuous monitoring. Result: 320% boost in AI answer visibility, 45% increase in influenced MQLs, more educated sales pipeline. Key pitfalls identified: treating GEO as one-time project, sacrificing quality for AI-generated content, ignoring traditional SEO fundamentals, failing to measure pipeline impact.

## Details
Pitfall #1: GEO requires continuous optimization — AI models and search interfaces evolve; static content loses visibility. Fix: establish ongoing monitoring, updating, and expanding Answer Pillars. Pitfall #2: over-relying on AI to write articles without expert human review leads to shallow content. Fix: use AI as drafting assistant, not replacement for domain expertise. Pitfall #3: in rush to optimize for AI, some teams neglect technical SEO, site speed, mobile experience — these still influence how AI engines crawl/evaluate authority. Pitfall #4: tracking citations insufficient without connecting GEO visibility to CRM data and influenced opportunities.
