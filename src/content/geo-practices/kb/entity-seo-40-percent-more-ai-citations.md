## Summary
Entity SEO shifts optimization from keyword strings to real-world things (companies, people, products, concepts). Google's Knowledge Graph contains 500B+ facts about 5B entities. Sites optimizing for entities earn 40% more AI citations per Rankeo's 100,000 AI response analysis.

## Details
Rankeo's analysis cites Authoritas 2025 study of 100,000 AI responses. Backlinko notes entity recognition comes from: structured data (Knowledge Graphs, Shopping Graphs), human conversation (Reddit, Quora, podcasts), and multimodal recognition (transcripts from YouTube reviews). AI systems learn from informal mentions — a podcast comparison between two products gets encoded as entity relationships. You cannot game entity recognition the way you could game PageRank. Brands need consistent entity signals across schema, Wikipedia/Wikidata, social profiles, sameAs references, and third-party coverage.
