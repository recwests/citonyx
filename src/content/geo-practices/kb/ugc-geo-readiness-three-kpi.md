## Summary
Bazaarvoice's Tamara Sanderson introduces three GEO KPIs: Accessibility (crawlable for LLMs), Abundance (volume across touchpoints), Authenticity (genuine regulatory-compliant feedback). 'Two customers' concept — optimize for shoppers AND AI. Most common mistake: working in silos.
## Details
Three properties make UGC uniquely suited for GEO: (1) authenticity at source — AI prioritizes customer feedback over polished copy, (2) cost efficiency — cheaper to create, high citation value, (3) regulatory tailwind (GDPR, Omnibus Directive, FTC). GEO audit scores category visibility and surfaces three gap types: missing claims/one-sided content, quality gaps vs competitors, search-query blind spots. Top priority: fix accessibility first (crawlable UGC), break down organizational silos, double down on priority products rather than spreading thin. Engines to watch: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude.
