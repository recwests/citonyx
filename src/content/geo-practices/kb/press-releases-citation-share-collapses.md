## Summary
Press releases now account for only 0.2% of AI citations, falling from 0.4% in April 2026 — the sharpest category decline in Meltwater's dataset. Earned editorial coverage at 37.6% is 188x more valuable for AI visibility than press release distribution.

## Details
Meltwater's May analysis shows press release citation share dropped 50% month-over-month (0.4% to 0.2%). Earned/news media held steady at 37.6% of citation domain count. The lesson: publishing a claim is not the same as earning authority for that claim. Wire distribution still serves SEC disclosure functions but provides near-zero AI visibility leverage compared to earned editorial, structured data, and authoritative third-party sources. Digital PR remains the highest-ROI channel for AI citations.
