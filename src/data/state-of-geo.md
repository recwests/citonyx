# State of GEO

## 1. Thesis

Generative Engine Optimization in mid-2026 confronts a paradox: [71% of all AI citation sources are single-engine exclusive](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-seventy-one-percent-single-engine-exclusive) — each platform reads a different web — yet peer-reviewed research shows self-bias causes AI to disproportionately cite AI-generated content, driving [79.6% of simulations toward diversity collapse](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-ai-search-collapse-79-percent). Against this centrifugal dynamic, brands with integrated SEO+GEO strategies report [81% measurable gains](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-integrated-seo-ai-strategy-81-percent-gains) vs 36% for siloed approaches — because every platform converges on entity clarity, third-party proof, and extractable evidence. [94% of B2B buyers now use LLMs for vendor research](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-b2b-buyers-94-percent-llm-research); the question has shifted from 'how do I rank?' to 'does every AI platform know who I am — and can they prove it from my content?'

## 2. The State Now

[AI Overviews trigger on 86.7% of US searches](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-ai-overviews-86-percent-us-searches) (commercial-intent 88.5%), up from 56.9% YoY, reaching 2.5B monthly users — outpacing ChatGPT's [800–900M weekly](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-900-million-weekly-users-2026). On an all-query basis AIOs appear on [48–50% of Google US queries](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-ai-overviews-48-percent-ctr-drop-61) — the 86.7% figure above measures commercial-intent prompts specifically, a different denominator — though [70% of triggering keywords show volatility](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-overview-volatility-70-percent); only [4–8% of AIO citations overlap with organic top-20](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-overview-volatility-70-percent). [37% of consumers now start search with AI](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-thirty-seven-percent-start-search-with-ai); [only 8% click through when an AI summary is present](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-pew-8-percent-click-through-ai-summary). Google shipped a Search Console AI visibility report — [impressions only, no clicks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-search-console-ai-visibility-report) — and [confirmed no AI-specific schema is required](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-google-no-special-ai-schema-required): "GEO is still SEO at the core." Content with author attribution is [4x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-google-ai-overviews-author-name-bio-required); the freshness window was cut from 365 to 180 days. [Google I/O 2026 made AI Mode the default for 1B+ users](https://citonyx.com/learn/tools#entry-google-io-2026-ai-mode-default), and the [June 2026 Spam Update extended SpamBrain enforcement](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-june-2026-spam-update-scaled-content-abuse) to AI Overviews and AI Mode. [50% of web articles are now AI-generated](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-fifty-percent-web-articles-ai-generated) — the raw material of the self-reinforcing feedback loop underlying the diversity-collapse risk.

But aggregating platforms conceals more than it reveals. [CiteLens confirms each engine follows its own citation rulebook](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-citelens-four-engine-rulebooks): Google AI Mode cites top-10 organic at 93%, Perplexity at 89%, Claude follows brand search demand (53%), ChatGPT ignores both (30% overlap). [44% of cited pages appear in exactly one answer before vanishing](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-forty-four-percent-citations-appear-once) (median lifespan 11–15 days).

**ChatGPT** has the narrowest aperture: it cites the vendor's own first-party site [68% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-first-party-site-68-percent-citations), but [only 15% of retrieved pages get cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-cites-15-percent-retrieved-pages) — two-stage source selection. Wikipedia accounts for [29.7% of top cited pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency), brand homepages 23.8%. Citation follows steep position decay — top retrieval result cited [58%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-position-citation-decay-58-14), position 10 only 14%. ChatGPT and Google organic share only [4.2% of cited URLs](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-google-4-percent-overlap) — near-zero overlap. ChatGPT's [traffic share dropped 22 points to 64.5%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-traffic-share-64-percent-gemini-rises) as Gemini rose to 21.5%.

**Perplexity** is the freshness engine: content updated within 30 days earns [82% citation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points) vs 37% for content over a year old — a 45-point advantage. It responds to new content in [5–7 days vs 3–5 weeks for ChatGPT](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points) and cites [13.8 sources per query](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-platform-citation-divergence-breadth-depth) (breadth) vs ChatGPT's 7–8 (depth). [46.7% of its top-10 sources are Reddit](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-46-percent-reddit-sources). Perplexity cites at [17.7% vs ChatGPT's 8.8%](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-perplexity-cites-2x-more-than-chatgpt-claude) and [converts B2B sign-ups at 11x traditional search](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-perplexity-converts-11x-b2b). Perplexity [abandoned advertising entirely](https://citonyx.com/learn/monetization#entry-perplexity-abandons-ads-subscription) in 2026.

**Claude** cites [brand domains 64% of the time with zero Reddit citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-claude-citation-brand-domains-64-percent) yet is a [UGC outlier at 2–4x the rate of other engines](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-claude-ugc-outlier-2-4x) — its user-generated citations come from communities other than Reddit. It biases toward premium editorial — NYT, Atlantic, New Yorker at [~2x the rate of other platforms](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-claude-premium-editorial-bias), with the longest freshness window. Claude and ChatGPT share only [8% of cited sources](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-claude-chatgpt-8-percent-source-overlap). Claude leads the [enterprise LLM market at 32% share](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-claude-leads-enterprise-llm-market) (vs OpenAI 27%) and drives [10.6% of signups but shows as 0.1% in GA4](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-claude-attribution-gap-ga4-vs-survey).

**Gemini** is the most schema-responsive engine, citing brand-owned sites at [52% — highest of any platform](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) — and shares only [13.7% of citations with Google's own AI Overviews](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. The Gemini app reached [750M monthly users](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gemini-750-million-monthly-users). The Gemini 3 update collapsed AI Overview–organic top-10 overlap from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gemini-3-collapsed-top-10-overlap); fan-out sub-query mapping is now dominant.

**Grok** exists in its own category: [45% of citations from X/Twitter](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-grok-x-twitter-citations-45-percent) (34% organic, 11% verified-only). Posts with 5,000+ likes get 4.6x citation probability.

**Cross-platform landscape.** [Community platforms capture 52.5% of all AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-community-platforms-52-percent-citations) vs 47.5% brand domains. [YouTube generates 188,863 AI citations monthly](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-youtube-dominates-ai-citations-188k) (+56.4% MoM) — the most-cited single source. [Wikipedia reached 83,191 citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-wikipedia-citation-growth-55-percent) (+55.2% MoM). [Verified structured data accounts for 54.53% of distinct citation sources](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-yext-structured-data-54-percent-citations). [84% of AI citations come from earned media](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-earned-media-82-percent-ai-citations-muckrack) (Muck Rack third edition); journalism alone 27%. [Conductor's benchmark](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-conductor-ai-referral-benchmark-1-percent) across 13,770 domains found AI referral = 1.08% of all web traffic, growing 1% MoM, ChatGPT driving 87.4%. Top-3 Google pages are [34x more likely to be AI-cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-seo-floor-top-3-pages-34x-cited) than pages ranked 31–100 — traditional SEO ranking still gates AI visibility.

**Content architecture converges on extraction.** [Answer capsules immediately under H2 are the strongest single content signal — 72.4% citation predictor](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-capsules-72-percent-predictor). [Cited sentences average 10 words](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-cited-sentences-median-10-words); nothing over 18 words gets cited. [44.2% of LLM citations come from the first 30% of text](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-llm-citations-44-percent-front-loaded); claim-rich introductions get cited 2.1x more. [Topical authority (0.76) predicts ChatGPT citations 4x better than domain authority (0.18)](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-topical-authority-0.76-correlation). [Source diversity compounds: one type = 18% coverage, five or more = 78%](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-source-diversity-compounding-1-to-78). [Named authors are 25% more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-named-authors-25-percent-citation-lift); [opinion and analysis is cited more than neutral descriptions](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-opinion-analysis-content-cited-more). Median cited content age [collapsed to 298 days](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-collapsed-298-days).

**Economics and attribution.** On queries with AI Overviews, organic CTR drops [61%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-ai-overviews-48-percent-ctr-drop-61); even queries without them saw 41% decline. AI traffic converts at [14.2% average vs Google organic's 2.8%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-platform-specific-citation-conversion-rates) — a ~5x lift — with spreads by platform (Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%); a [separate benchmark](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-search-converts-4-4x-better-than-organic) orders them differently (ChatGPT 15.9%, Perplexity 10.5%, Claude 5.0%) — platform conversion is measured inconsistently across studies. Brands cited in AI Overviews see [+35% organic CTR and +91% paid CTR](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-cited-brand-premium-35-91-ctr). Yet [27% of signups come from AI vs 0.5% in Google Analytics](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-attribution-gap-54x-undercount) — a 54x undercount. [60% of AI-cited URLs never appear in Search Console or standard rank trackers](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-citation-reach-rate-33-percent-seo-tools-gap); Citation Reach Rate averages 33%.

**B2B is the most advanced frontier.** [B2B SaaS dominates at 34% of tracked citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent). [Forrester finds AI tools are the #1 cited information source in B2B buying](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-forrester-ai-tools-number-one-b2b-source). B2B AI traffic converts [9–23x vs organic](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-b2b-buyers-94-percent-llm-research). [Healthcare publishers are losing 34–46% organic traffic](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-healthcare-geo-traffic-collapse-46-percent) as [63–85% of health queries answered directly in AI Overviews](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-healthcare-geo-traffic-collapse-46-percent).

**Technical barriers and enforcement.** [AI crawlers now account for 40–50% of bot-level activity](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-ai-crawlers-activity-40-percent-2026); 65–70% are live user queries, not training. [73% of websites block AI crawlers](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-73-percent-websites-block-ai-crawlers); [JS-rendered content fails 77% of parser attempts](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-js-rendered-content-77-percent-fail) — static HTML with schema achieves 94% parse rate. [AI systems fetch pages with ~2s hard timeout](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-page-speed-ai-retrieval-2s-timeout); TTFB under 1s is critical. From September 15, 2026, [Cloudflare's three-tier classification](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-cloudflare-three-tier-crawler-classification) blocks Training and Agent crawlers by default on ad-supported pages. [llms.txt has 784+ implementations](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-784-implementations-unconfirmed) but only Perplexity and Anthropic confirm use — [97% of published files get zero AI requests](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-zero-requests-google-ignores), and Google explicitly ignores it.

**Local GEO is an open frontier.** [88% of local businesses have no active GEO strategy](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-local-businesses-88-percent-no-geo). AI search shifts from proximity-first to context-first: [intent and relevance override geographic distance](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-local-geo-ai-context-over-proximity). A [150+ review threshold](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-local-geo-150-review-threshold) applies for consistent AI visibility. [Incomplete GBP data triggers a silence penalty](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-local-geo-gbp-data-silence-penalty).

**The schema paradox persists.** One study shows [2.3x citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-2-3x-citation-lift) from FAQPage, HowTo, Article, Product schema; another tracked 1,885 pages adding schema vs 4,000 controls and found [zero independent effect](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-near-zero-independent-effect). Both are valid: schema amplifies quality but does not create it. Attribute-rich Product/Review schema outperforms generic by [20pp (61.7% vs 41.6%)](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-attribute-rich-schema-20pp-citation-lift). [FAQPage triggers in 89% of Q&A queries](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-faqpage-89-percent-trigger-rate) with 3.2x lift.

## 3. What's Winning

**Entity** remains the dominant signal class. The SEO-to-GEO Divergence Index shows brand entity mentions at NIS [0.918 vs domain rating at 0.397](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-brand-entity-mentions-surpass-domain-rating). Branded web mentions correlate [r=0.664 vs backlinks r=0.218](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks); top quartile averages 169 AI Overview mentions vs 14 for the next tier. The [Brand Stature Ladder](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-stature-ladder-73-44-11-percent) shows global brands at 73% citation rate, mid-market 44%, niche 11%. [Wikidata QID with sameAs schema](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent) lifts citations 40% with zero ongoing cost. Brands with a Wikipedia presence earn [3x higher ChatGPT citation rates](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency).

**Review depth** is the single highest correlated variable: [50+ reviews on G2/Capterra = 3.2x citation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-depth-strongest-geo-predictor). [Review velocity (r=0.72) outperforms star rating (r=0.31)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-velocity-vs-star-rating-citation-correlation). A [B2B SaaS case study](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-87-percent-citation-rate-reviews-pr) went from 12% to 87% citation rate in 4 months by scaling G2 reviews from 43 to 287 plus 14 PR placements.

**Answer-first content architecture.** [Answer capsules under H2 = 72.4% predictor](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-capsules-72-percent-predictor). [HubSpot's AEO template](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-hubspot-aeo-template-60-words) — answer within 60 words after H2 — drove 3x lead conversion. [Named-source quotations with credentials](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-quotation-addition-strongest-geo-tactic): +42.6% lift (strongest Princeton GEO tactic). [19+ data points per page](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-data-rich-content-19-stats-5x-citations): 5.4 citations vs 2.8. [Original datasets](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-original-datasets-highest-roi-tactic) remain the highest-ROI GEO tactic — AI cannot synthesize data it cannot access. [Document structure drives 17.3% lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-document-structure-17-percent-citation-lift), headings accounting for 44.9% of the gain.

**Third-party proof.** [Digital PR drives 25% of LLM citations but only 6% of practitioners use it](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-digital-pr-evidence-gap-25-percent-6-percent) — the widest evidence gap in GEO. An industrial lighting brand achieved [88.6% AI recommendation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-88-percent-ai-recommendation-pr-consensus) — own site contributed 4.5%, PR reprints drove 59.8%. [Comparison tables +34%, llm.txt +32%, FAQ schema +28%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift) coverage lift within 14–21 days. [Source diversity compounds](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-source-diversity-compounding-1-to-78): one type = 18%, five or more = 78%.

**Case study convergence.** Compiled benchmarks show AI traffic gains up to [2,300% in the strongest documented case](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-geo-case-study-benchmark-2026) (a manufacturing vertical), clustering around a 90–180 day inflection point. [Fulton: zero to $18,164/month in 12 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-fulton-35x-ai-revenue-case-study), Google organic doubled as side effect. [Fintech B2B: +61% branded AI mentions, 189% comparison traffic growth](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-fintech-b2b-geo-case-study-naturallinks). [B2B SaaS outranked Salesforce and Zapier on 40+ prompts in 30 days](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-momentum-b2b-saas-outrank-incumbents). [ChatGPT shopping citations surged from 8% to 87% in 5 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-chatgpt-shopping-citations-surge-87-percent); AI-referred traffic converts at 30–40%. The [GainFrame playbook](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-gainframe-playbook-31-percent-chatgpt) — Quick Answer blocks, question H2s, FAQ schema, multi-schema JSON-LD, IndexNow — made ChatGPT the top acquisition channel at 31%.

## 4. Losing Signals

The KB currently flags no signals as formally deprecated or stale. Several contested findings and structural constraints are worth tracking.

**Google self-conflict.** Gemini and AI Overviews share only [13.7% of citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. AI Overview–organic overlap collapsed from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-aio-overlap-organic-collapse-76-to-38). Optimizing for one Google surface does not optimize for another.

**Only [36 global brands](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-semrush-universal-36-brands)** maintain top-100 visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews simultaneously.

**Store-bought backlinks** show [near-zero transfer to AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-store-bought-backlinks-no-ai-citation-transfer). Google's [listicle crackdown (January 2026)](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-google-listicle-crackdown-2026) caused 29–49% visibility losses; [self-promotional listicles get cited but 43% of the time AI recommends a competitor](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-self-promotional-list-43-percent-backfire).

**[Credibility paradox](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-credibility-paradox-ai-visibility-not-trust):** being cited in AI answers does not guarantee being believed (Burson/Profound). [AI Overviews reduced Wikipedia traffic by ~15%](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-ai-overviews-wikipedia-traffic-loss-15-percent) — causal evidence that AI answers cannibalize even authoritative sources.

**Freshness is real but overclaimed.** The controlled advantage is [25.7%](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-25-percent-advantage), not the viral 4.3x figure. But the [~4.5-week half-life](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-citation-half-life-4-5-weeks) makes continuity discipline mandatory regardless.

## 6. What To Do

1. **Audit per platform, not in aggregate.** Use [GSC's AI Mode and AI Overviews segmentation](https://citonyx.com/learn/tools#entry-gsc-ai-mode-segmentation). Add dedicated tracking for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini. The [CiteLens finding](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-citelens-four-engine-rulebooks) that each engine follows distinct citation rules makes aggregate optimization impossible.

2. **Build the entity foundation first.** Wikidata entry + sameAs Organization schema. Zero-cost, [40% citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent). Wikipedia presence for [3x higher ChatGPT citation rates](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency).

3. **Shift link budget to mention budget.** [Brand mentions are 3x stronger than backlinks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks). PR placements, G2/Trustpilot reviews, Reddit community. [Review depth (50+ = 3.2x)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-depth-strongest-geo-predictor) and [review velocity (r=0.72)](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-velocity-vs-star-rating-citation-correlation) are the highest-leverage activities. [Source diversity compounds](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-source-diversity-compounding-1-to-78): one type = 18% coverage, five = 78%.

4. **Restructure for passage-level extraction.** Every H2 needs a direct answer block in the first 40–60 words. [Answer capsules under H2 = 72.4% predictor](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-capsules-72-percent-predictor). [Cited sentences average 10 words](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-cited-sentences-median-10-words) — short, front-loaded wins. [HubSpot's template](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-hubspot-aeo-template-60-words) drove 3x conversions. [Named-source quotations](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-quotation-addition-strongest-geo-tactic) (+42.6%), [data-rich pages with 19+ stats](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-data-rich-content-19-stats-5x-citations), and [original datasets](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-original-datasets-highest-roi-tactic) compound.

5. **Adopt weekly-to-monthly publication cycles.** [Content <13 weeks is ~2x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-13-week-shelf-life). [Content velocity 12+/month = 200x faster AI visibility](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-velocity-12-pieces-200x). Perplexity rewards days-old content. [Comparison tables +34%, llm.txt +32%, FAQ schema +28%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift) coverage lift within 14–21 days.

6. **Audit AI crawler access — hard deadline.** [AI crawlers are 40–50% of bot activity](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-ai-crawlers-activity-40-percent-2026) — ensure 14+ user-agents are allowed in robots.txt. [JS-rendered content fails 77% of parser attempts](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-js-rendered-content-77-percent-fail). [TTFB under 1s](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-page-speed-ai-retrieval-2s-timeout) is critical for the ~2s hard timeout. From September 15, 2026, [Cloudflare's three-tier classification](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-cloudflare-three-tier-crawler-classification) blocks Training and Agent crawlers by default on ad-supported pages.

7. **Deploy AI Discovery Files, but temper expectations.** llms.txt, llms-full.txt. [Anthropic and Perplexity confirmed support](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-adoption-2026); Google and Meta do not.
