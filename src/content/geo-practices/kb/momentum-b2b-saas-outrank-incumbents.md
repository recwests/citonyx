## Summary
Peec AI's B2B SaaS GEO case study: 100 prompt-specific articles generated 10x AI visibility in ~30 days, outranking Salesforce and Zapier on 40+ prompts. Sessions from AI search doubled. Listicle-structured content was critical — 74.2% of AI citations target "Top N" listicle formats.

## Details
The strategy used prompt-level gap analysis to identify 100 specific AI search prompts where the brand had zero visibility. For each prompt, a dedicated article was created with listicle structure (e.g., "Top 10 Tools for X"), statistical evidence, and clear entity definition. Listicle format was deliberately chosen because GenOptima found 74.2% of AI citations come from "Top N" content. The 30-day timeline demonstrates that content velocity combined with format optimization can rapidly overcome incumbent advantage in AI search — even against category giants.
