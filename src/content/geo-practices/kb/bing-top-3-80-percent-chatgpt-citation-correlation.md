## Summary
Analysis of 1,000 ChatGPT citations across 200 buyer-research queries found Bing top-3 ranking correlates with citation at ~80%. Content above 2,000 words strongly correlates with multiple citations per source. Named-author bylines produce 1.4x higher citation rates. FAQ schema does not move citations independently of content quality (Capconvert, Mar 2026).

## Details
Capconvert analyzed 1,000 ChatGPT search citations across 200 queries spanning ecommerce, B2B SaaS, professional services, and consumer research categories (late 2025–early 2026). 80% of cited URLs ranked in Bing top 10; 60% were Bing top-3. Pages cited multiple times averaged 3,400+ words vs. 1,200 for single-citation pages. Named-author pages showed 1.4x lift. Schema markup produced 1.1-1.2x lift. Wikipedia (~8% of all citations) and aggregators (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, ~6%) were cited above their Bing rank. Source: capconvert.com/learn/blog/what-1000-chatgpt-citations-reveal.
