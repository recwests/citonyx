## Summary
Google now requires author name and bio for AI Overview citation eligibility (4x citation rate with attribution). Content >6 months old must show dateModified to remain eligible (freshness window: 365→180 days). Multi-source consensus model cites 3-8 sources per answer.

## Details
March 2026 update: author name and bio required for AI Overview eligibility. Google deployed a fact-checking layer cross-referencing claims against authoritative sources. Video transcripts/captions now regularly cited alongside text. Dominant content formats: definitive guides (35%), comparison content (28%), how-to guides (22%). Multi-format content earns 2.3x more citations than single-format. E-E-A-T functions as a binary gate at Stage 3 of AIO source selection pipeline. Multi-format content (guides + comparisons + instructions) earns citations at 2.3x the rate.
