## Summary
The SEO Floor study (114,034 observations) found a top-3 Google page is 7.82x more likely AI-cited than rank 11-30; Tier 1 vs Tier 4 odds ratio ~34x. 75.4% of aggregate citation events from outside top 30 is a denominator artifact — deep-tier pages vastly outnumber top-30 pages. Schema markup strongest content predictor (OR=1.31).

## Details
A pre-registered GEO composite of 7 features adds small but real predictive power (Z-sum OR=1.06; PCA-1 OR=1.15 per 1 SD). Schema markup drives this effect. Statistics density and list structure show small positive effects; heading density shows small negative. 77% of deep-tier citations are one-hit-wonders cited by a single AI platform. Sharp platform divergence: Claude cites 0.6% UGC in deep tier; Perplexity cites 24%. SEO ranking gates AI citation; GEO features add independent lift on top.
