## Summary
Pattern 2 of three high-citation structures: "stat + source + implication." Pages that introduce a specific sourced number get cited as the carrier of that fact; generic claims are ignored. Princeton GEO study: statistics +32.8%, quotations +42.6%, citations +27.7% visibility lift.

## Details
Other winning patterns: (1) definition + 3 bullets + comparison table; (3) FAQ-anchored sections from People Also Ask/Reddit/AI follow-ups. Cite real primary sources. The geo-citation-lab dataset confirms pages with numbers/definitions/comparisons/how-to are far more "absorbed" into answers.
