## Summary
Loganix synthesis of 6 studies: AI traffic converts at 14.2% vs Google organic 2.8%. Per platform: Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%. Each platform has distinct source preferences requiring separate content strategies.

## Details
OtterlyAI: ChatGPT provides clickable links, Perplexity emphasizes domains, Google prioritizes brand visibility. Google AI Mode: 59.8% brand citations, 21% to Google properties, 62.4% YouTube social citations. ChatGPT: Wikipedia 7.8%, Reddit 37% social, editorial sites. Perplexity: Reddit 6.6%, YouTube 73% social citations. Claude: narrowest source pool (54% unknown), 9.1% owned citation share. Gemini: brand-owned websites 52.1%.
