## Summary
YouTube functions as foundational AI infrastructure, generating 188,863 citations across eight major LLMs in May 2026 — the highest single-source count. Citations grew 56.4% month-over-month, driven by AI platforms reading transcripts, descriptions, and metadata.

## Details
Meltwater's May 2026 analysis of 8M+ citations found YouTube is the single most-cited source. Google AI Mode alone generated 73,813 YouTube citations, AI Overviews 38,002, Gemini 37,924, and Perplexity 25,949. AI platforms read structured information around videos: transcripts, descriptions, titles, and metadata. Product explainers, category education, and comparison content can all become AI-citable when clearly structured. YouTube holds a 200x citation advantage over other video platforms per 5WPR's Citation Source Index.
