## Summary
Hands-on testing across 200+ domains found pages with comprehensive schema are 3.2x more likely to be cited in AI Overviews. FAQPage schema triggers in 89% of matching Q&A queries. Speakable schema powers 60% of voice responses.

## Details
The 'schema stacking' approach layers Article, FAQPage, HowTo, Speakable, Organization, and BreadcrumbList on single pages. Moving from no schema to basic Article yields +45% citation lift. Adding FAQ+HowTo gives +180%. Full stack gives +320%. Entity-focused schema with sameAs linking to Wikidata/Wikipedia gives +450%. Critical mistakes: schema not matching visible content (trust penalty), FAQ answers under 40 words (won't trigger), missing dateModified, orphan schema without supporting page content.
