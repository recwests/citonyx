## Summary
A B2B SaaS company implemented the CITABLE framework (extractable content architecture + prompt mapping + fact-dense pages) and grew monthly AI-referred free trials from approximately 500 to 3,500+. The framework prioritizes making every page citable by AI engines rather than ranking for keywords.

## Details
The CITABLE framework consists of: (1) prompt mapping — identifying which user questions map to each product feature/page, (2) fact-dense content — each page includes specific, verifiable claims with numbers and sources, (3) extractable structure — clear headings, bullet points, comparison tables, and standalone answer blocks, (4) external citation building — earning third-party mentions that reinforce entity authority. The 7x trial growth occurred over roughly 6 months. Key insight: B2B SaaS naturally fits AI citation patterns because buyers ask specific evaluation questions that map directly to extractable product content.
