## Summary
Claude and ChatGPT share only 8% of cited sources for the same prompts. Claude aligns with Google organic rankings 64% of the time; ChatGPT does not follow Google rankings at all. Each engine reads a fundamentally different web.

## Details
AIVO analysis (2026): Claude's source selection correlates strongly with traditional Google rankings (64% alignment), making traditional SEO relatively more effective for Claude optimization. ChatGPT draws from a divergent web corpus with near-zero Google ranking alignment. Strategic implication: a single GEO strategy cannot serve both platforms. Optimize for Claude with traditional SEO signals (backlinks, authority, structured data). Optimize for ChatGPT with community presence (Reddit, Wikipedia), conversational content, and original research. The 8% overlap means brands appearing on one engine are effectively invisible on the other.
