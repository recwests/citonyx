## Summary
ChatGPT cites only ~15% of pages it retrieves. FAQ schema pages earn ~3x more citations than prose. 44.2% of citations come from first third of page. Tables extracted at 81% vs 23% for paragraphs. Pages with 19+ named statistics + expert quote strongly outperform. Bing Webmaster Tools presence is prerequisite.

## Details
To get cited by ChatGPT: claim and optimize Bing Webmaster Tools (ChatGPT's live search runs on Bing index). Lead each page with 40–60 word direct answer. Add FAQ and Article schema. Convert comparable facts into tables. Raise fact density to 19+ verifiable statistics with named sources. Include at least one attributed expert quote. Refresh content every 2–3 months. Sites with >32,000 referring domains are 3.5x more likely to be cited — a 'trust cliff' favoring established brands but structure/data signals let smaller sites compete.
