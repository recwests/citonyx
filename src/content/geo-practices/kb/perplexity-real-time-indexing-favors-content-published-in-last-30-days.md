## Summary

Perplexity's real-time web retrieval architecture has no knowledge cutoff. 82% of its cited content was published within the last 30 days. Year signals in titles and headings ('2026') improve citation rates by ~30%. Fresh content within hours of indexing can earn citations.

## Details

- Perplexity averages 21.87 citations per response — highest of any major AI platform.
- Favors pages with question-format H2/H3 headings, proprietary statistics, named sources, and verifiable methodology.
- Reddit accounts for 46.7% of Perplexity's top citation sources.
- Unlike ChatGPT's static training data — Perplexity performs live web search per query.
