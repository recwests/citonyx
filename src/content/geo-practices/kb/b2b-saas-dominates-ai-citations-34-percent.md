## Summary
Xpand Media tracked 50 paid client engagements + 200 control sites (Jan-Mar 2026). Sector breakdown: B2B SaaS 34% (Perplexity leads), DTC 19% (ChatGPT leads), Fintech 14%, AI infra 11%, Hospitality 8%, Healthcare 7%. ChatGPT + Perplexity = 68% of all citations. RankScope 2026 benchmarks: above 30% citation SOV = strong, 10-30% = present, below 10% = effectively invisible. Most brands starting GEO measure below 5%. A 26% increase achievable in 90 days.

## Details
Gemini grew fastest, doubling month-over-month. Claude and Copilot remain niche but disproportionately cite long-form primary research — a signal for in-depth content publishers. Sector-specific engine preferences: B2B SaaS → optimize for Perplexity first; DTC/e-commerce → ChatGPT; Hospitality/Travel → Gemini (local-search heavy). RankScope 2026 benchmarks: 1-2 dominant brands at 30-45% SOV, 3-5 brands at 10-25% SOV (those with dedicated GEO programs), the rest sharing 15-25% across dozens of brands below 5%. B2B SaaS has most at stake in GEO because enterprise software buyers start vendor research with AI search. Gartner Reviews captures 81.7% of analyst-relations citations. Recommendation: start with ChatGPT + Perplexity for most clients, then layer in Gemini for regulated or local-search-heavy verticals. Median baseline visibility: 8-12% citation rate; leaders at 40%+.
