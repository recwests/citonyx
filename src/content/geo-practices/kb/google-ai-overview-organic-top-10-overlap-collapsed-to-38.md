## Summary

In mid-2025, 76% of AI Overview citations came from organic top 10. By early 2026, Ahrefs found 38% and BrightEdge as low as 17%. Semantic completeness (r=0.87), multi-modal content (156% higher selection), and structured data (73% improvement) now outweigh ranking position.

## Details

- 47% of AI Overview citations in 2025 came from pages ranking below position 5 (Ahrefs).
- YouTube brand mentions are the single strongest correlating factor with AI Overview visibility.
- LinkedIn appears in AI Overviews' top 10 cited sources at 13% share.
- Pages cited in AI Overviews earn 35% more organic clicks than non-cited competitors.
