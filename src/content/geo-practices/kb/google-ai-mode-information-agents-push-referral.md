## Summary
Google launched AI Mode information agents for Ultra subscribers ($199.99/mo) on June 12, 2026. Unlike zero-click AI Overviews, agents push synthesized updates with links to the web whenever new information appears — creating the first proactive, source-linked referral surface in agent-era search.

## Details
Agents monitor five data categories: blogs, news sites, social posts, real-time finance, and real-time shopping/sports. Users set up agents conversationally ('Keep me updated on...'). Google positions this as the evolution of Google Alerts with context synthesis. AI Mode crossed 1B MAU at I/O 2026. Source-selection algorithm is undisclosed — treat GEO recommendations as extrapolated from AI Overviews signals.
