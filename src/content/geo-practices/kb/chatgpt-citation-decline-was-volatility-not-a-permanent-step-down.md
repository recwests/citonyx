## Summary
seoClarity's 5-market study (US, UK, Canada, Germany, Italy) Feb-May 2026: ChatGPT citations dropped 86-94% at March-April trough, then rebounded toward pre-March levels in May. The April 19 update introduced double compression — both zero-citation rate surged (Germany reached 85%) AND citations per cited response collapsed. Italy bucked the trend, improving zero-citation rate during the collapse.

## Details
March 8 update: US zero-citation rate doubled from 28% to 48%. April 19 update: even when cited, fewer total citation slots per response. Germany: initially defied trend (52%→41% zero-citation in March) then collapsed to 85% in April. Italy: only market to improve in April (67%→52% zero-citation). UK: sharpest overall decline. The durable takeaway: AI search is inherently unstable; brands need strategy that holds through swings.
