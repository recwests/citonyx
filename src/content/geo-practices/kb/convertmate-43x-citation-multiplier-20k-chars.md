## Summary
ConvertMate's 2026 GEO benchmark measured a 3.2x citation multiplier for content updated within the prior 30 days. Ahrefs cross-engine data confirms AI-cited URLs are on average 25.7% fresher than non-cited URLs (1,064 days vs 1,432 days since last update).

## Details
Stale dates and outdated statistics silently decay citation rate, especially on Perplexity (most freshness-aggressive engine) and AI Overviews on commercial queries. Stale dateModified that doesn't reflect actual changes gets deprioritized as 'fake fresh'. Critical: refresh category, pricing, and comparison pages every 30 days; show update dates in both visible text and schema markup (must match); replace year-tagged statistics each quarter.
