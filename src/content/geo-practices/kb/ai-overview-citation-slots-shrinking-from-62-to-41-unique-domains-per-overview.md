## Summary
A cross-platform citation analysis (Content Intelligence Lab, April 2026) found the average AI Overview now cites 4.1 unique domains, down from 6.2 in early 2025 — a 34% reduction. Fewer citation slots means higher stakes for every piece of content and more intense competition for inclusion.

## Details
This contraction of citation slots makes domain-level authority and content structure even more critical. With fewer slots available, being in the top 5 most-cited sources per query is increasingly winner-takes-most. The trend suggests AI platforms are becoming more selective about sources as they optimize for answer quality and brevity.
