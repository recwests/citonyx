## Summary
Google's generative AI controls in Search Console (ability to block content from AI Overviews) is now rolling out beyond UK sites. Multiple US-based site owners confirmed seeing the setting. Access via Settings or search.google.com/search-console/settings/search-gen-ai.
## Details
Initially launched a month ago only for .co.uk sites. Barry Schwartz confirmed rollout via multiple practitioners: Jamie Indigo, John McAlpin, Kuldeep Sharma, Zakk Glista, Jennifer Otto. Setting available under Search Console Settings > Search Generative AI. Not all sites have access yet. This expands publisher control over whether content appears in AI Overviews. Also: Google recently expanded AI performance reports beyond UK alongside this rollout.
