## Summary
Graphite ran 1,528 simulations across GPT-5.2, Gemini 3 Pro, Claude Sonnet 4.5 using 1,019 prompts and 1M+ LLM API calls. 79.6% of simulations end in collapse (responses become near-identical). Self-authored references cited disproportionately — even controlling for quality.
## Details
38.9% (Jan 2026) to 42.7% (Jun 2026) of ChatGPT's own search references are already AI-generated. Self-authored references cited at 38.9% vs 9.4% for AI-generated originals vs 7.4% for human-written — a 4-5x self-bias. For entity questions (marketer-critical), collapse means the same 5 products mentioned in same order every time. Search simulation (most realistic): 77.2% entity/75.4% editorial collapse. Implication: as more AI content is published, AI answers will increasingly converge, reducing diversity of brands cited.
