## Summary
Machine Relations Index (28,870 source events, 7,124 domains, 6 engines, June 2026): 71% of all cited sources appear in exactly one AI engine's responses. Even the most overlapping pair (Google AI Mode + Grok) agrees on just ~20% of sources. Each engine operates a near-independent citation ecosystem.

## Details
The 71% single-engine exclusivity figure is the most striking measure of platform divergence. Perplexity cited Gartner 0 times while Google AI Mode cited it 98 times — a complete blind spot. Claude cited Crunchbase 51 times versus Grand View Research 5 times, revealing per-engine domain preferences. Strategic implication: brands must track and optimize for each engine independently. A brand cited by ChatGPT may be completely invisible to Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, or Grok.
