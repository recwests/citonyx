## Summary
Fulton (footwear health D2C) used XLR8 AI's RAG optimization to reverse-engineer LLM retrieval. Blog-attributed revenue grew from $516 to $18,164 (same 30-day window, YoY). Google organic traffic doubled as side effect — optimizing for AI and Google intent turned out to be structurally identical actions.

## Details
Phase 1 (6 weeks): zero AI visibility to 700% revenue increase from AI search. Content generated with high cosine similarity to key foot health queries embedded in product pages. Phase 2: 35x YoY blog-attributed revenue growth. Key insight: product listicles drove 2x more revenue per post than educational content. Google organic clicks grew 167% (7K to 225K/week). No traditional SEO campaigns were run. Confirms that GEO and SEO optimization are converging structurally.
