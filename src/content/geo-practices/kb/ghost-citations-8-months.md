## Summary
OtterlyAI experiment tracked 15 deleted comparison pages across 7 AI engines. Pages never reached 0% citations even months after deletion. About 35% of all citations came after pages were removed. Claude cited 78% of deleted pages — most persistent. Gemini produced 31% of all ghost citations — largest volume.
## Details
Pages were deleted once live data was collected. 93% of citation volume survived the first week post-deletion. Claude most persistent (78% of deleted pages still cited 8 months later). Gemini produced largest ghost volume (31% of all ghost citations). ChatGPT's ghost citation rate dropped faster — 58% after week 1, declining to 14% by week 12. Perplexity showed moderate ghost persistence. Implications: audit backlinks before deleting content; AI citation decay is real but very slow; deleting content doesn't stop AI citations for months.
