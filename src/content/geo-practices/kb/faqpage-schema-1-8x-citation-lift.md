## Summary
Prompt Architect's Q1 2026 controlled panel (2,400 cited passages, same domain across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini): FAQPage schema produces 1.8x citation lift vs untagged equivalents. Tier 1 schema (Article, FAQPage, Organization) provides highest ROI with lowest effort. JSON-LD format preferred.

## Details
Schema.org's own LLM-readiness guidance plus Princeton/Georgia Tech/Allen AI GEO research confirm: pages with well-formed JSON-LD get cited at measurably higher rates. Kongzilla data: 71% of ChatGPT-cited pages include schema; pages with proper schema are cited 3.2x more often. Schema strategy hierarchy: Tier 1 (Article, FAQPage, Organization) = high lift, low effort. Tier 2 (HowTo, Product, BreadcrumbList) = medium-high. Tier 3 (Dataset, ClaimReview, SoftwareApplication) = specialist. JSON-LD format beats Microdata and RDFa for LLM parsing.
