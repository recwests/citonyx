## Summary
Cyrus Shepard analyzed 54 experiments, patents, and case studies to create the first evidence-weighted AI citation ranking factors. Top factors (URL Accessibility 9.5, Search Rank 9.4, Fan-out Rank 9.3) align with traditional SEO. Structured data scores 5.6. LLMs.txt scores 2.0 — no credible evidence it influences citations.

## Details
Each factor scored on repeatability (consistency across studies), strength of evidence (study size), and official support (docs/patents). Key findings: URL Accessibility 9.5 — basic crawlability remains prerequisite. Fan-out Rank 9.3 — ranking for related sub-queries is nearly as important as the main query. Answer Near Top 8.8 — 44.2% of citations from top 30% of page. Freshness 7.0 — varies by query type. Structured Data 5.6 — consistent but small positive effect. LLMs.txt 2.0 — no credible evidence. Core takeaway: 'win SEO, win AI citations.'
