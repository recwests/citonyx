## Summary
Previsible's AI Traffic Study tracking November 2024 to May 2026 documented Claude's visibility growing 64x, surpassing Perplexity. Gemini showed steady 3.2x growth to become the second-most visible AI model behind ChatGPT. Claude's growth concentrated in developer, technical buyer, and professional service queries.

## Details
The 64x growth trajectory is consistent with Claude's increasing adoption in professional research and coding contexts. Claude particularly favors high-quality writing, expert explanations, long-form reasoning, and authoritative documents over SEO-optimized content. Gemini's steady 3.2x growth reflects its tight integration with Google's ecosystem signals including indexed content, structured data, YouTube, and Maps/entity data. Key implication: multi-platform tracking is non-negotiable — each engine grows at different rates with different content preferences.
