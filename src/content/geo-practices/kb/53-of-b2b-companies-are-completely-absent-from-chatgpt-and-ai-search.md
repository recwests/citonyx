## Summary
AnswerManiac.ai Q2 2026 report tested 100 B2B companies across HR tech (32), fintech (33), and B2B SaaS (35). 53 of 100 were never mentioned by ChatGPT or Perplexity in response to 42 buyer-intent queries. Only 22 were cited by both engines. Perplexity cited more companies than ChatGPT. Highly visible companies shared: detailed comparison pages, use-case guides, integration documentation, recent content, structured data, and third-party references.

## Details
42 problem-oriented buyer-intent queries per company (not generic 'best software' prompts). ChatGPT with GPT-4o and Perplexity Sonar Pro. HR tech: 15/32 cited. Fintech: 16/33 cited. B2B SaaS: 16/35 cited. Companies with 100% visibility: Multiplier, Assembly, Breezy HR, Gem, Papaya Global, Justt, Treasury Prime, Dwolla, MX, Footprint, Scribe, Fireflies.ai, Fathom, Avoma, Close, Crisp. Gemini and Claude excluded due to API constraints.
