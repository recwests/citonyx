## Summary

Superlines data (March 2026) shows citation volumes differ by 615x between Grok and Claude for the same brand. Community platforms drive 48% of AI citations but vary wildly by engine — Perplexity references community platforms in 90%+ of answers, Gemini in only 7%. Sentiment flips 6.7x more often than mention presence.

## Details

ChatGPT Search cites avg 4.1 sources per answer (lower than Perplexity's 5.8). Source mix: Wikipedia 14%, news 22%, Reddit/Quora 9%, industry blogs 17%, brand sites 11%. 22% of tested outputs showed engines disagreeing entirely on whether to mention the same brand. Single-engine optimization is a losing bet in 2026.
