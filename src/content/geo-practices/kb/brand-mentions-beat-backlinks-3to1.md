## Summary
Gemini is trained on the Knowledge Graph, so entity clarity is now AI-citation eligibility, not a vanity play. Pages with 15+ connected KG entities are 4.8x more likely to be cited (ALM Corp). Domain authority correlation with AIO citation fell to 0.18 in 2026.

## Details
Priority order: entity home (About page as source of truth) → Wikidata QID → sameAs schema (Organization/Person with @id, sameAs to LinkedIn/Crunchbase/Wikidata) → internal entity linking → off-site corroboration (editorial, Wikipedia, review platforms). Google's June 2026 "Clarity Cleanup" purged billions of low-confidence entities — ambiguity is now worse than absence. Consistent naming across G2/Crunchbase/LinkedIn matters.
