## Summary

Reach GEO 2026 guide identifies FAQPage, Article, Organization, and HowTo as the four highest-impact schema types for GEO. The sameAs property is the most underused GEO schema field — it anchors entity graph. Correct implementation on top 10 pages is a one-week project with compounding returns.

## Details

FAQPage has highest direct citation impact for question-answer content. Article schema signals authorship and freshness (dateModified). Organization schema with sameAs links to LinkedIn, Wikipedia, Crunchbase creates entity recognition. HowTo schema works for process content. Incorrect or conflicting schema creates entity confusion and suppresses citation.
