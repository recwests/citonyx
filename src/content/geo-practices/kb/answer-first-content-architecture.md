## Summary
Answer-first content architecture — putting the direct answer in the first 1-3 sentences using explicit definitions (X is…, The main causes are…, The best options are…) — is confirmed as the highest-return GEO content practice. A 2026 citation study found direct "answerability" was among the strongest predictors of AI citations.

## Details
Answer-first structure works because AI engines extract the first clear statement matching a user query. Required: explicit definitions without ambiguity, factual claims with numbers where possible, and complete standalone sentences that don't require surrounding context. Marketing language ("unlock your potential," "revolutionary solution") without facts reduces extractability. The structure applies at page level (meta description + H1) and section level (each H2/H3 followed by a direct answer). Comparison tables and numbered lists following the answer block further boost citation probability. This contradicts the traditional SEO hook→background→answer structure and requires explicit rewrites for legacy content.
