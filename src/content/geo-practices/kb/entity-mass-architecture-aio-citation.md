## Summary
Entity mass — the total declared, verified, cross-referenced identity signals of a brand — determines AI Overview citation, not page authority or backlinks. A brand with thin entity mass can rank #1 organically and get zero AIO citations because retrieval systems cannot confirm what the page represents.

## Details
Entity mass components: explicit entity declarations in visible page text, structured data (Organization/Person/Service schema), cross-domain entity references from authoritative third-party sources, and sameAs network density. Google's AI Overviews system evaluates source quality through entity salience — the degree to which declared entities match query entities. Cited brands get 35% more clicks than non-cited pages in top 10. Entity mass improvements compound across engines — fixes that earn Google AIO citations also improve Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Claude citation rates.
