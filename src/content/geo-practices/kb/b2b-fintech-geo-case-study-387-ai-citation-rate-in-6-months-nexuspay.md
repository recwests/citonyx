## Summary
B2B fintech SaaS went from 3% to 87% AI citation rate across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude in 6 months. Entity SEO, FAQ pages, comparison content, and thought leadership drove the transformation. AI-referred traffic grew 340%, converting at 4.2% vs 2.8% from traditional search.

## Details
The client had strong traditional SEO (DA 54, top 3 rankings) but 3% AI citation rate. Three highest-impact tactics: Organization schema added 340 lines of JSON-LD across the site, 8 comprehensive FAQ pages, 3 comparison landing pages with HTML tables, original research report. Perplexity responded fastest (91% citation rate) while ChatGPT took longer. GEO authority builds exponentially — first 8 weeks modest (3%→30%), last 8 weeks dramatic (30%→87%).
