## Summary
Analysis of 8,500 AI Overviews across 4 platforms (Mar-Jul 2026): only 41-54% of AI citations come from traditional top 10 organic positions. Perplexity shows weakest correlation (31% from top 10). Correlation between organic rank improvement and citation share increase is only 0.34. After Google's May 2026 Core Update, AIO citations from positions 11-50 increased 34%.

## Details
Citation source breakdown: Top 3 = 17-23% (median pos 1-3), Positions 4-10 = 24-31% (median pos 6), Positions 11-50 = 28-35% (median pos 22), Positions 51-100 = 8-12%, Outside top 100 = 7-14%. Queries vary: commercial 45-54% from top 10, informational 38-47%, transactional 52-61%, local 29-41%. Pages citing 8+ external sources received citations 2.1x more frequently.
