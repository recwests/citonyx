## Summary
A mid-sized home fitness e-commerce brand ($1.2M monthly revenue) increased AI Visibility Index from 18 to 79 (+340%) and recommendation rate from 4% to 68% in 8 weeks. Key levers: semantic completeness overhaul and trust signal density.

## Details
Semantic pillar moved from 35 to 82 (+134%) via AI-facing summary blocks, precise entity definitions ('Mid-market home fitness equipment brand for serious home gym builders'), and examples with bounded criteria. Trust pillar moved from 12 to 68 (+467%) through 48 new G2/Capterra reviews in 6 weeks, 3 customer case studies with logos/metrics/quotes, and features in 2 niche publications. Initial problem: AI classified brand as 'generic dropshipping store' with zero authoritative citations and no comparison pages.
