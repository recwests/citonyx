## Summary
OtterlyAI analyzed 1+ million citations across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews (Jan-Feb 2026). Community platforms dominate: Reddit and Quora account for 52.5% of citations vs 47.5% for brand-owned domains. News/media 20.3%, community forums 5.9%. Reddit is the single most-cited domain across all three platforms.

## Details
Reddit dominates every platform analyzed. Editorial content consistently outperforms commercial pages — AI search engines show clear preference for informational over transactional content. Perplexity leans hardest on community sources (16.9% of citations from forums). ChatGPT mentions brands frequently but provides weak link citations, creating awareness without traffic. Google AI Overviews shows the strongest brand preference at 59.8% brand citations but community content remains significant. Strategic implication: authentic community participation and user-generated content strategies are now core GEO tactics, not optional brand-building.
