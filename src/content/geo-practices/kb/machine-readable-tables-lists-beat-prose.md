## Summary
Structured, answer-focused content is 28% more likely to be cited (Yext). Perplexity favors 3–7 citations/answer, high-authority domains, primary sources. ChatGPT favors 2,900+ word long-form (+59% citation) with FAQ/comparison tables. Gemini rewards complete Organization schema.

## Details
44.2% of all LLM citations come from the first 30% of text — front-load citable claims. Declarative, self-contained sentences; avoid "it/this" referring to prior paragraphs. Each paragraph understandable if extracted alone. Comparison pages with 3 tables earn +25.7% citations (AirOps); shortlist pages averaging ≤10 words/sentence earn +18.8%.
