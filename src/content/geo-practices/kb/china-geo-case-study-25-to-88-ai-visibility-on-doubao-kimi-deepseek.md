## Summary
First documented GEO case study for Chinese AI platforms. Global ADN leader targeting enterprise IT teams: AI visibility went from 25% to 88% across Doubao, Quark, Kimi, and DeepSeek. Lead growth +388.9% (44 qualified leads/month), 92% enterprise-level inquiries. Strategy: keyword layering (120+ keywords), content restructuring to problem-analysis-conclusion-case format, custom AI agent for technical consultation, multi-platform coverage.

## Details
Chinese AI platforms represent a fundamentally different citation ecosystem — Doubao (ByteDance), Kimi (Moonshot AI), DeepSeek, and Quark (Alibaba) each have distinct citation patterns. Strategy: full-funnel GEO — discoverability + conversion — with embedded inquiry entry points. 30+ core keywords in Top 3 positions. Leads focused on procurement and solution implementation. Closed-loop system: users discover, evaluate, and convert within the AI interaction journey.
