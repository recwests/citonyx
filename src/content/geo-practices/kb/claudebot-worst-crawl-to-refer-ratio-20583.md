## Summary
May 2026 Presenc AI snapshot of top 1,000 sites: ClaudeBot crawls 20,583 pages per single referral (worst ratio). PerplexityBot delivers ~210:1, OAI-SearchBot best at 85:1. 25% of top sites block GPTBot. All four major search bots respect robots.txt. Recommended strategy: block training bots, allow answer-engine retrieval bots.

## Details
GPTBot blocks plateaued at 25% (up from 5% in 2023). ClaudeBot block rate rising from 9.6% to 10.1%. PerplexityBot returns 10x more referrals per crawled page than ClaudeBot. OAI-SearchBot treated separately at 5% block rate vs GPTBot's 25% — publishers are increasingly differentiating training vs answer-engine crawlers. Google-Extended only governs Gemini training, not AI Overviews. The economic asymmetry means hosting ClaudeBot crawl costs far exceed any traffic value it returns. Publishers should audit crawler-specific referral ROI before blanket-blocking or blanket-allowing AI bots.
