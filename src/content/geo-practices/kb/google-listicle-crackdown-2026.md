## Summary
Google's January 2026 enforcement against self-promotional listicles caused documented visibility losses of 29-49%. ClickUp lost an estimated 7M organic traffic over 6 months. The cascade effect: losing Google visibility can also reduce AI citations across LLMs that rely on Google's index.

## Details
Lily Ray documented ~30 affected sites with identical patterns: dozens/hundreds of self-ranking lists, rapid AI-generated output, artificial date refreshing, and schema misuse. Google spokesperson Jennifer Kutz confirmed active targeting (The Verge, April 2026). Since many AI engines rely on Google's index, this cascades to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Copilot visibility. NP Digital's April 2026 survey found 68% of GEO practitioners still rely on self-published listicles — the highest-risk tactic under active suppression.
