## Summary
AI coverage by content age: under 3 months = 48%, 3-6 months = 39%, 6-12 months = 31%, 12-24 months = 23%, over 24 months = 18% (Erlin, 500+ brands, 2026). Brands updating monthly see ~23% higher coverage. Staleness penalty: 1.8% lost per inactive month. 53% of cited content was updated within the last 6 months.

## Details
Perplexity has extreme recency bias: 82% citation rate for content within 30 days, falling to 37% for older content (Whitehat SEO 2026, via QuickSEO). ChatGPT cites content ~458 days newer than Google on average. Adding a visible 'Last updated' date and one new data point per quarter maintains citation signals.
