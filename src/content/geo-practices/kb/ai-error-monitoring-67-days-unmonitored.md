## Details
Competitive citation displacement is the dominant risk: 80% of lost citations are taken by competitors, with median recovery time of 45 days. High-traffic prompts churn at 23% month-over-month. GEO monitoring is now a brand risk management function, not just an optimization tracking exercise. Only 16% of brands systematically track AI search performance, meaning 84% have no visibility into citation errors. The 67-day detection gap means errors compound before brands respond. Monitored brands using tools like Profound, Otterly, or Erlin catch inaccuracies in pricing, product descriptions, or brand sentiment within two weeks.
