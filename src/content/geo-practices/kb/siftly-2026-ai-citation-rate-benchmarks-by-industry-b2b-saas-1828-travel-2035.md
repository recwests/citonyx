## Summary
Siftly published 2026 AI citation rate benchmarks by industry based on platform data across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. B2B SaaS median 18–28%, Travel 20–35%, Healthcare 10–18%, Finance 8–16%. Citation rates vary 3–4x across industries. Measure citation rate = cited prompts ÷ total tracked prompts, per engine.

## Details
A 'good' citation rate is best judged against industry median rather than absolute numbers. Directions matter more than absolute — track monthly trend. Technology/SaaS top 25%: >38% citation rate, top 10%: >52%. Perplexity consistently shows higher citation rates than ChatGPT across all industries. Methodology: 50 representative queries per industry tested across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews. Key caveat: citation rates are higher for market leaders and lower for newer sites.
