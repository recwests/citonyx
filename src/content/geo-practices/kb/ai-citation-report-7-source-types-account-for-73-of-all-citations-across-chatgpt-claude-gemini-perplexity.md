## Summary
Magna AI's first Annual AI Citation Report (May 2026): 150+ client engagements, 14 industries, standardized monthly probes. Just 7 source types account for 73% of all AI citations. Wikipedia 47% citation density on factual queries. Reddit/Quora = 24% combined. Editorial publications third. Government/.gov/.edu = 11.75x more likely cited than average. Google properties (YouTube) = ~23% of AI Overview citations. Top 50 brands = 28.9% of all citations collectively.

## Details
Platform-specific patterns: Google AI Overviews maintain 93.67% correlation with top 10 organic results (strongest link to traditional rankings). Perplexity emphasizes Reddit (46.5% of citations) with real-time indexing of 200B+ URLs. ChatGPT matches Bing top 10 results 87% of the time, relies heavily on Wikipedia and parametric knowledge. Only 11% of domains cited by both ChatGPT and Perplexity — cross-platform optimization is mandatory. Structural finding: AI answers on factual queries draw heavily from Wikipedia regardless of engine, making Wikipedia presence a foundational GEO tactic for broad factual terms.
