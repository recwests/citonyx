## Summary
Authority Tech's GEO Measurement Framework (Jun 2026): defines 5 core metrics — (1) citation share: % of in-category prompts where your brand is named, (2) AI share of voice: weighted for passage prominence and engine spread, (3) source attribution: which specific pages earn citations per engine, (4) prompt coverage rate: what % of buyer-journey prompts trigger your brand, (5) pipeline-influenced conversions: AI citations → CRM data → revenue. The AI attribution dark spot means GA4 misses AI influence: a ChatGPT recommendation that a user remembers and searches directly appears as 'organic' or 'direct' in analytics.

## Details
Traditional analytics cannot measure GEO: GA4 is referral-based but AI engines often don't produce clickable links. The AI attribution dark spot is growing as AI handles more search volume (25% drop in traditional search volume per Gartner). Citation gap (delta between search ranking and AI citation) averages 34 positions for mid-market B2B brands — ranking 2-35 positions in search often means zero AI citations. A dedicated measurement stack needs: GEO monitoring tool (for citation/prompt data) + CRM integration (for pipeline impact) + survey/test data (for brand lift). Recommended cadence: weekly citation checks, monthly portfolio review, quarterly attribution analysis.
