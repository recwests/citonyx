## Summary
Seer Interactive's dataset of 82,000 Gemini responses across 20 brand workspaces showed citation rate fell from 99% to 76% between February and March 2026 — a 23-point drop. One workspace fell from 96% to 3.7% in a single week. Individual brand impact ranged from -12pp to -92pp.

## Details
This was the single largest AI search citation behavior shift in Seer's entire dataset (monitoring since November 2025). The decline happened without a visible model switch (Gemini 3 had shipped in January). Methodology: citation rate measured per response, not per domain. 99% citation rate meant 99 of 100 Gemini responses included at least one source link. By March, 76 of 100 did. Implication: brands relying on stable Gemini citation rates face structural volatility risk.
