## Summary
Google's March 2026 Core Update was 9.5/10 volatility, targeting pages rephrasing existing top content without adding original information. SEMrush reported 55%+ of sites affected. AI-assisted content with human editing gained +30-80% traffic; unedited AI articles lost 40-90%.

## Details
The update specifically targeted content that rephrases or summarizes existing top-ranking pages without adding original research, data, or insight. Ahrefs found 86.5% of top-ranking pages still contain some AI content — the penalty is not AI use per se but the absence of human-added value. The winning formula: AI drafting + substantial human editing, original data integration, expert review, and unique analysis. The December 2025 Core Update had already expanded E-E-A-T beyond YMYL to all content, creating the foundation for this stricter enforcement.
