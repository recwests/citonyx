## Summary
Multiple independent studies confirm AI-referred visitors convert at dramatically higher rates. Seer Interactive: ChatGPT 15.9% conversion vs organic 1.76%. Perplexity 10.5%, Claude 5%. B2B SaaS sees highest uplift at 23x. AI visitors spend 68% more time on site. AI referral traffic averages ~1.08% of total. Adobe: AI referral traffic grew 693% YoY holiday 2025. HubSpot: 3x better lead conversion from AEO sources. McKinsey projects $750B US revenue influenced by AI search by 2028.

## Details
ConvertMate's GEO Benchmark (12,500+ queries, 8,000 domains) originally found 4.4x baseline. Seer Interactive (June 2025): ChatGPT converts at 15.9%, Perplexity 10.5%, Claude 5.0% vs 1.76% organic. Ahrefs: AI search visitors = 12.1% of signups from 0.5% of traffic (24:1 ratio). Adobe: AI referral traffic grew 693% YoY holiday 2025; AI-driven revenue per visit up 254% YoY. Only 16% of brands track AI search performance. ChatGPT drives 87.4% of all AI referrals. AI platforms strip referrer data inconsistently — ChatGPT appends utm_source=chatgpt.com since mid-2025 but not all links. Yolando: standard attribution misses 60-80% of AI-search revenue. PPL Studio: five-metric framework (citation rate, AI share of voice, referral conversion, brand search lift, pipeline velocity). AWS WAF now enables AI traffic monetization. HubSpot: 3x better lead conversion from AEO traffic. ChatGPT users click 1.4 external links per visit vs 0.6 from Google organic. B2B SaaS firms see the highest conversion uplift — up to 23x vs organic.
