## Summary
Magna's AI Citation Report 2026 analyzed 150+ client engagements across 14 industries. Wikipedia appears in ~47% of factual AI answers across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. Reddit and Quora combined now account for 24% of citations, up 8 percentage points — the biggest source-mix shift.

## Details
Seven source types account for 73% of all citations. The shift from long-tail websites to community platforms is the largest 2025-to-2026 change. Brands with mentions across 25+ trusted sources are cited at ~4x the rate of those in <10 sources. Schema markup correlates with 2.3x higher citation rates. Question-form H2 headings produce 1.8x higher citation rates than statement headings. Review volume (100 reviews at 4.5 stars) cited at 1.6x the rate of 20 reviews at 5.0 stars. The report confirms Wikipedia functions as a shared trust layer: AI models reference it as an independent authority, not a promotional channel.
