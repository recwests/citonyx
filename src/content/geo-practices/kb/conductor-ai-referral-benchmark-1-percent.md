## Summary
Conductor's 2026 benchmark (13,770 domains, 3.3B sessions, 17M AI responses, 100M citations): AI referral traffic = 1.08% of all web traffic, growing ~1% month-over-month. ChatGPT drives 87.4% of AI referrals. IT industry highest at 2.8%.

## Details
AI traffic converts at premium rates but absolute volume remains small relative to organic search. Industry breakdown: IT 2.8%, Finance/Insurance 2.2%, B2B SaaS 1.8%, Ecommerce 0.7%, Healthcare 0.5%. The 1% MoM growth rate means AI referral will double to ~2% within a year. Combined with the attribution gap (70.6% of AI traffic invisible in default GA4), actual AI-driven business impact is significantly undercounted. For the Citonyx testbed: prioritize AI referral as a growth channel, not a replacement for organic. Track separately from organic GA4 with proper attribution setup.
