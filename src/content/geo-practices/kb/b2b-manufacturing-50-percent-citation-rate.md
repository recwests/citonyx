## Summary
A 40-year-old gear factory with near-zero digital presence achieved 50% citation rate across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews in 112 days. AI even cited them for keywords without dedicated landing pages based on accumulated technical authority.

## Details
112-day program (Feb 3 to May 25, 2026): 4 phases — technical deep-dive articles (AS9100, planetary gear design, steel selection), automotive content, EV micro-geometry, medical robotics plus 200-question GEO audit. 10 of 20 core keywords cited by at least one AI engine. Remarkably, 4 cited keywords had no dedicated landing page. Average organic rank improved from 21.29 to 17.79. Engagement rate 65.75% (1.8x site average). Budget: $1,300 per month.
