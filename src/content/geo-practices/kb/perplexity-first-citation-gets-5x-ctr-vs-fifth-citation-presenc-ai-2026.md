## Summary
Presenc AI analyzed Perplexity citation patterns in 2026: first citation gets ~5x CTR of the fifth citation. Average sources per answer rose to 5.8 (from 4.2 in 2024). Research/comparison queries generate highest citation counts (7.3 avg). Citation position correlates with source authority, content relevance, and passage quality.

## Details
Perplexity answers cite average 5.8 sources per response. By query type: factual 4.2, comparison 6.1, research/analysis 7.3, how-to 5.4, current events 6.8, technical 5.1. Position-based CTR (relative): 1st=100 (baseline), 2nd=72, 3rd=51, 4th=33, 5th=21, 6th+=12. First citation is dramatically more valuable than being cited anywhere else. Perplexity's citation-rich format provides most transparent window into AI source selection.
