## Summary
Semrush reports 93% of AI Mode sessions end without a click. AI Overviews: 83% zero-click. Traditional search: ~58.5%. For every 1,000 Google searches, ~360 result in a click. On mobile, zero-click rises to 77%. Strategy must decouple visibility from traffic expectations.

## Details
Ahrefs confirms: 58% lower CTR for position 1 when AI Overviews appear. Seer Interactive: 61% organic CTR drop on AIO queries. Pew: 8% click-through with AI summaries vs 15% without. The practical implication: brands must optimize for "answer presence" (being cited inside the AI-generated answer) as a separate KPI from clicks. Brand search volume becomes a critical proxy metric — users see brand name in AI answers, then search for it directly later. Being cited in the answer is the new ranking.
