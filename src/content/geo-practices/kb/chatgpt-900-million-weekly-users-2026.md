## Summary
ChatGPT grew from 200M weekly active users (late 2024) to 800-900M in 2026 — 4x growth in 18 months. Daily prompts reached ~2.5B, handling ~17% of global digital queries. AI-platform sessions worldwide reached 45 billion per month. Only 14% of marketers actively track AI search performance.

## Details
Data synthesized from OpenAI disclosures, Similarweb, Graphite, QuickSEO, and Conductor. ChatGPT prompt volume jumped nearly 70% between January and June 2025 (Bain). Perplexity now processes 780M search queries/month — 239% growth in under 12 months. HubSpot found 50% of consumers use AI-powered search. McKinsey reports 44% of AI search users call it their primary product-discovery channel vs 31% for traditional search. Google AI Overviews serve 1.5B monthly users. Gemini app crossed 750M monthly users. Gartner predicted 25% traditional search volume drop by 2026. Eight Oh Two's 2026 study of 500 AI users found 37% start search with AI tools. 60% say AI gives clearer answers. The shift from emerging channel to mainstream discovery is complete: AI search is where purchase decisions start, ahead of traditional search, retailer websites, and review sites combined.
