## Summary
Google's Gemini app reached 750 million monthly active users as of early 2026. Combined with ChatGPT's 900 million weekly users, AI search platforms now reach audiences comparable to major social networks. Scale alone makes GEO essential.

## Details
Gemini's 750M MAU represents a 3.8x user growth from its 5.7% traffic share position. The user base is now large enough that citation absence on Gemini means missing a significant portion of the AI-aided research audience. Combined ChatGPT + Gemini reach exceeds 1.5B users — approaching the scale of Instagram or TikTok. For GEO practitioners: Gemini optimization requires different tactics than ChatGPT (schema markup, factual citation structure, entity alignment with Google's Knowledge Graph). Google's distribution advantage (native Android integration, Workspace, Chrome) means Gemini's user base could surpass ChatGPT in 2026-2027.
