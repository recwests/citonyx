## Summary
Study tracked 12,000 AI responses across Google AI Mode, AI Overview, Gemini, and GPT-5.4-mini covering ~200 brands. Only 23.1% of brand mentions include a citation. ChatGPT has highest mention-to-citation rate (28.3%) and cites&names most (92.7%). Category ('best tools') queries: only 7.2%. Google AI Mode cites 33.1 URLs per response (BuzzStream, Jun 2026).

## Details
Data covers 221,946 cited URLs, 47,550 resolved to tracked brand domains. Single-brand queries: 39% mention-to-citation. Head-to-head: 35.8%. Healthcare leads (34.5% mention-to-citation, 82.8% cited-to-mentioned). Retail lowest (16.5%, 49.6%). Mentions and citations must be tracked separately — they measure different things.
