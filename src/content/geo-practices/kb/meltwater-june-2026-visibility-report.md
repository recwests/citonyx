## Summary
Meltwater's June 2026 report (8M+ citations, 8 LLMs) ranked YouTube #1 at 188,863 citations (+56.4% MoM), followed by Reddit 111,860 (+13%), NIH 91,227 (+41.3%), Wikipedia 83,191 (+55.2%). Earned/News sources account for 37.6% of all citations. Press releases declined to 0.2% from 0.4%.

## Details
Per-model ecosystem differentiation confirmed: Claude favors Statista/NIH (data-centric), ChatGPT favors Wikipedia/NIH (institutional), Copilot favors Forbes/LinkedIn (B2B), AI Mode favors YouTube/Reddit/Yahoo (reach engine), Gemini shows balanced distribution. YouTube optimization emerges as a core GEO lever — transcripts, descriptions, chapters, and metadata directly influence citation rates. Source type breakdown: Earned/News 37.6%, Social 5.8%, Reviews 1.6%, Press Releases 0.2%.
