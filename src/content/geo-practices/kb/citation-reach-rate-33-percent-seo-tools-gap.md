## Summary
Citation Reach Rate (CRR) measures the overlap between URLs cited by AI engines and those tracked by SEO platforms. Average CRR is just 33% — 60% of AI-cited URLs are invisible to standard SEO monitoring tools like Google Search Console.

## Details
This means most brands don't know which of their pages are being cited by AI. Standard SEO tools miss the majority of AI citation activity. Brands relying solely on Google Search Console or traditional rank trackers have severely incomplete AI performance data. Dedicated monitoring — manual or automated AI citation tracking — is required to understand actual AI visibility. The CRR metric provides a standardized way to measure monitoring completeness. Combined with the 54x AI attribution undercount in GA, this means most brands operate blind to their AI performance.
