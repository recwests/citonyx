## Summary
OtterlyAI analyzed 379,321 Claude citations across 16,406 domains. Brand/company domains = 64% of all citations. Reddit cited 0 times. LinkedIn alone = 43.1% of social citations. Top 10 domains only 9.5% of citations — extremely distributed. Only 13% domain overlap with ChatGPT.
## Details
Claude sources differently from ChatGPT (only 13% domain overlap). Brand domains dominate but no single brand owns the space (top brand <3% of brand citations). Reference-grade, well-structured content gets cited. News/Media second at 14.9%. Social is negligible — LinkedIn is the only social platform with meaningful citations (43% of social slice). Reddit and X are effectively absent. Education/encyclopedia sources cited repeatedly (anchor domains). Recommendations: build first-party reference pages (product/solution/comparison), structure as documentation, prioritize breadth, invest in professional LinkedIn content, ignore Reddit/X for Claude specifically.
