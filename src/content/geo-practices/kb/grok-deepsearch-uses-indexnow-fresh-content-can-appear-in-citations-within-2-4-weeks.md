## Summary
Grok's DeepSearch mode uses Bing's live index alongside xAI's own crawler. Submitting via IndexNow protocol (available through Cloudflare, Yoast SEO, Rank Math) immediately after publishing is the highest-leverage action for fast Grok citation visibility. Sites typically appear in 2-4 weeks.

## Details
DeepSearch activates most often for recent news, current data requests, comparison queries, and 'what is the best X right now' phrasing. When DeepSearch is off, Grok answers from its trained knowledge base — strategy must target both modes. X (Twitter) co-citation matters: if your domain appears in X posts from authoritative accounts, citation probability increases. No other AI engine has direct access to X's real-time firehose.
