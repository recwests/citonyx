## Summary
Perplexity cites content updated within 30 days at 82% vs 37% for 1+ year old — a 45-point freshness premium (ZipTie.dev). Content older than 60-90 days loses ground without new citations. Perplexity moves fastest: new content cited in days, ChatGPT in 3-5 weeks, AI Overviews in 4-5 weeks.

## Details
RankScope case study: e-commerce brand achieved 26% Perplexity citation rate in 75 days (from 1%), +247% referral sessions, $68K/mo attributed revenue. New buying guides generated citations within 5-7 days. Professional services firm: first Perplexity citations at day 19, ChatGPT day 28, AI Overviews day 34. ConvertMate 12,500-query benchmark: content updated within 30 days earns 3.2x citation multiplier. Ahrefs: AI-cited URLs are 25.7% fresher than non-cited (1,064 vs 1,432 days). Perplexity-first GEO strategy delivers fastest ROI.
