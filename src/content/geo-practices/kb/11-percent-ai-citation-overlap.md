## Summary
ChatGPT and Perplexity share only 11% of cited sources per Averi's 680M citation audit. Alexandrya 2026 benchmarks (340 B2B brands, 4,200 queries): Perplexity cites 34% avg, ChatGPT 21%. Top quartile: 71% on Perplexity vs 52% on ChatGPT. Only 12% of brands maintain high consistency >50% on all platforms. 57% in low bracket. Superlines documented up to 615x citation volume variance for the same brand between platforms. Multi-platform tracking is essential for accurate GEO measurement.

## Details
Averi (March 2026) analyzed 680M AI citations — 11% domain overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity. Passionfruit found 12% across 3 engines. Alexandrya 2026: Perplexity cites 34% avg, ChatGPT 21%. Only 12% of brands maintain >50% consistency across all platforms. ChatGPT cites Wikipedia+Reddit heavily; Perplexity weights on-page structure. Google AI Overviews correlate 93.67% with organic top 10. Slate HQ tracked 300K+ citations across 6 B2B SaaS brands for 90 days: Claude had highest owned citation share at 9.1%, ChatGPT consistently lowest. Superlines documented 615x citation volume variance. Ahrefs found brand mentions correlate 0.664 vs backlinks at 0.218 — 3x stronger signal. ChatGPT drives 87.4% of all AI referral traffic (Conductor 2026). 87% of ChatGPT citations match Bing's top 10.
