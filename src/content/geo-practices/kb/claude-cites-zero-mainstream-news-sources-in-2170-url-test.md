## Summary
Oltre AI analyzed 2,170 URLs cited by Claude Research (powered by Brave Search). Zero citations from Forbes, TechCrunch, NYT, WSJ, Bloomberg. Zero from Reddit, LinkedIn, YouTube, Medium, Quora, Hacker News. 58.5% .com, 28.1% .ai, 5.1% .io. 56% under /blog/ path, 47% listicle-style paths. Only 3% were homepages. 86.7% overlap between Claude-cited URLs and Brave Search top organic results (Profound 2025).

## Details
Claude's live-web research uses Brave Search, not Google. Google SEO playbooks can miss Claude entirely. 24% of cited URLs contained a year token (2024-2026). Claude prefers pages that look like 'answers': deep /blog/ articles, listicles (/best-..., /top-10-..., /alternatives-..., /vs-...), FAQPage and Article schema help. Recommended: publish deep answer pages on predictable cadence, refresh quarterly, use semantic HTML, cite 3-5 authoritative external domains per page.
