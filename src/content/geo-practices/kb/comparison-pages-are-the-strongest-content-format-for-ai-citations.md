## Summary

Analysis of 116 B2B websites found X-vs-Y comparison pages strongest content predictor of AI search traffic. Ranqo's 100K-prompt study confirmed best-of listicles are the single most-cited content format at ~21% of all citations. Highest-leverage pages for brand visibility across AI engines.

## Details

Content cited in AI responses is 25.7% fresher than organic top-10 results. 'Answer capsules' (5+ sentence standalone quotes) get cited 3.2x more. 44% of ChatGPT citations come from the first third of content. Pages with clean hierarchy and list organization give LLMs strongest cues to interpret and cite content.
