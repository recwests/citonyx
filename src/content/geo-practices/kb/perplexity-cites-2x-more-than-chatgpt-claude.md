## Summary
A 7-week automated study (Feb 13-Apr 3, 2026) tracked 2,342 citations across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity with 12 query types. Perplexity cites at 17.7% — 2x ChatGPT (8.8%) and Claude (9.1%). Brand-name queries trigger 100% citations; generic industry queries 0%.

## Details
The experiment ran automated citation checks with 12 different search query types. Generic queries like "best AI consulting firms" returned 0% citation rate for non-major brands — dominated by BCG, Deloitte, and Accenture. The top 10 most-cited companies share common traits: massive external authority, press coverage, directory listings, and industry publications. Confirms AI engines favor established authority over content optimization for generic queries. ChatGPT Search retrieves from Bing index. Perplexity is most retrieval-driven with 30-60 day first-citation timelines. Brand queries are the fastest path to Perplexity citations.
