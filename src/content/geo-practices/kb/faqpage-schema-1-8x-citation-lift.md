## Summary
BrightEdge 2026 data: pages with FAQ schema and bullet lists appear in 73% of AI Overviews citations vs 31% for plain paragraphs. Target Flesch-Kincaid grade level 8-10 — dense academic prose cuts inclusion rates by more than half. Google's AI extraction algorithm continuously rotates citations; defending a citation requires keeping dateModified current and replacing outdated statistics.

## Details
AI Overview extraction pulls from the first paragraph — answer must be in opening 100 words. Each H2/H3 section should start with the answer, not background context. FAQPage schema is the single highest-leverage type for question-based queries (89% trigger rate). HowTo schema for procedural content. Organization schema with sameAs URLs to LinkedIn and company website increased appearance in ChatGPT search by 41% (NetWebMedia client test). Specific numbers, dates, and inline citations are essential for extraction.
