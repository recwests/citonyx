## Summary
Single-day controlled study (July 7, 2026): 8 intent-matched query pairs in Traditional Chinese + English across ChatGPT GPT-5.5, Gemini 3.1 Pro, and Perplexity. ~170 citations analyzed. Zero Chinese-language pages cited by answer engines. Google Chinese SERPs = 86% Chinese content. Answer engines default to English sources or parametric memory.

## Details
Gemini triggered retrieval 0/8 for Chinese queries — answered stale from parametric memory without fetching. Perplexity and ChatGPT consistently returned English-language results even for Chinese queries about Chinese topics. Chinese creator/UGC layer is effectively unindexed by answer engines. GEO = engine × language × query type matrix. Non-English content strategies require fundamentally different approaches. Brands serving Chinese markets cannot rely on English GEO tactics.
