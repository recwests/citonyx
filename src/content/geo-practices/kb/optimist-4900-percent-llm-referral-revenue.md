## Summary
Optimist worked with a large B2B tech company on a 14-month AEO transformation. Results: 4,900% increase in LLM referral revenue (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) and 2,622% growth in LLM referral traffic. Strategy: content architecture redesign for citability, SME bylines, and a proprietary first-party data program (Optimist, Feb 2026).

## Details
The client had strong SEO but zero AI citation presence. Three-layer strategy: (1) content architecture redesigned for AEO — clear claims, structured answers, source-worthy formatting, systematic refresh of existing catalog; (2) SME bylines with attributable expertise; (3) first-party data program with proprietary studies. The brand shifted from 'ranking on Google' to 'being recommended by ChatGPT.' Revenue from LLM referrals grew 49x over 14 months. Source: yesoptimist.com/case-study-aeo-chatgpt-referral-revenue.
