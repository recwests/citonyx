## Summary
Peer-reviewed research using difference-in-differences across 161K matched article pairs and 46.5M observations found AI Overviews reduce daily Wikipedia traffic by ~15%. Cultural content dropped most; STEM showed smaller declines. Estimated 11.5M fewer daily visits (~4.21B/year) redirected.

## Details
The arXiv study (2602.18455) used rigorous causal methodology on 52,262 English articles. A second arXiv study (2604.27790) covering 14,212 queries found generative search shifts visibility away from popular domains toward Google properties. Sites blocking Google's AI crawler don't protect traffic — blocking "accelerates the loss." However, AI-referred visitors convert 42% better, spend 48% longer on site, generate 37% higher revenue per visit. Trade-off: lower volume but higher quality. Organic CTR dropped 61% on queries with AIOs per Seer Interactive.
