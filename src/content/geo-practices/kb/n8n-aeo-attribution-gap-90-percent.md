## Summary
n8n compared GA4 last-touch vs post-conversion survey data. GA4: AEO = 1% of conversions. Survey: AEO = 9% of conversions. 90% of AEO conversions never click a citation link, arrive as 'direct' traffic. AEO conversions growing 2x month-over-month since late 2025. AEO conversion rate 33% higher than organic search.
## Details
Graphite's research on n8n workflow automation platform. Key data points: GA4 last-touch credits AEO with ~1% of conversions, but post-conversion survey shows ~9%. This mirrors social media pattern from a decade ago: strong influence, invisible in last-touch. Since tracking began in late 2025, LLM-attributed conversions doubled month-over-month. AEO conversion rate 33% higher than organic search. Two recommendations: (1) account for this gap when reporting AEO ROI to stakeholders, (2) implement post-conversion survey — single 'how did you hear about us' question is enough.
