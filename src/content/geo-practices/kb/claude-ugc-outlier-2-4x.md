## Summary
Claude cites user-generated content at 2-4x the rate of other AI engines across all sectors, and 10x more than Gemini in Food & Beverage. Powered by Brave search, Claude averages 5.67 sources per answer. Top sources: NYT, The Atlantic, New Yorker, Economist. 3x more likely than ChatGPT to cite content 2-4 weeks old.

## Details
Claude's UGC affinity (powered by Brave search infrastructure) makes Reddit, forum discussions, and community content disproportionately influential in Claude responses. This contrasts sharply with ChatGPT's institutional-source bias (Wikipedia, NIH, brand homepages) and Gemini's brand-owned domain preference. For brands seeking Claude visibility: active community presence, forum discussions, and Reddit engagement produce citation returns that other engines don't match. Claude also tolerates moderately older content (2-4 weeks) better than freshness-sensitive engines like Perplexity.
