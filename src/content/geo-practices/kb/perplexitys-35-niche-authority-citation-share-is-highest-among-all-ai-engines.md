## Summary
Perplexity allocates 42% of citations to news/publisher content, 35% to niche authority sites, 12% to academic/research, and 11% to government/institutional sources. The 35% niche authority share is the highest among all AI engines (Claude 24%, ChatGPT/Google AI ~28%). Perplexity stores 400+ PB of index data (200B+ URLs). Every query triggers live web search — no training-data-only mode. Branded mentions correlate with Perplexity citations at 0.664 Spearman.

## Details
Perplexity's 3-layer RAG pipeline: retrieval (proprietary + Bing), reranking (L3 reranker with quality thresholds), synthesis. First citation gets 5x the CTR of the fifth. Inline per-claim attribution — every claim tied to a source URL. Optimal extractable block: 40-60 words. 18% of answers cite the same domain multiple times from different pages (multi-passage citation). Structured data increases citation likelihood by 2.1x. Original data/statistics increase by 3.7x. Reddit focus mode gives dual advantage.
