## Summary
ConvertMate analyzed 12,500+ queries and 8,000 domains for the first comprehensive GEO benchmark (2026). Key findings: pages >20K chars get 4.3x more AI citations; 83% of AI Overview citations come from outside organic top 10; AI search traffic converts 4.4x better than traditional; 68.7% of cited pages use logical heading hierarchies; 61% of cited pages use structured data (ConvertMate, Mar 2026).

## Details
Corroborated by Princeton GEO framework (Aggarwal et al., KDD 2024, 76 citations) and 2026 industry data (BrightEdge, Semrush, HubSpot). Content updated within 30 days gets 3.2x more citations. Original statistics boost visibility by up to 40%. Brands are 6.5x more likely cited via third-party sources. Only 6.82% of ChatGPT results overlap with Google top 10. 92% of marketers plan to optimize for AI search but only 40.6% currently do. Source: convertmate.io/research/geo-benchmark-2026.
