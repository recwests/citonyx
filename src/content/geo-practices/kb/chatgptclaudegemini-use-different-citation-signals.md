## Summary
RanketAI tracked 50 brand domains across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini (Jan-Mar 2026). Each LLM uses distinct citation criteria. ChatGPT: direct-answer paragraphs + Bing ranking + statistics. Claude: source attribution + verifiability + balanced presentation. Gemini: E-E-A-T + freshness + Google SERP signals. FAQPage schema: 89% Gemini-cited vs 58% Claude. Author info: 91% Claude-cited vs 54% ChatGPT (RanketAI, Mar 2026).

## Details
Cross-LLM cited brands share FAQPage schema + direct-answer paragraphs + author info + external citations. llms.txt adoption rate is 3.2x higher among cross-cited brands. Priority for all: allow GPTBot, ClaudeBot, Googlebot in robots.txt. FAQPage schema + 8+ question-format H2s benefits Gemini and ChatGPT most. Named-author + source links for each claim benefits Claude and Gemini. Direct-answer paragraphs (50-200 chars) benefit ChatGPT and Claude. Source: ranketai.com/en/blog/explainer-ranketai-guide-02.
