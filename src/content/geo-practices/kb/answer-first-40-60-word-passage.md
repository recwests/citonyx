## Summary
Wins Featured Snippets first (strongest AIO predictor), then place a 40–60 word answer directly under each H2. Pew measured median AIO length 67 words; Gemini extracts the passage matching that shape. A page ranking #7 with a clean 50-word answer beats a #1 page with buried intro.

## Details
Use native HTML tables (extracted at 96%+ accuracy vs prose), numbered lists, short 1–3 sentence paragraphs. Avoid putting key data in images/canvas (Gemini can't read). Each H2+answer pair must be quotable in isolation — no "as mentioned above." Cover 5–10 sub-questions per page to feed AI Mode's query fan-out (up to 16 parallel sub-queries).
