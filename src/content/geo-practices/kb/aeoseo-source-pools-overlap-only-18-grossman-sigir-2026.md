## Summary
Research presented at SIGIR 2026 (Grossman) found AEO and SEO source pools overlap only ~18%. A page can rank #1 in Google and still never be cited inside an AI answer. Separate page-level answer-readiness check required beyond traditional SEO optimization.

## Details
This finding underscores that optimizing for search rankings and AI citations require different approaches. Five extraction gates determine AI citability: (1) is the passage retrievable, (2) is the answer extractable as a standalone block, (3) are claims backed by verifiable evidence, (4) is the entity clearly identifiable, (5) are third-party sources corroborating. Only ~18% of pages pass both SEO and AEO gates simultaneously.
