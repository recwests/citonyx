## Summary
Oltre.ai analyzed 2,170 Claude-cited URLs: Claude favors deep blog pages and listicle-style content over homepages, citing almost no mainstream news or social platforms. Claude uses Brave Search for web retrieval. To get cited: publish answer-first sections, add freshness signals, use semantic HTML and structured data.

## Details
Claude's citation patterns differ sharply from ChatGPT: only 13% of cited domains overlap. Claude prefers practitioner blogs over mainstream media. Key factors: answer-first structure in first 40–60 words, named sources with dates, comparison tables, FAQ sections with 6+ substantive answers. Claude rewards content closer to 'reference-grade documentation' than marketing copy. Content sweet spot: 1,800–2,500 words with 7+ H2 sections, at least 1 comparison table, FAQ with 6+ answers.
