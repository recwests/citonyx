## Summary
Only 4.2% of URLs cited by ChatGPT also rank in Google's organic top-10. Classical SEO optimization does not transfer to ChatGPT visibility. The two systems draw from fundamentally different web corpora — optimizing for one does not serve the other.

## Details
Shashko's May 2026 study analyzed 153,425 citations across 6 platforms and 5,000 queries. ChatGPT's near-zero overlap with Google organic is the most extreme divergence documented. The median cited sentence length is 10 words, with nothing over 18 words cited. Cited sentences cluster in the top 37% of the page. Freshness signal intensified: median cited content age dropped from 2.2 years to 298 days. Gemini expanded its text fragment coverage to 84.1%, while Google AI Mode dropped it to 0%.
