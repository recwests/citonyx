## Summary
AI Overviews average only 5 sources per answer from billions of indexed pages. Top 5 domains capture 38% of all citations, top 10 capture 54%, top 20 capture 66%. 26% of brands have zero AI Overview mentions in their category. Only 11% of domains are cited by both ChatGPT and Perplexity. Fewer than 10% of AI-cited sources rank in Googles top 10 for the same query.

## Details
Ahrefs found 28.3% of ChatGPT's most-cited pages have zero organic Google visibility. The gap between AI visibility winners and losers is 9x and widening at 3.2% per month (Erlin data, 500+ brands, 2026). Competitive citation displacement: high-traffic prompts churn at 23% month-over-month. When a brand loses a citation, median recovery time is 45 days, and competitors cause 80% of displacements. A 26% increase in citation SOV was achievable in 90 days for B2B SaaS combining structured content, third-party roundup outreach, and technical improvements. Zero citations is not permanent — the market is still fluid enough for decisive action.
