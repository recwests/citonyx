## Summary
AI citations decay on a monthly timescale — median half-life approximately 4.5 weeks, 40-60% of cited domains rotate month-to-month, 70-90% within 6 months. GEO requires continuous maintenance, not one-time optimization.

## Details
Profound longitudinal tracking of 240M citations. Monthly citation drift: AI Overviews approximately 59.3%, ChatGPT approximately 54.1%, Copilot approximately 53.4%, Perplexity approximately 40.5%. Cross-platform overlap is minimal — approximately 76% of citations unique to a single platform, only approximately 0.8% on all four. Gemini 3 reshuffled approximately 42% of cited domains. New pages can earn first citation in median approximately 6.81 days (Profound, approximately 900 newly-published pages tracked Mar-May 2026).
