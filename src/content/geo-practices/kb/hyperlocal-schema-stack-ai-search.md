## Summary
AI assistants in local search run parallel retrievals, apply hard filters (location, hours, insurance), weight prominence, and compress to 1-3 recommendations. Generic "Chicago dentist" pages don't work. The hyperlocal schema stack — LocalBusiness, Service, Review, FAQPage, OpeningHoursSpecification, acceptedPaymentMethod, paymentAccepted, currenciesAccepted — turns every customer filter into a structured field AI can read.

## Details
Local AI search fundamentally differs from traditional local SEO: AI assistants don't show a map pack — they recommend 1-3 businesses with reasoning. Every customer decision filter must have a corresponding schema field. Neighborhood-level pages naming cross streets, transit stops, and landmarks outperform city-wide pages. The complete schema stack ensures AI engines can verify availability, hours, payment methods, and service areas without ambiguity — critical for the "hard filter" stage of AI local recommendations.
