## Summary
5W Public Relations annual State of AI Search 2026: 15+ categories, 10,000+ engine prompts, year of tracking across 5 engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews). Citation share concentrates faster than market share. Top-cited brand wins disproportionate consideration (self-reinforcing). Gap between leaders and challengers widening year over year.

## Details
Each engine has distinct citation preferences; Reddit is disproportionately influential. Earned media remains strongest single GEO signal. Citation decay is real and measurable within months. 62-page PDF with full category deep-dives, scoring framework (citation share, query share, sentiment, source mix, density). Prescriptive 90-day, 6-month, 12-month playbooks by company size, category, and starting position. Schema markup now table-stakes — its absence is a critical gap; next frontier is structured citation density across third-party sources.
