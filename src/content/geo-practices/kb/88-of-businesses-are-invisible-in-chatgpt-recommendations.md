## Summary

Omni Eclipse manually checked 1,700 businesses across 32 industries in Australia, UAE, and US (Jan-Mar 2026). Only 11.9% appeared in ChatGPT recommendations. 77% of Google page 1 businesses were invisible in ChatGPT. UAE had highest visibility (14.5%), US the lowest (7.1%).

## Details

- Industries with 0% visibility: roofing (US), solar installers (AU), private schools (UAE).
- Strong review ecosystems (50+ reviews), niche specialization, and media presence correlate with visibility.
- Google Ads does NOT transfer to AI visibility — 96% of advertisers invisible in ChatGPT.
- Gartner predicts 25% traditional search volume drop by end of 2026.
