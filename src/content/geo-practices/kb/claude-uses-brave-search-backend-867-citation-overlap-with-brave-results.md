## Summary
Claude uses Brave Search as its web retrieval backend, not Google. Profound research found 86.7% citation overlap between Claude's responses and Brave Search top results. Your Brave Search visibility determines Claude citation eligibility more than Google rankings. Claude cites fewer sources than other platforms but with more prominence.

## Details
Only 16% of brands systematically track AI search across platforms. Claude rewards verifiable credibility: named authors, primary data, structured content, source diversity. A brand with high domain authority but no structured facts, author attribution, or third-party validation will be invisible to Claude even ranking #1 on Google. Claude's crawler infrastructure includes ClaudeBot, Claude-User, and Claude-SearchBot. Optimize for Brave Search index to improve Claude citations.
