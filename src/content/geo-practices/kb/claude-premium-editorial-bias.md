## Summary
Claude citations are powered by Brave Search (86.7% overlap measured by Profound). Claude cites the fewest sources per response (5.67 avg) and biases toward premium editorial — NYT, The Atlantic, The New Yorker, The Economist at 2x+ rate of other engines. Claude has the longest freshness window of any engine.
