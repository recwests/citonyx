## Summary
Rejoice Ojiaku's PEE framework adapts classic essay structure for AI-optimized content: Point (clear, single idea per paragraph), Evidence (stats, use cases, data), Explain (why it matters — AI fills gaps left unexplained). Three quick fixes: lead with answer, support with proof, remove unnecessary complexity.
## Details
The PEE framework ensures every paragraph is a self-contained, citeable unit that an AI can extract independently. Point = one clear claim per paragraph (no multi-idea paragraphs). Evidence = supporting statistic, data point, or use case (AI citations target sentences with numbers). Explain = why the evidence matters — without this, AI may misinterpret or skip the paragraph. Practical application: audit existing content through PEE lens — each paragraph should pass the 'can an AI cite just this paragraph?' test. Most effective for FAQ sections, comparison pages, and how-to content where AI frequently extracts specific answers.
