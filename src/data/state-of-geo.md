# State of GEO

## 1. Thesis

The defining reality of GEO in mid-2026: there is no single "AI citation" game. Five platforms draw from different webs, sharing as little as [11% of cited domains](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-11-percent-ai-citation-overlap). Yet beneath the divergence, every platform converges on entity clarity, structural evidence, and continuous brand footprint expansion. The old question was "how do I rank for this keyword?" The new question is "does every major AI platform know who I am — and can they prove it from my content?"

## 2. The State Now

[AI Overviews appear on 86.7% of US searches](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-ai-overviews-86-percent-us-searches) (commercial-intent 88.5%), reaching 2.5B monthly users versus ~800M for ChatGPT. Google shipped a dedicated AI visibility report in Search Console showing impressions (no clicks, no CTR, no query-level data), and stated that [AIO optimization is SEO for Google specifically](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-search-console-ai-visibility-report). Content with author attribution is [4x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-google-ai-overviews-author-name-bio-required), and the freshness window was cut from 365 to 180 days.

But aggregating platforms conceals more than it reveals.

**ChatGPT** has the narrowest aperture: it cites the vendor's own site [68% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-first-party-site-68-percent-citations), relies on [Wikipedia for 29.7% of its top cited pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency) and brand homepages for 23.8%. ChatGPT largely deprioritizes video, community discussion, and personal blogs — though community platforms account for 52.5% of citations industry-wide. Citation follows steep position decay — the top retrieval result is cited [58% of the time](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-chatgpt-position-citation-decay-58-14), position 10 only 14%.

**Perplexity** is the opposite: [24% niche-site citation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-46-percent-reddit-sources) (2–3x ChatGPT), aggressive freshness premium — content updated within 30 days earns [82% citation rate vs 37% for content over a year old](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points), a 45-point advantage. Perplexity responds to new content in [5–7 days](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-freshness-premium-45-points) vs 3–5 weeks for ChatGPT. It draws [46.7% of its top-10 sources from Reddit](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-perplexity-46-percent-reddit-sources).

**Claude** biases toward premium editorial — citing the New York Times, the Atlantic, and the New Yorker at roughly [2x the rate of other platforms](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-claude-premium-editorial-bias). It has the longest freshness window: 3x more likely than ChatGPT to cite content 2–4 weeks old. Claude's AI visibility [grew 64x](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-previsible-claude-64x-growth) over the tracking period, overtaking Perplexity.

**Gemini** is the most schema-responsive and conservative engine, citing brand-owned sites at [52%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) — the highest rate of any platform. Gemini is the fastest-growing, having [doubled month-over-month](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent). Critically, it shares only [13.7% of its citations with Google's own AI Overviews](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. The Gemini 3 model update collapsed the AI Overview–organic top-10 overlap from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gemini-3-collapsed-top-10-overlap). Fan-out sub-query mapping is now the dominant mechanism.

Across all platforms, the median cited-source half-life is approximately [4.5 weeks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-citation-half-life-4-5-weeks). 40–60% of cited domains rotate month-to-month. Content <13 weeks old is about [2x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-13-week-shelf-life), though a controlled Ahrefs study of 16.975M URLs shows the actual freshness advantage is [25.7%](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-25-percent-advantage) — not the unsourced 4.3x figure. [Community platforms](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-community-platforms-52-percent-citations) capture 52.5% of all AI citations versus 47.5% from brand domains. Top-3 Google pages are [34x more likely to be cited by AI](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-seo-floor-top-3-pages-34x-cited) than pages ranked 31–100 (114,034 observations), confirming that traditional SEO ranking gates AI visibility.

Google's AI Overviews now trigger on [48% of queries](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-google-ai-overviews-48-percent-ctr-drop-61) (healthcare 88%, education 83%, B2B tech 82%). On queries with AIOs, organic CTR drops 61%; even queries without AIOs saw a 41% CTR decline. [83% of AI Overview citations come from outside the organic top 10](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-search-converts-4-4x-better-than-organic). Being cited is now the primary CTR preservation strategy.

[Unmonitored brands take 67 days to detect AI citation errors](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-error-monitoring-67-days-unmonitored) vs 14 days for monitored ones. Error rates: e-commerce 18%, SaaS 12%, finance 11.3%.

The economics are stark: AI traffic converts at a [14.2% average](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-platform-specific-citation-conversion-rates) vs Google organic's 2.8% — 4.4x — with platform spreads (Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%). Brands cited in AI Overviews see [+35% higher organic CTR and +91% higher paid CTR](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-cited-brand-premium-35-91-ctr).

**The schema paradox:** One study shows [2.3x citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-2-3x-citation-lift) from FAQPage, HowTo, Article, and Product schema. Another tracked 1,885 pages adding schema vs 4,000 controls and found [zero meaningful lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-near-zero-independent-effect). Both are valid: schema does not independently _cause_ citation — authoritative sites use both schema and quality content. But attribute-rich Product/Review schema with populated ratings outperforms generic by [20pp (61.7% vs 41.6%)](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-attribute-rich-schema-20pp-citation-lift). [FAQPage was deprecated for rich result display in May 2026](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-faq-rich-results-deprecated-signal-remains), but the AI citation extraction signal remains active. Comparison tables deliver [+34%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift), llm.txt +32%, and FAQ schema +28% coverage lift within 14–21 days.

## 3. What's Winning

The strongest verified signals all prove a brand exists independently of its own website.

**Entity signals dominate.** The SEO-to-GEO Divergence Index shows Brand Entity Mentions at NIS 0.918 versus Domain Rating at [NIS 0.397](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-brand-entity-mentions-surpass-domain-rating) — entity signals are more than twice as predictive as link authority. Across 75,000 brands, branded web mentions correlate at [r=0.664 vs backlinks r=0.218](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks) — a 3x gap. The top quartile averages 169 AI Overview mentions; the next tier manages just 14. The [Brand Stature Ladder](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-stature-ladder-73-44-11-percent) shows global brands at 73% citation rate, mid-market 44%, niche 11%.

**Review depth** is the single highest-correlated variable: [50+ reviews on G2/Capterra = 3.2x citation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-review-depth-strongest-geo-predictor). **Wikidata QID with sameAs schema** lifts citation rates by [40% with zero ongoing cost](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent). Google's [LLM patent](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-google-llm-patent-teaching-ai-who-you-are) confirms the SEO goal is teaching AI who you are. **Third-party sources** account for [68% of AI citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-third-party-sources-68-percent-citations); one source = 18% lift, five or more = 78%.

**Content signals:**

- [Named-source quotations with credentials](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-quotation-addition-strongest-geo-tactic): +42.6% citation lift (strongest Princeton GEO tactic)
- [19+ data points per page](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-data-rich-content-19-stats-5x-citations): 5.4 citations vs 2.8, nearly double. Statistics + sources boost visibility 40%
- [Answer-first architecture](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-answer-first-content-architecture): direct answer in first 1–3 sentences. [44.2% of LLM citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-llm-citations-44-percent-front-loaded) come from the first 30% of text; claim-rich introductions get cited 2.1x more
- [Multi-format content](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-multi-format-content-2-3x-citations): guides + comparisons + how-to = 2.3x citation rate
- [Original datasets](https://citonyx.com/learn/basics#entry-original-datasets-highest-roi-tactic): highest-ROI GEO tactic — AI cannot synthesize data it lacks access to
- [Topic targeting](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-topic-targeting-replaces-keyword-targeting) replaces keyword targeting

**Case study validation:** B2B SaaS [12% to 87% citation rate in 4 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-87-percent-citation-rate-reviews-pr) (G2 reviews, 14 PR placements, comparison hubs, schema). Industrial lighting [88.6% AI recommendation rate](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-88-percent-ai-recommendation-pr-consensus) — own site contributed only 4.5% of citations, PR reprints drove 59.8%. B2B manufacturing [50% citation rate in 112 days without landing pages](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-b2b-manufacturing-50-percent-citation-rate). Fulton [from zero to $18,164/month in 12 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-fulton-35x-ai-revenue-case-study), 700% revenue increase in 6 weeks, Google organic doubled as side effect. Snack brand [75% generative citation rate from zero in 4 months](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-snack-brand-75-percent-citation-rate-ahe-framework) via direct-answer blocks and FAQ/HowTo schema. CITABLE framework from [~500 to 3,500+ monthly AI-referred trials](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-citable-framework-b2b-saas-trials). The compiled benchmark shows [2,300% AI traffic growth](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-geo-case-study-benchmark-2026) with a 90–180 day inflection point, and at least one case achieved [6.8% to 46.7% in 7 days](https://citonyx.com/learn/case-studies#entry-geo-seven-day-visibility-jaeckert-case-study).

## 4. Losing Signals

The KB currently flags no signals as formally deprecated or stale. But several contested findings and structural limitations are worth tracking:

**Google self-conflict.** Gemini and AI Overviews share only [13.7% of citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-gemini-schema-responsive-52-percent-owned) on identical queries. The AIO–organic overlap collapsed from [76% to 38%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-aio-overlap-organic-collapse-76-to-38). Optimizing for one Google surface does not optimize for another.

**The schema paradox persists.** Schema produces [2.3x lift in one study](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-2-3x-citation-lift) and [zero independent effect in another](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-schema-markup-near-zero-independent-effect). The difference is methodological — observational vs controlled — and the resolution is that schema amplifies quality but does not create it.

**Freshness is real but overclaimed.** The controlled advantage is [25.7%](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-25-percent-advantage), not the viral 4.3x figure. But the ~4.5-week half-life means continuity is still mandatory — the penalty for staleness is persistent churn, not a multiplier.

**Only 36 brands** maintain top-100 visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews simultaneously ([Semrush 126M prompts](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-semrush-universal-36-brands)). Those outside this set face structural disadvantages that no single content fix resolves.

**Hallucination trajectories diverge.** [GPT-5-mini hallucinates at 11.4%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gpt-5-citation-hallucination-halved), down from GPT-4o-mini's 45.3%, but Claude Haiku 4.5 increased 8%. Multi-model consensus achieves 95.6% accuracy, but relying on any single platform carries citation integrity risk.

## 6. What To Do

1. **Audit per platform, not aggregate.** Use [GSC's AI Mode and AI Overviews segmentation](https://citonyx.com/learn/tools#entry-gsc-ai-mode-segmentation). Add dedicated tools for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini. [AIO brands cited at 59.8%](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-overviews-brand-citation-rates) vs ChatGPT 44.7% and Perplexity 28.9% — different platforms, different levers. [B2B SaaS dominates at 34% of tracked citations](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-b2b-saas-dominates-ai-citations-34-percent).

2. **Build the entity foundation first.** Wikidata entry + sameAs Organization schema. Zero-cost intervention, [40% citation lift](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-wikidata-qid-entity-seo-signal-40-percent). Brands with Wikipedia earn [3x higher citation rates](https://citonyx.com/learn/entity-seo#entry-chatgpt-wikipedia-29-percent-dependency).

3. **Shift link budget to mention budget.** [Brand mentions are 3x stronger than backlinks](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-brand-mentions-3x-stronger-than-backlinks). PR placements, G2/Trustpilot reviews, Reddit community. One firm used [AI Discovery Files](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-llms-txt-ai-discovery-files-competitive) to score 99/100 against national brands — structured facts over backlinks.

4. **Restructure for passage-level extraction.** Every H2 needs a direct answer block in the first 40–60 words. [44.9% of citation improvement comes from macro-structure](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-document-structure-17-percent-citation-lift). The [GainFrame playbook](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-gainframe-playbook-31-percent-chatgpt) — Quick Answer blocks, question H2s, FAQ schema, IndexNow — made ChatGPT the top acquisition channel at 31%.

5. **Adopt monthly publication cycles.** [Content <13 weeks is ~2x more likely to be cited](https://citonyx.com/learn/content#entry-content-freshness-13-week-shelf-life). Perplexity rewards days-old content; ChatGPT responds in 3–5 weeks. [Comparison tables +34%, llm.txt +32%, FAQ schema +28%](https://citonyx.com/learn/schema-markup#entry-comparison-tables-llm-txt-faq-schema-lift) coverage lift within 14–21 days.

6. **Audit AI crawler access — hard deadline Sep 15, 2026.** [73% of websites block AI crawlers](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-73-percent-websites-block-ai-crawlers). [JS-rendered content fails 77% of parser attempts](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-js-rendered-content-77-percent-fail). [Cloudflare's three-tier classification](https://citonyx.com/learn/technical#entry-cloudflare-three-tier-crawler-classification) blocks Training and Agent crawlers by default on ad-supported pages. Unlike the monthly content cadence, this is a one-off with a fixed date.

7. **Deploy AI Discovery Files.** llms.txt, llms-full.txt, and robots.txt allowances for all three Cloudflare crawler tiers as needed. [GPT-5's reduced hallucination](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-gpt-5-citation-hallucination-halved) makes well-structured content more likely to be cited accurately.

8. **Budget for continuity, not campaigns.** [$35–60K/year](https://citonyx.com/learn/monetization#entry-b2b-geo-budget-guide-35-60k), 15–30% of marketing budget, 12–18 month payback. The [4.5-week half-life](https://citonyx.com/learn/measurement#entry-ai-citation-half-life-4-5-weeks) means GEO is a continuity discipline — once you stop investing, the citations rotate out within weeks.
