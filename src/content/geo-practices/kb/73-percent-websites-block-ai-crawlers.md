## Summary
OtterlyAI analyzed 1M+ citations across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews (Jan-Feb 2026). 73% of sites have technical barriers: robots.txt blocking GPTBot/ClaudeBot, CDN security rules, and JS rendering issues. Schema-tagged chunked content gets 3-5x more citations.

## Details
Community platforms dominate 52.5% of citations vs 47.5% brand domains. Three barriers identified: robots.txt blocks (GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, OAI-Searchbot), CDN restrictions (Cloudflare/AWS), and JS rendering. Specific crawler list: GPTBot for training, OAI-Searchbot for real-time search, ChatGPT-User for Custom GPTs, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot. Content structured with schema markup, chunked format, and clear source attribution gets 3-5x more citations.
