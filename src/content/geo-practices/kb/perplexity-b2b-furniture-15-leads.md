## Summary
A B2B furniture distribution company implemented a citation-first content strategy targeting Perplexity's unique algorithm characteristics — citation-heavy, freshness-priority, real-time RAG — and generated 15 or more qualified sales leads per month. Perplexity's fast re-indexing cycle enabled rapid iteration and optimization based on measured content changes.

## Details
The strategy exploited Perplexity's unique characteristics: (1) citation-first design — every answer must cite sources, making extractable content a direct lead generator, (2) freshness premium — Perplexity updates its index multiple times daily and strongly favors recent content with clear dates, (3) real-time RAG — responds quickly to on-site content changes, enabling rapid A/B testing. The B2B furniture use case is notable because Perplexity's B2B buyer-intent audience converts at higher rates than general search. Content format prioritized: datasheet-style specifications, comparison tables, pricing transparency, and clear "last updated" dates. Confirms Perplexity as the fastest-ROI platform for B2B buyer-intent queries.
