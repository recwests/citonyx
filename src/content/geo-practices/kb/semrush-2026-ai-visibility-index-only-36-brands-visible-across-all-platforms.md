## Summary
Semrush's 2026 AI Visibility Index expanded from 2,500 to 126M US AI search prompts (Jan-Apr 2026). Only 36 brands (Universal 36) maintained top-100 visibility across all platforms every month. On Gemini, overlap between mentioned brands and cited domains is as low as 30%. ChatGPT cites 15 sources per response on average; Gemini cites only 3. Brands in less concentrated categories (Finance top-3 = 41.4%, Industrial = 42.2%) have more opportunity.

## Details
Visibility concentration varies sharply by industry: News/Media top-3 brands = 82.9% of visibility, Consumer Electronics top-3 = 76.9%, Finance top-3 = 41.4%. 81% of organizations that fully integrate SEO and AI visibility reported increased traffic/leads vs 36% for siloed approaches. Patagonia maintained ~79-80 AI visibility score supported by consistent descriptions across OutdoorGearLab, REI, Switchback Travel, GearJunkie, and Reddit.
