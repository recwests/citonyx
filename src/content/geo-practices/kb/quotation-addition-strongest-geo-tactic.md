## Summary
Princeton's GEO paper (Aggarwal et al., KDD 2024) tested 9 content modifications. Quotation Addition: +42.6% Position-Adjusted Word Count — strongest single lever, confirmed in 2026 vendor data. Statistics Addition: +32.8% PAWC. Cite Sources: +27.7% avg, but +115% for rank-5 content. Keyword stuffing: -8.7%.

## Details
Named-source quotations with credentials satisfy generative engines' verification expectations. Hashmeta: 89% of frequently-cited pages have visible bylines vs 31% of rarely-cited. Article schema with author, datePublished, dateModified is critical. 2026 field validation confirms the directional ranking holds — Quotation Addition strongest, Statistics Addition #2. Pages with quotes/statistics show 30-40% higher visibility in AI answers (Superlines 2026). Fluency Optimization: +28.7%. Expert quotes with credentials + source URLs cited more than paraphrased claims.
