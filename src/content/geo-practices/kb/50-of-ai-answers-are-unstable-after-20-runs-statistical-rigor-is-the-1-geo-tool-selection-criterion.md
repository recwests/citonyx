## Summary
AEOsim's 17-criterion comparison of 7 GEO tools (Jul 2026): 50% of AI answers remain unstable after 20 runs — yet most tools sample once per day. There are at least 3 'ChatGPTs' (UI vs API vs mobile) and most tools measure the cheapest API version. Only 2 of 7 tools verifiably probe the user-facing UI. Surface divergence means a brand's citation share can differ 38% on one tool vs 12% on another for the same period.

## Details
Analytika leads at 66/100 (losing on price $200/mo). Shortlist: Otterly $29, Writesonic $79, Peec $95, Profound $99, Searchable $125, Scrunch $250. Top recommendation: shortlist 2-3 tools, run 8 demo checks including 20-run stability test, 3-surface overlap (UI vs API), branded vs unbranded split, crawl-log join. Key metrics to track: citation share (not traffic or mentions — the only metric that survives a model update), AI share of voice (weighted for prominence + engine spread), and source attribution per page. Median citation churn between two ChatGPT runs in 48h = 31%.
