## Summary
High-traffic AI prompts churn at 23% month-over-month. When a brand loses a citation, median recovery time is 45 days, and competitors are the cause 80% of the time (Erlin, 500+ brands, 2026). Unmonitored brands take 67 days on average to discover AI errors; monitored brands detect them in 14 days (79% faster).

## Details
Industry error rates: e-commerce 18%, SaaS 12%, financial services 11.3%. Google AI Overviews are 44% more likely to criticize brands than ChatGPT (BrightEdge, March 2026). Gap between AI visibility winners and losers is 9x and widening at 3.2% every month. Only 16% of brands systematically track AI search performance.
