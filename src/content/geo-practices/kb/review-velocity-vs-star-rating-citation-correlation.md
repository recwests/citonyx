## Summary
Review velocity (rate of new reviews per month) correlates r=0.72 with AI citation rates vs r=0.31 for average star rating. The flow of recent reviews signals ongoing customer satisfaction — AI favors recency in social proof data just as it does in content freshness.

## Details
50+ reviews is the minimum threshold before AI engines consider the review set statistically meaningful. After 50 reviews, velocity (2+ new reviews per week) matters more than absolute score. This extends the freshness preference to user-generated content. Significant for review strategy: incentivize recent review flow rather than just targeting a high average rating. A brand with 100 reviews at 4.5 stars but no recent activity may be outranked by a competitor with 60 reviews at 4.2 stars but earning 3 new reviews per week.
