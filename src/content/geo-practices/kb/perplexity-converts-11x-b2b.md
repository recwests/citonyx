## Summary
Perplexity converts B2B sign-ups at 1.66% vs 0.15% from traditional search — an 11x advantage. Despite holding only 15-20% of AI referral volume, it is the highest-ROI platform per citation earned for B2B brands.

## Details
ChatGPT dominates AI referral traffic volume at 87.4% but delivers low direct clicks — pipeline impact operates through brand recall. Google AI Overviews cut organic CTR by ~35% when triggered, but arriving traffic converts at 6.69% for B2B SaaS. AI search traffic across all platforms converts at 14.2% vs Google's 2.8% organic baseline (Loganix synthesis of six studies spanning 680M citations). Per-model: Claude 16.8%, ChatGPT 14.2%, Perplexity 12.4%. Prioritization heuristic: Perplexity first (conversion quality), then AI Overviews (reach), then ChatGPT (brand recall).
