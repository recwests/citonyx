## Summary
94% of B2B buyers now use LLMs during vendor research before visiting any website (6sense 2026). Sagefrog's "Share of Model" metric measures brand citation share in AI-generated vendor shortlists. B2B AI referral traffic converts at 9-23× the rate of organic. Early-adopter GEO budgets average 12% of digital marketing budget.

## Details
MarketScale confirms 73% of B2B buyers use AI tools during purchase research. Platform preferences differ: Perplexity favors whitepapers with citations; Google AIO prioritizes information gain; ChatGPT prefers structured vendor comparisons. The Share of Model metric is becoming a standard boardroom KPI. Key B2B GEO tactics: source stacking (earning citations across multiple platforms simultaneously), third-party review presence (G2, Capterra), and original research data that creates information gain.
