## Summary
Previsible's 2026 State of AI Discovery Report analyzed 6.77M LLM sessions across 166 GA4 properties (Nov 2024-May 2026). ChatGPT commands 92.4% of trackable AI referral traffic, growing 12.8x over 19 months. Claude grew 64x, overtaking Perplexity in March 2026. Perplexity peaked in March 2025 and fell 61%. Gemini quietly became #2 with 3.2x growth.

## Details
Monthly LLM sessions hit 644,478 in May 2026 (9.9x from Nov 2024). ChatGPT is the only LLM sending meaningful referral traffic in most industries. AI Overviews excluded from this measurement. Claude's 4x acceleration in Jan-Mar 2026 suggests developer/technical audience shift. Perplexity fell from 8.9% to ~1% share. Copilot collapsed 96% from peak. ChatGPT 92.4% share means optimizing for 'AI visibility' without prioritizing ChatGPT is optimizing for an abstraction. AI referral traffic lands on internal search results ~25% of the time.
