## Summary
Concurate applied an 8-step GEO framework for a B2B financing platform: TL;DR sections, author bios, FAQ schema, authoritative backlinks, Q&A-driven blogs, and llms.txt. Results over 4 months: ~100% increase in AI-driven referrals, 315% jump in AI Overview appearances, 158.6% more page-1 keywords, and 300% more qualified leads (Concurate, Jun 2026).

## Details
The client initially had zero AI citation presence despite steady SEO. Key GEO tactics: (1) TL;DR sections for AI interpretation; (2) author names and expert bios; (3) FAQ schema; (4) authoritative backlinks; (5) guest posts with natural brand mentions; (6) Q&A blogs matching generative query patterns; (7) llms.txt file; (8) weekly prompt tracking. AI referral sessions grew from 43 to 86 (ChatGPT: 21→66). Limitations: single client in B2B financing; results may not generalize. Source: concurate.com/generative-engine-optimization-case-study.
