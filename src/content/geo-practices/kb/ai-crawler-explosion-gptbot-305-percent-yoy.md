## Summary
GPTBot grew 305% YoY (May 2024-2025). AI bot share of all bot traffic rose from ~10% to 22% in ~24 months. Meta-ExternalAgent surged to 16.7% (from near-zero). Dedicated training crawlers now generate ~50% of AI bot traffic. Bots are 31.2% of all HTTP requests in early 2026; trajectory crosses human traffic before end of 2027. Cloudflare's default config now blocks AI bots — many sites lose visibility accidentally by using Cloudflare defaults.

## Details
Q1 2026 AI bot market share: Googlebot (AI portion via Google-Extended) ~22%, Meta-ExternalAgent 16.7%, GPTBot 12.0%, ClaudeBot 11.7%, Bytespider 7.1% (collapsed from 42% due to geopolitical blocking), Applebot 5.8% (+124% single month from Apple Intelligence EU rollout), PerplexityBot 4.2%, OAI-SearchBot 3.4%. Ten AI crawlers now matter for visibility. Critical distinction: browse bots (ChatGPT-User, Claude-Web) vs training bots (GPTBot, anthropic-ai). Hybrid policy recommended: allow retrieval/search bots, block training bots on sensitive directories.
