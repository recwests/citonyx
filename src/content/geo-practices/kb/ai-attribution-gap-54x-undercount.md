## Summary
AI referred 27% of new signups but Google Analytics attributed only 0.5% — a ~54x attribution gap. Standard analytics tools cannot properly attribute AI-referred conversions because most AI traffic arrives without HTTP referrer headers (direct/typed-bookmarked in GA).

## Details
This means brands drastically underestimate AI's contribution to conversions. Dedicated AI attribution — UTM-tagged AI-specific links, custom tracking, or AI-citation monitoring tools — is essential for accurate ROI measurement. Without it, GEO investment appears to have negative ROI when it actually delivers 54x more conversions than tracked. Combined with the 33% Citation Reach Rate (60% of AI-cited URLs invisible to SEO tools), most brands operate blind to their actual AI search performance. Fixing attribution is the first step to justifying GEO investment.
