## Summary
Industrial lighting brand Amico achieved 88.6% AI recommendation rate across Google AI Overview, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot. Key insight: brand website contributed only 4.5% of citations — PR syndication drove 59.8%.

## Details
Amico had zero AI presence despite strong Amazon/Home Depot listings. The 10-prompt commercial buying taxonomy plus entity clarity plus PR syndication through high-authority channels (barchart.com 59.8%, financialcontent.com) plus extractable comparison pages drove results. Per model: Google AI Overview 100%, Gemini 97%, Perplexity 94%, AI Mode 91%, Copilot 60%. Average recommendation position was 1.05. Monitoring period Dec 2025 to Mar 2026.
