## Summary
Brandofy tracked hundreds of brands across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Overviews. Most correlated variable: review platform depth on G2 (B2B), Capterra, Trustpilot. 50+ recent reviews = 3.2x citation rate vs <20 — holds when controlling for market share and traffic. Single highest-impact GEO activity.

## Details
OverTheTopSEO case study (ProjectFlow): G2 review campaign from 43 to 287 reviews was the highest-impact single activity. Result: 0% to 87% AI citation rate in 4 months. 23% of AI brand mentions include at least one significant inaccuracy or negative qualifier. 61% of brands with declining citation rates had a specific competitor action (review campaign or editorial coverage) preceding the decline. Top 2-3 brands in any category capture 71% of all AI citations on average.
