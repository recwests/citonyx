## Summary
Perplexity processes 780M+ monthly queries with a 3-layer reranking pipeline. Factor weights: content relevance 30%, visual/citation placement 20%, domain authority 15%, content freshness 15%, source diversity 10%, structured data 10%. Commercial queries emphasize trust signals (G2, Capterra, TrustPilot) more heavily.

## Details
Perplexity is fundamentally different from ChatGPT/Claude — it is a real-time retrieval engine with inline source links on every answer. Its 3-layer reranking pipeline distinguishes it from simpler RAG systems. Informational queries weight relevance more heavily; commercial queries emphasize trust signals. Perplexity rewards recency heavily: articles under 30 days get cited 3.2x more frequently. Brands mentioned across Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn get a 10% boost from source diversity signal. Understanding these weights enables targeted content and distribution strategy.
