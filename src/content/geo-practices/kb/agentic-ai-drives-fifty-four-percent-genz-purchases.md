## Summary
McKinsey 2025-2026 research: AI assistant recommendations now drive 54% of millennial/GenZ purchase decisions vs 31% for traditional search clicks. 23% of organizations already scaling agentic AI in at least one function, 39% actively experimenting. Cisco projects 56% of customer support interactions will involve agentic AI by mid-2026. GEO objective expanding from being mentioned to being cited in AI recommendations that drive automated action.

## Details
McKinsey projects ~$750B in US revenue flowing through AI-powered search channels by 2028. Enterprise AI adoption jumped from 55% to 78% in a single year. Practical GEO implication: audit brand representation in AI responses not just for accuracy but for actionability — can an AI agent correctly book, purchase, or recommend based on structured data? Agentic AI requires deterministic, schema-rich, factually precise content that leaves no ambiguity for automated decision-making. Brands optimizing only for human consumption via traditional AI Overviews miss the agentic AI wave.
