## Summary
No universal GEO playbook exists. Marketbridge analyzed 17,000+ AI citations across industries: AI engines do not evaluate credibility uniformly. SaaS driven by comparison pages; healthcare by authoritative medical sources; finance by regulatory compliance signals.

## Details
In B2B purchase context, 89% of buyers go straight to an LLM for a shortlist (Forrester 2026). Reddit's outsized citation rate is a general-query phenomenon — when isolating industry-specific prompts, citation sources shift dramatically. SaaS visibility driven by comparison pages and feature tables; healthcare by authoritative medical sources and peer-reviewed content; finance by regulatory compliance signals and brand trust. Recommendation: build deeply informed, industry-specific GEO strategies grounded in how each market earns trust. Generic best practices (FAQ schema, llms.txt, brand mentions) help but don't substitute for vertical expertise.
