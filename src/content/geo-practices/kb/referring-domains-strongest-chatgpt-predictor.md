## Summary
SE Ranking's analysis of 216K pages found referring domains are the strongest ChatGPT citation predictor: sites with 350K+ RDs average 8.4 citations vs 1.6 for sites under 2,500 RDs. Notably, ~28% of ChatGPT-cited pages had zero organic search visibility — AI citation mechanisms differ fundamentally from traditional ranking.

## Details
Pages invisible to traditional search can still get AI citations if they have strong backlink profiles. The ~28% of cited pages with zero organic visibility suggests ChatGPT's citation mechanism relies more on web-wide authority signals than Google's ranking algorithm. This creates an opportunity: sites that have earned links (through PR, partnerships, or industry recognition) but don't rank on Google can still achieve ChatGPT citations. However, store-bought or low-quality backlinks do not transfer to AI citation value — only earned, contextual links drive this signal.
