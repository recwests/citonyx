## Summary
Only 12% of local businesses have an active GEO strategy (GrowthPro AI 2026). With AI-powered local search growing rapidly and AI assistants recommending only 1-3 businesses, early adopters capture disproportionate share before competition arrives. The window for first-mover advantage in local GEO is open but narrowing.

## Details
The 88% gap represents both a massive opportunity and an impending risk. As AI-powered local search adoption grows, businesses without GEO-optimized local presence will be invisible to AI assistants. Unlike traditional local SEO where a Google Business Profile guaranteed some visibility, AI assistants apply hard filters and compress recommendations to 1-3 results — the penalty for non-participation is total exclusion. The low adoption rate means early movers capture citation share that will be increasingly difficult to dislodge as AI assistant usage grows.
