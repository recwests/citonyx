## Summary

Zyppy research on ChatGPT citations shows the first 30% of a page accounts for 44.2% of all LLM citations. FAQ schema correlates with ~40% higher citation weighting in ChatGPT. Pages with FAQ schema and inline citations see measurably higher source selection rates.

## Details

- Positioning: 30-70% of page = 31.1% of citations; final 30% = 24.7%.
- Structured data markup (FAQPage, Article, BreadcrumbList) gives 73% improvement in AI Overview selection rates.
- Front-load answers: put direct response and strongest data in the first third.
