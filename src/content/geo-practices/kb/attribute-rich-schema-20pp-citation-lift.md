## Summary
730 AI citations studied: generic schema (Article, Organization, BreadcrumbList) provides zero measurable AI citation advantage. Attribute-rich Product/Review schema with populated pricing, ratings, specifications outperforms by 20pp (61.7% vs 41.6%, p=.012). Largest effect for domains with DR ≤60.

## Details
Corrected GEE model found schema presence odds ratio 0.678 (p=.296) — null effect for generic types. Ahrefs tracked 1,885 pages adding JSON-LD schema vs 4,000 controls: AI Overview citations showed 4.6% decline relative to control. AI Mode (+2.4%) and ChatGPT (+2.2%) changes were insignificant. FAQPage schema remains effective: Onely found 3.2x citation rate (41% vs 15%). The @graph pattern with stable @id URIs is the single most under-deployed B2B technique. Schema without authority is no better than no schema.
