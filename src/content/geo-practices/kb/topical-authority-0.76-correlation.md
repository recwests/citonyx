## Summary
Cite.solutions found topical authority (0.76 Spearman correlation) predicts ChatGPT citation rates more than 4x better than domain authority (0.18). Deep topical coverage within a subject area matters far more for AI citations than traditional SEO authority metrics.

## Details
This finding challenges the assumption that only high-DR sites get AI citations. Topical authority — measured as depth and breadth of coverage within a specific subject area — is the dominant predictor. A focused site with 200 deeply-researched articles on one topic outperforms a general site with high DR but shallow coverage. This validates the "content clusters" and "pillar page" approach for GEO: build comprehensive topical libraries rather than spreading content thinly across many subjects.
