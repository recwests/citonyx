## Summary
Google rolled out hover pop-up link cards in AI Overviews and AI Mode (Feb 2026). On desktop, hovering over highlighted text within an AI Overview shows link cards allowing direct navigation to source websites. This could help reverse some AIO-driven click-through rate decline by making citations more accessible.

## Details
Link icons within AI Overview responses are now more descriptive and visually prominent on both desktop and mobile. The 'Show more' button in AI Overviews now transitions users into AI Mode — a conversational experience maintaining context. This design is a deliberate effort by Google to keep users within its AI ecosystem. Google VP Robby Stein: 'people prefer an experience that flows naturally into a conversation.' Available on mobile devices first.
