## Summary
URL volatility is structural: Google AI Mode returns 91% different URLs on repeat same-day searches, and 40–60% of cited sources change monthly. Optimize for candidate-pool inclusion, tracked across many repeat runs.

## Details
eMarketer's Nate Elliott notes 40–60% of cited sources change month-to-month across Google AI Mode and ChatGPT, making AI visibility far less stable than organic rankings. KIME found AI Overviews vs AI Mode share only 10.7% URL overlap / 16% domain overlap. The strategic fix: stop measuring single-snapshot yes/no visibility; measure citation share across many repeat runs per query (60%+ = durable, <20% = unreliable). Optimize for inclusion in the engine's candidate pool via structured content, FAQ schema, dated original data, earned media, strong Bing visibility, and clear entity signals.
