## Summary
Presenc AI compiled documented GEO case studies: Diggity Marketing 2,300% AI traffic growth in manufacturing (6 months), Hedges Company 200% monthly in auto parts, fintech Ramp 7x visibility (3.2% to 22.2%), legal services firm 312% organic growth with 4.2x AI referral conversion rate. Four consistent patterns emerge.

## Details
Four patterns across all successful case studies: (1) content restructuring into answer-first/extractable format, (2) original research and proprietary data, (3) schema markup implementation, (4) earned media/third-party coverage. Brands implementing at least 3 of 4 saw meaningful lift within 90-180 days. Smaller brands often go from near-zero to measurable AI presence within 6 months with larger proportional lift. Below 60 days is unrealistic; 6 months is the common inflection point.
