## Summary
The before/after isn't "more content" — it's "same content, restructured." Question-led H2s, 40-60 word direct answer, then expanded context, then FAQ schema. HubSpot achieved 3x lead conversion from AEO traffic with this pattern.

## Details
HubSpot 2026 State of Marketing: 58% of marketers say AI-referred visitors convert higher. HubSpot's pattern: question-led H2 → 40-60 word direct answer → expanded context → structured FAQ with FAQPage schema. Hashmeta e-commerce: +50% zero-click visibility from rebuilding product pages around explicit question intent + HowTo and FAQPage schema. ZeroClickHero ecommerce case: 38% AI citation rate, 4.1% conversion, $9,300/month AI-attributed sales, 78% higher AOV than Google traffic. Best practice: sections of 120-180 words between headings create clean extraction boundaries. TL;DR sections, bullet points, and data blocks most likely to be quoted.
