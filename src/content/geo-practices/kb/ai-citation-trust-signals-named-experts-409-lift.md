## Summary
Research on AI citation trust signals: named expert quotes with credentials produce +40.9% citation lift. Statistics paired with named sources produce +30.6% lift. Only 38% of AI-cited sources rank in Google top 10. 88% of Google AI Mode citations come outside organic top 10. ChatGPT drives 87.4% of all AI referral traffic (AuthorityTech, Jul 2026).

## Details
ChatGPT retrieves through Bing's index, favoring consensus sources, named authors, and listicles. It cites 7-8 sources per response but only ~15% of retrieved pages (Zyppy 2025). Named-author odds ratio 1.40 vs 1.12 overall — 25% more likely cited. Perplexity cites on 100% of queries at 13.8% per-query rate, with 80% of cited content not ranking in Google top 10.
