## Summary
First large-scale study of citation durability across 23M cited sources: 44% are one-hit wonders appearing in exactly one AI answer. Only durable citations compound into brand authority. Perplexity retains sources ~2x longer than ChatGPT.

## Details
Writesonic's index of 2B AI conversations tracked 23M cited sources across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and AI Overviews (Apr–Jun 2026). Median citation lifespan: 11-15 days. After GPT-5.5 update (May 2026), ChatGPT's share of citations to third-party sites jumped to 96%, but average vendor site captured just 4.3%. In 90% of cases, when a new brand enters an AI answer, another brand gets dropped — fixed seats dynamic. Operational takeaway: durable citations (not just getting cited once) is the real KPI. Getting re-cited consistently requires content refresh cycles aligned with platform-specific half-lives.
