## Summary
Multiple studies converge: AI citations heavily favor fresh content. Ahrefs analysis of 17M citations: AI-cited content 25.7% fresher than organic. ChatGPT shows strongest recency: 76.4% of most-cited pages updated within 30 days. Effective shelf life for AI citation eligibility ~13 weeks. Content updated within 3 months is ~2x more likely to be cited.

## Details
Platform ranking by freshness obsession: Perplexity (most extreme, ~50% current-year), ChatGPT (strong recency), Gemini (balanced), Google AI Overviews (least). Implementation: quarterly update cadence for high-value pages. Add visible Last Updated dates and changelogs. Use dateModified schema reflecting real changes. At least 20% substantive revision required — changing date alone risks Google spam penalties. Prioritize statistics-heavy and comparison pages first — these compound freshness with statistics citation advantage.
