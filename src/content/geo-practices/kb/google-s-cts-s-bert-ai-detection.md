## Summary
Google operates two interconnected AI detection systems: S-CTS (Scalable Cluster Termination System) identifies entire networks of AI content generators by infrastructure and velocity patterns, terminating all connected sites simultaneously. S-BERT detects the mathematical 'signature' AI text leaves — persistent even after paraphrasing or switching models.

## Details
S-CTS signals include: narrative templates repeated across thousands of pages, publishing velocity (200+ articles in 3 days), shared server/API infrastructure, and cross-domain relatedness. S-BERT converts text into vector embeddings and compares against a corpus of known AI-generated content — changing vocabulary does not evade detection. LoRA updates within days of new model launches. Protection: original human insight, proprietary data, controlled publishing velocity, diversified infrastructure.
