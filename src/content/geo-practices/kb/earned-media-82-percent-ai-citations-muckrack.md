## Summary
Muck Rack's Generative Pulse research (May 2026, 25M citations) found 82% of AI citations come from earned media. Stacker/Scrunch controlled study: identical content distributed via third-party news outlets produced 239% median lift in AI visibility (34% citation rate vs 11% for brand-owned). Averi 2026: brand-owned websites contributed just 11.6% of vendor citations in B2B categories (40 categories analyzed).

## Details
EMNLP 2025 research confirmed LLMs evaluate the publication name before content quality — Forbes article about your product earns citations an equivalent blog post cannot. 73.6% of PR professionals now use AI for campaign ideation. 66.2% track AI citations as a KPI (up from near-zero 18 months ago). The digital PR market projected $25.4B by 2032. Effective formats: original data/reports (citation magnet), expert commentary, case studies, comparison content, and tools/calculators. AI citation rates highest for content published within first 7 days.
