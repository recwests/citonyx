## Summary
Profound's 240M-citation longitudinal tracking reveals content citation half-life ranges 3.4-5.7 weeks depending on platform. Monthly citation drift: AI Overviews 59.3%, ChatGPT 54.1%, Copilot 53.4%, Perplexity 40.5%. Cross-platform overlap is minimal — ~76% of citations are unique to a single platform.
## Details
The 3.4-5.7 week range is platform-dependent: Perplexity decays fastest (3.4 weeks due to freshness aggression), AI Overviews mid-range (4.7 weeks on commercial queries), ChatGPT longer (5.7 weeks due to Bing index latency). New pages earn first citation in median ~6.81 days (Profound tracking 900 pages, Mar-May 2026). Gemini 3 reshuffled ~42% of cited domains. Only ~0.8% of citations appear on all four major platforms. The practical implication: GEO is a continuity discipline requiring monthly content refresh cadences, not quarterly or annual optimization. Content decay is the hidden tax on one-time GEO efforts.
