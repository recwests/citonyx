## Summary
Ahrefs found that only 37.9% of pages cited in Google AI Overviews also rank in the top 10 organic results as of March 2026, down from 76% in August 2025. Being #1 in Google no longer guarantees AI Overview citation. AI citations have decoupled from traditional ranking as an independent optimization target.

## Details
The 38 percentage point collapse in 7 months represents the fastest decoupling of traditional and AI search metrics ever documented. The mechanism: AI Overviews use query fan-out (8-16 sub-queries per query), naturally diversifying sources beyond what traditional ranking serves. This means content optimized solely for Google ranking is increasingly invisible to AI systems — and vice versa. Brands with strong organic rankings but poor answer-first content quality lose AI visibility. Publishers tracking only Google Search Console see a shrinking portion of their total search footprint. The 37.9% overlap is the current baseline and continues declining.
