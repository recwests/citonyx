## Summary
Mike King's Relevance Engineering framework separates AI search from checklist SEO. Three-bucket measurement: input metrics (fan-out query rankings, passage relevance, bot activity), channel metrics (SOV, citation rate, sentiment), performance metrics (conversions, revenue). Generated $26M additional value for one client.
## Details
Team composition for Relevance Engineering: AI engineer + content strategist + UX + digital PR + SEO — not just an SEO play. Input metrics track whether content is retrievable (query fan-out coverage, passage-level relevance scores, AI crawler activity). Channel metrics track whether it's cited (share of voice in AI answers, citation rate across platforms, citation sentiment). Performance metrics track whether citations drive business outcomes (conversions, revenue, lead quality). The framework rejects 'set and forget' GEO — requires continuous monitoring and iteration across all three buckets.
