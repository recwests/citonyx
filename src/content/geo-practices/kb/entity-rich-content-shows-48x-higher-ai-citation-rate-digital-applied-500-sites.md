## Summary
A 2026 study of 500 B2B SaaS sites (Digital Applied) found content with 15+ connected entities shows 4.8x higher AI citation probability. Entity clarity correlates at +0.71 with citation rate vs domain authority at only +0.18. Google's Knowledge Graph now holds 500B+ facts on 5B+ entities.

## Details
68% of B2B buyers now use LLMs as primary vendor research tool (Machine Relations, 2026). Entity SEO: build cross-referenced entity signals (Wikidata, GBP, Organization schema with sameAs array linking to LinkedIn/Crunchbase, NAP consistency). Six entity signals AI engines evaluate: name resolution, sameAs corroboration, knowsAbout declarations, NAP consistency, topical co-occurrence, and third-party entity reinforcement. 6–12 months to see measurable improvement.
