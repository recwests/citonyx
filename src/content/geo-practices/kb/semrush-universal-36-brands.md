## Summary
Semrush's 2026 AI Visibility Index scaled from 2,500 to 126M US AI search prompts (Jan–Apr 2026) across ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Mode, and AI Overviews. Only 36 brands maintained top-100 across all 4 platforms. ChatGPT cites ~15 sources/response; Gemini cites ~3. Mentions ≠ citations — on Gemini, overlap can be as low as 30%.

## Details
Industry concentration varies: News top-3 share = 82.9%, Consumer Electronics = 76.9%, Finance = 41.4%, Industrial = 42.2%. Patagonia maintained ~79-80 AI visibility score supported by OutdoorGearLab, REI, GearJunkie, Reddit. Integrated SEO+AI teams reported 81% traffic increase vs 36% for siloed teams. ChatGPT cites ~15 sources/response; Gemini ~3. The Universal 36: YouTube, Google, Reddit, Amazon, Facebook, Apple, Walmart, Disney, Nintendo, and 27 others.
