## Summary
Local AI search ranking factors in 2026 follow a hierarchy: proximity (primary, geographic distance), review count and sentiment (secondary, social proof volume), citation consistency (tertiary, NAP alignment across platforms). Traditional local SEO signals apply but AI weights them differently.

## Details
AI local search differs from Google's local pack in three ways: (1) proximity weighting is more extreme — AI models strictly prioritize the nearest relevant business, narrowing the service area that can compete, (2) review depth (detailed, recent reviews with specific keywords) outperforms star rating alone — a 3.8-star business with 200 detailed reviews may outrank a 4.5-star business with 20 generic reviews, (3) citation consistency across Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing Places, and Facebook is cross-referenced — any NAP inconsistency creates AI hesitation. The 45% overlap between map pack and AI local results means separate optimization tracks.
