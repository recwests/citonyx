## Summary
Chatarmin's CMO Richard Wagentristl reports ChatGPT grew from <1% to 5-10% of booked demos. No single 'secret sauce' — channels compound. Marketing budget €1M/yr, 5-person team. Gemini rising, ChatGPT cooling, Claude on the rise.
## Details
Chatarmin's demo channel breakdown: LinkedIn ~20%, Google/Google Ads ~20%, ChatGPT 5-10% (from <1%), partners/referrals significant. Marketing budget: ~€1M/yr (payroll included), 5 people (1 in-house SEO, 2 content, 1 GTM engineer, 1 head). No single growth hack — SEO, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok all compound together. AI engine observations: Gemini gaining from Google ecosystem, ChatGPT cooling from peak, Claude rising. Key advice: start with free channels (content compounds), radical transparency as differentiator, let data pick topics (scrape content performance, double down on winners).
