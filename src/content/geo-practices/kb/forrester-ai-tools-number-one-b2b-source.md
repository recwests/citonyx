## Summary
Forrester's January 2026 research confirms AI tools are now the most-cited interaction type in B2B buying — ahead of vendor websites, product experts, and sales. The GEO-16 framework audited 1,700+ citations and found page quality score predicts citation at odds ratio 4.2.

## Details
The reformat playbook for B2B case studies: (1) lead with the result as a direct statement near the top, (2) use structured data (FAQPage, Article, HowTo schema), (3) format for machine extraction, not narrative flow. Standard case studies (challenge → solution → results narrative) fail AI extraction. The fix takes hours per asset, not months. AI systems favor original, expert-driven, human-authored material. Customer success stories represent third-party evidence these systems prioritize — but only when formatted correctly. If your case studies aren't getting cited, it's a format problem, not a quality problem.
