## Summary

Microsoft Clarity research revealed AI referral traffic converts at 1.66% versus 0.15% from organic search — an 11-fold advantage. A Broworks case study showed 10% of total organic traffic coming from LLMs, with 27% converting into Sales Qualified Leads. GrackerAI compendium of 8 cybersecurity case studies confirmed 3-5x higher conversion.

## Details

B2B buyers using AI assistants provide detailed context about tech stack, budget, and pain points upfront. AI synthesizes personalized recommendations. Prospects arriving from AI citations are past awareness stage and actively building shortlists. Average annual GEO investment $78K vs $560K+ for equivalent traditional content team (86% cost reduction).
