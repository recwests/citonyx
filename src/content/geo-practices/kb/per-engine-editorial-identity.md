## Summary
Optimize per engine, not per intent category. Match each engine's editorial identity (default source type per intent) before scaling content volume. Brand search volume is the strongest predictor of AI citations (r=0.334 cross-platform, 0.664 for Google AIO).

## Details
Only 11% of cited domains appear on both ChatGPT and Perplexity for identical queries. YouTube transcripts, image metadata, and podcast audio are now indexed (multimodal). Gemini is natively multimodal, evaluating entities across text/visual/video via cross-modal embeddings. Diversify content across formats, not just topics.
