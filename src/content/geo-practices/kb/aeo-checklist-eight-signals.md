## Summary
AuthorityTech's 2026 AEO framework identifies 8 weighted citation signals. Top 4 — FAQ Quality (20%), Answer Clarity (19%), Statistical Density (16%), Heading Structure (16%) — collectively drive 71% of AI citation likelihood. 44.2% of LLM extractions come from first 30% of page body.

## Details
The full 8-signal weight distribution: FAQ Quality 20%, Answer Clarity 19%, Statistical Density 16%, Heading Structure 16%, Content Freshness 8%, AI Crawler Access 8%, Schema Coverage 7%, Author Attribution 6%. Critically, 44.2% of LLM extractions target the first 30% of page body content — front-loading key answers and data is essential. FAQPage schema, clear direct answers under H2s, statistical evidence, and clean heading hierarchy form the foundation of AI-citable content architecture.
