## Summary
YouTube overtook Reddit as the #1 social citation source in LLM answers. YouTube now appears in 16% of LLM answers vs Reddit's 10% — a complete reversal from mid-2025. YouTube generated 188,863 citations across eight major LLMs in May 2026, growing 56.4% MoM. Reddit stock dropped 9.3% on the news. LinkedIn emerged as a surprise competitor with 15,835 citations. Google AI Overviews cite YouTube (18.8%) and Reddit (21%) at near parity.

## Details
Meltwater's May 2026 analysis of 8M+ citations. Four independent research firms (Bluefish, Emberos, Goodie AI, Profound) confirmed YouTube appears in 16% of LLM answers vs Reddit's 10%. YouTube cited 200x more than any other video platform. YouTube's share of social citations doubled from 18.9% to 39.2% between August and December 2025, while Reddit's share halved from 44.2% to 20.3%. Superlines first-party data shows Reddit still leads in absolute citation volume: 39,551 vs YouTube's 15,735 over 30 days. LinkedIn emerged with 15,835 citations. Perplexity still cites Reddit 6.1x more than YouTube. 86% of AI citations come from brand-managed sources; YouTube is additive not replacement. Google AI Mode alone generated 73,813 YouTube citations, AI Overviews 38,002, Gemini 37,924, Perplexity 25,949. Brands need transcripts, chapters, and structured metadata for YouTube content to be AI-citable.
