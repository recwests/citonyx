## Summary
Gemini cites brand-owned websites at 52.15% — highest of any major engine (Yext 6.8M citations). However, Gemini and AI Overviews share only ~13.7% of citations on same queries. Gemini has 26% institutional citations, 0.2% UGC — most conservative engine. Knowledge Graph connection makes entity clarity the highest-leverage signal.

## Details
Gemini inherits Googlebot's rendering pipeline (executes JS) unlike other AI crawlers. BrightEdge peer review: Gemini shows "conservative institutional trust," preferring .gov and .org at highest rates. AI Mode shows strongest mix of review sites, finance data, and news media (UGC ~7%). Gemini vs AI Mode overlap: 27%. Gemini vs AI Overviews overlap: 34%. AI Overviews and AI Mode share only ~13.7% citations. Organization schema + sameAs + Wikidata are highest-leverage for Gemini.
