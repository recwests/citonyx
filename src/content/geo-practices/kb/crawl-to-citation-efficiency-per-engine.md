## Summary
Presenc AI (April 2026) first published data joining crawl events to citation outcomes across major AI platforms. PerplexityBot+user-fetcher achieves 5-10x higher crawl-to-citation efficiency than training-focused crawlers. Perplexity's product design surfaces sources in every answer — tight crawl-to-cite feedback loop. GPTBot and Bytespider feed long-run model representation with near-zero short-term citation return.

## Details
Efficiency ranking: PerplexityBot+user-fetcher highest, OAI-SearchBot+ChatGPT-User mid-to-high, Google-Extended low-mid, ClaudeBot+user-fetcher low-mid, GPTBot very low (training-focused, delayed attribution), Bytespider very low. For publishers: Perplexity-targeted optimization is high-leverage relative to fetch volume. Optimize differently: Perplexity needs fetchable well-structured content; Claude needs authoritative primary-research-grounded content. April 2026 point-in-time with quarterly updates.
