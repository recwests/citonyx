## Summary
AI citation unit is the passage (200-400 tokens), not the full page. arXiv study across Brave, Google, Perplexity found document structure alone drives 17.3% average citation improvement. Macro-structure accounts for 44.9% of gain — more than sentence-level quality at 15.4%.

## Details
Every H2 section should open with a direct answer in 40-60 words. AccuraCast: 81% of AI-cited pages include schema markup. Brandofy: articles with definition blocks + FAQ sections + H2/H3 hierarchy cited at 2.4x rate. The "60/30/10" framework: 60% of pages protect, 30% fight for citation, 10% experimental. Metadata + freshness signals show strongest correlation at r=0.68; semantic HTML r=0.65; structured data r=0.63. Meso-structure (paragraph organization) = 39.7% of gain.
