## Summary
Brand mentions correlate 0.664 with AI citation probability vs 0.218 for backlinks — brand presence is 3x more influential. Store-bought backlinks and aggressive link building appear near-worthless for AI citations. Citation architecture decouples from classic domain authority.

## Details
This changes the old SEO authority model. For two decades, links were the main signal. In GEO, third-party mentions matter more. 85% of brand mentions in AI answers come from third-party pages. The mechanism: earned media placements in publications that both human buyers and machines trust build the citation architecture that all engines use. A domain with strong backlinks but thin entity mass and zero third-party mentions still gets zero AI citations. Investment should shift from link acquisition to mention-earning.
