## Summary
Ahrefs analyzed 78.6M ChatGPT queries: Wikipedia accounts for 29.7% of the top 1,000 cited pages, brand homepages 23.8%, app store listings 6.6%. 5W PR's index (680M+ citations across 5 engines): Wikipedia (13.15%) + Reddit (11.97%) drive >25% of all ChatGPT US citations.

## Details
No business press in ChatGPT's top 20 cited sources. Brands with Wikipedia pages earn 3x higher AI citation rates (Frase data). 5W Index revealed Claude biases toward NYT, The Atlantic, The New Yorker, The Economist. BrightEdge: 28% of ChatGPT-cited pages have zero organic search visibility — brand presence and link graphs beat rankings. Brands without Wikipedia presence should build Wikidata entry first (no notability requirement), then PR for Wikipedia.
