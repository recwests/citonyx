## Summary
Kargaev (SSRN, March 2026) quantified SEO vs GEO factor divergence: authority persists but shifts from backlink-centric (NIS 1.000 for SEO) to brand entity mentions (NIS 0.918 for GEO). Content shifts from relevance matching to evidence-bearing: statistics (NIS 0.747), citations (NIS 0.671), fluency (NIS 0.684). Technical hygiene recedes in both.

## Details
Drawn from Aggarwal et al. (2024), Ahrefs 75K brands, Backlinko 11.8M results, Semrush factors. Three core patterns: (1) authority broadens from link-graph to entity signals, (2) source credibility emerges as distinct GEO signal separate from authority, (3) technical hygiene becomes less differentiating. SEO builds infrastructure (crawlable, indexable content), GEO is selection layer (evidence, entity clarity, third-party validation). Brands with strong SEO but poor entity presence will underperform in AI search.
