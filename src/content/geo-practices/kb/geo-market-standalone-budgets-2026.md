## Summary
Presenc AI's 2026 State of GEO report: GEO evolved from experiment to mainstream discipline. Hiring for GEO-specialized roles rose sharply. Budgets carved from existing content/PR/SEO lines rather than added net-new. Tool spend skewed toward monitoring and measurement, not content production.

## Details
Technology, SaaS, and cybersecurity were earliest GEO adopters. Financial services, healthcare, and legal adopted cautiously, framed inside compliance frameworks. Budget benchmarks: brands carve GEO from existing content, PR, and SEO budgets rather than adding net-new lines. Tooling consolidated into clear categories: monitoring/measurement (Profound, Otterly, Peec AI, Brandi), content optimization, and crawler management. The market validates that GEO is primarily a measurement and adjustment discipline layered on top of existing content operations, not a standalone production function.
