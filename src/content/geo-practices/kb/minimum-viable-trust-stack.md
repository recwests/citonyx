## Summary
The SIGI Minimum Viable Trust Stack identifies 10 signals (13% of a 77-signal taxonomy) that account for ~80% of AI citation impact, with ~20 hours total implementation time. Top signals: proprietary data, editorial independence declaration, direct answer in first sentence, statistics, methodology section, question-format H2 headings, entity density >15%.
## Details
Three-phase implementation: Phase 1 (30 min) — verify AI crawler access in robots.txt, add no-paid-placement declaration. Phase 2 (8-12 hours) — rewrite H2s as questions, restructure paragraphs to lead with direct answers containing numbers, increase entity density, add methodology sections. Phase 3 (8-20 hours) — earn verified platform reviews, ensure multi-platform presence on 4+ platforms, produce proprietary research. Category rankings by impact: Content Uniqueness (8.3), Commercial Independence (7.1), Content Structure (6.8), Authorship & Provenance (6.1), Social Proof (6.0). This is a hypothesis-grade framework requiring behavioral validation — but the Pareto prioritization principle is broadly applicable for resource-constrained teams.
