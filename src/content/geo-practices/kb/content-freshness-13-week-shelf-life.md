## Summary
Multiple studies converge: AI citations heavily favor fresh content. ConvertMate 2026: content updated within 30 days gets 3.2x more citations. Ahrefs analysis of 17M citations: AI-cited content 25.7% fresher than organic. ChatGPT shows strongest recency: 76.4% of most-cited pages updated within 30 days. Pages not updated in 3+ months are 3x more likely to lose citations. Effective shelf life for AI citation eligibility ~13 weeks.

## Details
Platform ranking by freshness obsession: Perplexity (most extreme, ~50% current-year), ChatGPT (strong recency), Gemini (balanced), Google AI Overviews (least). Princeton KDD 2024: statistics addition improves visibility by 41%; citing external sources gives +115% for lower-ranked pages. 55% of cited AI Overview snippets appear in first 30% of page (ski-ramp rule). Front-loaded answers in first 150-200 words have 2.5x higher retrieval probability. Pages above 20K chars get 4.3x more citations than under 500 chars — but only if answer-first structured. Implementation: quarterly update cadence for high-value pages. Add visible Last Updated dates and changelogs. Use dateModified schema reflecting real changes. At least 20% substantive revision required.
