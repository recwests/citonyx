## Summary
Stacker and Scrunch research (March 2026) found articles distributed across third-party news sites achieve a median 239% lift in brand citations across AI engines. Separately, Ahrefs' 75,000-brand study found brand mentions are ~3x more influential than backlinks when LLMs decide which brands to cite.

## Details
Earned media accounts for ~25% of all LLM citations per Muck Rack research (March 2026). Most agency GEO retainers do not include a single press placement — this is the missing input. Muck Rack analysis of 1M+ AI citations found 95% of AI-cited links come from non-paid sources. Digital PR is now the primary mechanism for earning AI citations, replacing traditional link building.
