## Summary
A B2B service business implemented comprehensive GEO strategy including llms.txt, full-llms.txt, topical authority clusters, and first-hand data content. AI chat traffic grew to almost match organic search traffic, demonstrating AI-led discovery has become a meaningful acquisition channel.

## Details
Key tactics: llms.txt and full-llms.txt deployment, improved service page clarity and depth, stronger topical authority, first-hand data turned into high-value insight pages, FAQ optimization for conversational queries. The biggest performance driver was original insight-led content based on first-hand data. Combined with clean technical foundations, GEO became a visibility channel performing close to organic search in terms of volume.
