## Summary

The Perplexity brand citation rate is 13.05% versus ChatGPT's 0.59% — a 22x gap. Perplexity averages 8.2 sources per answer (3.4x ChatGPT). Reddit alone accounts for 20-24% of all Perplexity citations. LinkedIn Company Pages receive 59% of Perplexity's LinkedIn citations.

## Details

- Source: Profound (680M citations), Evertune (200M prompts), Tinuiti Q1 2026, Semrush (325K prompts).
- Perplexity's three-layer ML reranking system favors earned media from tier-1 publications.
- Platform passed 230M MAU in Q1 2026; 780M monthly queries.
- Perplexity-referred traffic converts at 3.1x rate of Google organic.
