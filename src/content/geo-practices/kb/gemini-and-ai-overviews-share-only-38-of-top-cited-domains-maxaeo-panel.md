## Summary
MaxAEO's April–June 2026 panel of 1,200 buyer-intent prompts across 60 B2B brands found only 38% overlap: a brand appearing in AI Overviews appeared in Gemini's parallel answer only 38% of the time. Gemini cites median 3.4 sources vs 6.8 for AI Overviews.

## Details
92% of Gemini's cited domains mapped to a groundingSupports segment — sources are genuinely used, not decorative. Gemini leans editorial: Reddit/forums made up 6% of citations vs 14% for AIO and 19% for Perplexity. Entity strength correlates: 71% of Gemini-cited brands had a Knowledge Panel or Wikipedia entity vs 52% for AIO-only brands. Optimizing for one does not guarantee the other.
