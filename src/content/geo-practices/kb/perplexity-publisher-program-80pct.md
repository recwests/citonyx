## Summary
Unlike OpenAI's flat-fee licensing deals, Perplexity pays per usage when content is cited in an answer or via Comet Plus subscription split. Premium-tier citations count ~3x; quality multiplier up to +50% for entity density/recency.

## Details
Analyst estimates: ~$8–15 per 1,000 citations; mid-tier publisher ~$5k–15k/mo. Onboarding includes a content-quality + structured-data review; Perplexity can remove publishers for manipulation. Open to enrolled partners (2,400+ by Q1 2026). This reframes citations as a revenue channel, not just traffic.
