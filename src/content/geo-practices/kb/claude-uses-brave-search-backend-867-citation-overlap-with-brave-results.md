## Summary
Claude's live-web research uses Brave Search's independent indexing. Profound (2025) found 86.7% overlap between Claude-cited URLs and Brave's top 10 organic results. Brave does not license results from Google or Bing — maintains its own crawl and ranking system. This means Google SEO alone will NOT guarantee Claude visibility. Brave prioritizes privacy-respecting sites (minimal tracking scripts, cookie walls, fingerprinting). Entity verification carries 30% of Claude's citation weight.
