## Summary
Optimize images (alt text, ImageObject schema), video (transcripts, VideoObject), audio (podcast transcripts). Early movers face near-zero competition for visual/audio citations. Wellows reports schema + multimodal content yields a +317% citation lift.

## Details
Gemini is natively multimodal and evaluates entities across text/visual/video simultaneously. Averi's 4-layer template (text + image + video + layered schema) shows one pillar can surface differently across ChatGPT/Perplexity/AI Overviews. Image alt text and descriptive filenames feed cross-modal embeddings.
