## Summary

Google's Gemini 3 model reshuffled about 42% of cited domains and collapsed top-10 citation overlap from 76% (mid-2025) to 38% (Q1 2026). Fan-out sub-query mapping is now the dominant citation mechanism.

## Details

Three structural shifts drove the collapse: deeper query fan-out (more sub-queries per user query), multi-modal source weighting elevating YouTube and Reddit, and topical-authority filtering over query-specific ranking. 31.2% of citations now come from positions 11-100, 31.0% from beyond rank 100. YouTube is the top cited domain (18.2% of outside-top-100 citations, 34% growth in 6 months). SEO Francisco case study: entity graph plus JSON-LD plus citation-ready content achieved 340% citation growth.
