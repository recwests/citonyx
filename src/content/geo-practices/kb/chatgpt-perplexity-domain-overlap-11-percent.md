## Summary
Engines disagree because each retrieves from its own index and weighs sources differently. A win on one engine barely predicts the other. Brand-level overlap (36–55%) is higher than source-level — so build entity/brand authority everywhere, but track citations per platform.

## Details
Perplexity: Reddit-led, 21.87 citations/answer (most), recency-weighted. ChatGPT: Wikipedia-led (47.9% of top-10), training-data priors, mentions brands without links. Gemini: Knowledge Graph + Google index, 52% first-party brand domains. Claude: conservative, cites NYT/Atlantic/Economist, fewer citations. Build the prompt universe per engine and audit monthly.
