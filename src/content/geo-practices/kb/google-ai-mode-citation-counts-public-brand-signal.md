## Summary
Google AI Mode publicly displays citation count and favicons per answer (tested late June 2026, reported July 1). Google cites 'cleaner reading experience' but effectively makes citation count a visible brand trust metric across ~200M+ users on 48% of tracked queries. SparkToro/Similarweb 2026: 68% of US searches end in zero clicks. When AI Overview appears, organic CTR drops ~60%.

## Details
Only 27.6% of clicks reach the open web. This transforms citation count from an internal SEO metric into a public-facing brand credibility signal visible to every user. For brands: being cited in Google AI Mode is now directly observable by consumers, making GEO a reputation management function as much as a traffic strategy. Brands with zero AI Mode citations are visibly absent in a public way.
