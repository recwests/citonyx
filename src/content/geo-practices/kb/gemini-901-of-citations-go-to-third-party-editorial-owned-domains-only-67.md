## Summary
Omnia's citation database (May 22–June 16, 2026) shows 90.1% of Gemini citations go to third-party editorial and independent web content. Owned domains account for only 6.7%, FAQ pages 0.09%. Average 4.28 distinct domains cited per answer — fewer slots than any other major engine.

## Details
Blog/article pages (32.74%) and review/comparison pages (12.17%) together account for ~45% of Gemini citations. Product pages (0.32%), pricing pages (0.14%), and FAQ pages (0.09%) are negligible. FAQ schema does not trigger Gemini citations — Google's own guidance confirms schema does not determine AI answer inclusion. Strategy implication: the content decisions that move Gemini citations are primarily about third-party editorial placements, not optimizing owned pages.
