## Summary
A one-van removals firm with no backlinks and a 4-day-old domain scored higher than nationally-known brands in ChatGPT's blind evaluation. Key: 9 AI Discovery Files (llms.txt, ai.txt, ai.json, identity.json) rendered from the same data layer as the visible site.

## Details
ChatGPT rated the site 99/100 on useful tools. The site shipped the full suite of 9 AI Discovery Files at root: llms.txt, llms.html, ai.txt, ai.json, identity.json, brand.txt, faq-ai.txt, developer-ai.txt, and robots-ai.txt following ai-visibility.org.uk specification. Critical: rendering from same data layer as the visible page means schema and JSON-LD cannot contradict each other. 844K+ adopters for llms.txt.

However, Ahrefs' June 2026 study of 137K domains found 97% of llms.txt files receive zero AI requests. ALLMO.ai analyzed 94,614 cited URLs — only 1 was an llms.txt page (0.001%). Thunderbit's Tranco Top 10K crawl: 5.86% valid adoption. Search Engine Land's 10-site test: 2 saw marginal improvement, 8 saw none, 1 declined. The one audience that reads llms.txt is coding agents (Cursor, Claude Code). Adoption ≠ impact. Proposed IETF draft in working group review.
