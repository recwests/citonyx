## Summary
Brands failing entity-resolution pass are excluded before passage scoring, regardless of rankings. Coherent name/URL/description across Wikidata, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, and Organization schema triggers entity merge.

## Details
Google's Knowledge Graph holds 500B+ facts on 5B+ entities and Gemini trains on it. June 2025 purge removed 3B+ entities — quality/consistency of signals now matters more than ever. Knowledge Panels correlate with 3–5x more AI citations. sameAs schema + Wikidata QID are the underused levers.
