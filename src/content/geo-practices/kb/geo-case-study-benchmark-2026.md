## Summary
Accumulated GEO case studies: Presenc AI (manufacturing 2,300%, fintech 7x, legal 312%), Broworks (10% total organic from LLMs, 27% SQL conversion), Fuel Online (+140% high-intent demo requests), Go Fish Digital (3-month campaign 3×'d leads via answer-first content + FAQPage schema + topical authority clusters).

## Details
Four patterns across all successful case studies: (1) content restructuring into answer-first/extractable format, (2) original research and proprietary data, (3) schema markup implementation, (4) earned media/third-party coverage. Broworks now generates 10% of total organic traffic from generative engines with 27% converting to SQLs. Fuel Online reported +140% demo requests. Go Fish Digital's 3-month GEO campaign tripled leads via answer-first content, FAQPage schema, and external citation building. TokenMinds Kadena case study and a SaaS client achieving 20+ free trial signups/month from ChatGPT citations further confirm the pattern. Brands implementing at least 3 of 4 patterns see lift within 90-180 days.
