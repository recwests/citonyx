## Summary
Generic advice isn't cited; a named, specific, ownable framework is. This raises the odds an AI attributes the method to your brand when summarizing a category.

## Details
Combine with answer-first structure and statistics. The tactic pairs with "earned third-party mentions" — directories (Clutch, G2, Capterra, DesignRush) and roundups are high-leverage because LLMs lift competitor roundup pages; getting listed + reviewed often beats pure content strategy.
