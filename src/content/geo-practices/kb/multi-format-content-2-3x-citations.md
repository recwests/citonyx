## Summary
Analysis of 10,000 AI Overview results: definitive guides (35%), comparison content (28%), how-to guides (22%) dominate citations. Content combining formats earns 2.3x more citations. Video transcripts now regularly cited alongside text sources.

## Details
Google's 2026 update gave significant weight to video transcripts/captions. Well-optimized YouTube videos with accurate captions and structured timestamps can earn citations even without text ranking. Perplexity: pages with comparison tables (2+ Markdown-formatted) receive citations 4.1x more frequently than text-only equivalents. Perplexity cites Reddit discussion threads at 3.2x ChatGPT's rate. For Google AI Overviews: FAQPage schema, HowTo schema, and Article schema confirmed as important for inclusion. Multi-format pages combine pillar guide + comparison table + step-by-step instructions.
