## Summary
The Gemini 3 upgrade (January 2026) replaced 42% of domains the model previously cited and began generating 32% more source URLs per response. Citation patterns are now less stable: brands appearing as first citation one day may not appear the next.

## Details
SE Ranking data shows the Gemini 3 upgrade was the most significant citation behavior shift Google has made. 42% of previously cited domains were dropped and new domains entered the citation pool. 32% more source URLs per answer means more slots but also more competition. Citation volatility increased significantly — brands need to track Gemini citation rates continuously and expect disruption around model updates.
