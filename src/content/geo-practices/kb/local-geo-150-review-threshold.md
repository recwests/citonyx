## Summary
AI local search results show a 150+ review threshold for consistent visibility. Businesses below this count are rarely surfaced regardless of proximity. Review volume functions as a binary eligibility gate for AI local citation, not just a ranking factor.

## Details
The threshold effect means local businesses must treat review acquisition as a prerequisite for AI visibility, not just a conversion signal. Below 150 reviews, even optimized GBP listings may be excluded from AI local responses. Above the threshold, review depth (detailed, keyword-rich reviews) and recency (reviews within 90 days) become differentiating factors. For the Citonyx testbed: if targeting local queries, prioritize review volume growth to 150+ across GBP and secondary platforms. Review velocity (rate of new reviews) signals ongoing relevance to AI systems. A consistent 5-10 new reviews per month is baseline.
