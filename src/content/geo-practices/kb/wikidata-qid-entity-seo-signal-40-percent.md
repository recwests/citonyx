## Summary
Brand entity optimization replaces pure keyword SEO in 2026. Wikidata has no notability requirement — any legitimate business can create an entry with a QID. Brands with Wikidata entries are cited 40% more. Organization schema sameAs linking Wikidata QID is the single most underused high-leverage signal.

## Details
Google's Knowledge Graph now holds 500B+ facts on 5B+ entities — Gemini is trained on it. Ahrefs' 1,885-page study found sameAs links to authoritative external sources (including Wikidata) significantly predicted AI Overview citation. knowsAbout declarations aligned to Wikidata Q-IDs produced citations at nearly 2x rate for domain-specific queries. The four-layer entity stack: Entity home → Wikidata QID → sameAs schema → corroboration (PR, reviews, mentions). Onely analysis: brand mentions correlate with AI visibility at 0.664 vs 0.218 for backlinks. Most brands underbuild the identity layer.
