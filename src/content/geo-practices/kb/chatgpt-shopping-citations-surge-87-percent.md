## Summary
ChatGPT's share of shopping-related query citations surged from 8% to 87% in 5 months. ChatGPT now commands 87.4% of all AI referral traffic. AI-referred traffic converts at 30-40% — far exceeding organic referral conversion.

## Details
KIME tracked ChatGPT's shopping citation rate growth representing a structural shift in product discovery. VentureBeat (Apr 2026) reports AI-referred traffic converts at 30-40%. Ahrefs found AI-referred traffic converts at 2x+ traditional organic. HubSpot's 2026 State of Marketing: 58% of marketers report AI-referred visitors convert at higher rates; HubSpot itself reports ~3x better lead conversion from AEO sources. Conversion rate from AI-referred visitors: +31% vs organic in ecommerce case study. McKinsey: 50% of consumers already use AI for product discovery; $750B in consumer spend at risk by 2028. For ecommerce brands: GEO investment for shopping queries is now urgent.
