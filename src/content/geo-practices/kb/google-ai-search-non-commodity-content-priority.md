## Summary
Google's July 2026 AI search guidance explicitly distinguishes commodity content (generic summaries) from non-commodity content (first-hand experience, specific outcomes). First-hand accounts with real experience prioritized. Reviews describing specific services and outcomes carry more weight than generic ratings.

## Details
Example: 'Why We Waived the Inspection and Saved Money' beats generic advice articles. Covers crawlability, mobile usability, page speed as technical floor. Local: Google Business Profile, citation consistency, specific reviews matter most. Google explicitly states AI features use the same Search index and ranking systems as traditional search — no special AI schema or separate protocol required. Schema markup still useful for structured data but not required for AI visibility.
