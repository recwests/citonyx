## Summary
Yext's analysis of 17.2M citations across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude found verified/structured data accounts for 54.53% of all distinct citation sources. Websites generated 4.31 citations per URL. Listings represented 54.53% of distinct sources.

## Details
The 17.2M citation study is one of the largest cross-platform citation audits. It confirms that structured, verified data (business listings, schema markup, knowledge graph entries) is the dominant source type across all major AI engines. Listings — structured data about entities — accounted for more than half of all distinct citation sources. For brands, this means ensuring structured data accuracy across all platforms is the single highest-impact GEO tactic, outpacing content creation alone.
