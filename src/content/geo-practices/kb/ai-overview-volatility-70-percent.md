## Summary
AI Overviews are highly volatile: only 28.4% of keywords show stable AIO presence. 7% show extreme volatility (appear/disappear daily). Presence volatility (0.586) is the main driver — Google still testing WHEN AIO should trigger.

## Details
Serpstat analysis: Low volatility 28.4%, Moderate 33.5%, High 31.1%, Extreme 7%. Content volatility (0.279) lower — consistent trusted source pool once AIO decides to show. Only 4-8% of AIO citations overlap with organic Top 20 — in 90%+ of cases, AI answers cite pages that don't rank well organically. Category bias: Health ~54%, Finance and YMYL high; Shopping, Sports, visual/real-time queries see far less AIO penetration. 13.14% of all queries triggered AI Overviews in March 2025, almost doubling in under 3 months. For practitioners: monitor AIO presence separate from organic rankings.
