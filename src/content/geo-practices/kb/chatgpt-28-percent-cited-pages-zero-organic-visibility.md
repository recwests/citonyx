## Summary
28.3% of ChatGPT's most-cited pages have zero organic visibility in Google. Under 10% of sources cited in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot rank in Google's top 10 for the same query. Strong SEO no longer guarantees AI visibility — the two systems are fundamentally decoupled. Separate GEO and SEO tracks are essential.

## Details
This Ahrefs data point is the defining GEO problem of 2026: SEO rankings and AI citations are separate systems with separate signals. Only 10% overlap exists between most-cited and highest-traffic pages from ChatGPT. The actionable pages — tools, signups, trials — drive traffic even though they are not the most-cited. Brands need two-track optimization: SEO for traditional search, GEO for AI citation. The decoupling creates opportunity for brands with weak SEO but strong brand signals.
