## Summary
Jaeckert & O'Daniel's AI visibility tool J.O.E. went from 6.8% to 46.7% visibility in 7 days for transactional prompts. Actions: published new landing pages, revised existing articles, developed YouTube content, published guest article. First pages appeared in AI monitoring after 3 days.

## Details
Key insight: GEO often moves faster than SEO because AI systems react quickly to changes in indexed content. Many SEO measures (specific content, updating existing content, good indexing, multilingualism) work well for GEO. The approach focused on transactional prompts with "I am looking for..." formulation — fully formed sentences, not keywords. When working on AI visibility, you often work on SEO visibility simultaneously. This demonstrates that structured, prompt-aligned content creation yields rapid AI citation results.
