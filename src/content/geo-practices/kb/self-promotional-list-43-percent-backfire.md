## Summary
Ahrefs experiment: 34 self-promotional pages, 9,886 AI answers tracked Feb-May 2026. Self-promotional listicles got cited but 43% of time AI recommended a competitor from same list. Citations were temporary — appearing only 1 in 3 eligible days. Only effective for new brands where AI has a knowledge gap.
## Details
For established brands, 94% of new mentions came from third-party content, not self-promo. Self-promotional listicles work as a temporary awareness play for new brands with zero AI presence, but the backfire risk is high — every list you create is also a citation source for your competitors. The 43% competitor recommendation rate means nearly half the time, the AI reads your listicle and recommends someone else. Citations were highly volatile: appearing on only 33% of eligible days. Better strategy: earn third-party citations through PR, original research, and genuine community presence.
