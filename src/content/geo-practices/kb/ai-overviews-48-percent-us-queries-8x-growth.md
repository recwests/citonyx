## Summary
Google AI Overviews now appear on ~48-55% of all US search queries — up from 6.49% in January 2025, an ~8x expansion in 15 months. Google confirmed "roughly 50% of US queries" in February 2026. Healthcare triggers at 88%, Education 83%, Entertainment 37%.

## Details
BrightEdge (9-industry tracker) and Axis Intelligence document the expansion to 48-55%. Organic CTR collapsed from 1.76% to 0.61% on AIO-present queries. Top-10 overlap fell from 76% to 38% in 7 months — 31.2% of cited pages rank 11-100 and 31% beyond position 100. Schema-marked pages are cited 2.3× more. Gemini 3 query fan-out expands queries into 8-20+ sub-queries. Cited brands earn 35% higher organic CTR and 91% higher paid CTR. Ahrefs reported AIO visitors convert at 23x (12.1% of signups from 0.5% of traffic). However, AIO citations perform at roughly Position 6 click levels. Even queries without AIOs saw 41% CTR decline YoY, confirming structural shift.
