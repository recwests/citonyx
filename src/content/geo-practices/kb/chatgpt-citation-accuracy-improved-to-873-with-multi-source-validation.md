## Summary

ChatGPT citation accuracy improved from 68% (2023) to 87.3% (April 2026). Multi-source validation requires corroboration across 2+ independent sources before citing a claim with high confidence. This cross-referencing reduced citation errors by 43%. Inferred citations (from training data) have 94.2% accuracy rate.

## Details

Three major inflection points: RAG in mid-2024 (76.9%), publisher partnerships + fact verification in early 2025 (82.1%), multi-source validation in April 2026 (87.3%). Only 14% of ChatGPT-generated citations link to verifiable sources as of 2026. Fact-checking layer catches 78% of potential errors before reaching users.
