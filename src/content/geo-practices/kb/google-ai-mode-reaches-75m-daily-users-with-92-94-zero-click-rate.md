## Summary

Google AI Mode launched to general availability in May 2026 and reached 75M daily users within weeks. Usage grew ~4x in 2 months. Zero-click rate is 92-94%, average query length 7.22 words (2x traditional Google), and only 1-3 sources cited per response. External domain visits per session dropped to 6-8%.

## Details

AI Mode compresses source selection dramatically. Traditional Google offered 10+ organic results; AI Mode cites 1-3 sources. Citation share replaces rank tracking. Average session time is 49 seconds (77 sec for brand comparison queries). Brands must track 'Share of Model' instead of traditional SERP position.
