## Summary
A multi-channel GEO program lifted a B2B SaaS brand from 12% to 87% AI citation rate across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews in 4 months. The brand had strong organic SEO but near-zero AI citation presence.

## Details
The 4-phase strategy included: (1) 14 PR placements in Inc, HBR, Forbes, PMI; (2) G2 reviews from 43 to 287 with 4.7 average rating; (3) Reddit community strategy in 8 subreddits; (4) comparison hub vs 12 competitors; (5) original research (n=500 survey). Results: 87% citation rate, 890% Perplexity traffic increase, 67% brand search growth, 34% trial signups lift.
