## Summary
Semrush's 2026 AI Visibility Index analyzed 126 million US AI search prompts across 22 industries (Jan–Apr 2026). Findings: 45% of marketing leaders cannot accurately measure brand visibility in AI answers; only 9% have tools to track all relevant metrics. AI traffic to US retail surged 1,324% (Oct 2024–May 2026).

## Details
The study tracks brand mentions, citations, and sentiment across ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini. Provides benchmarks across 22 industries. Brand-mention similarity analysis between platforms reveals low overlap, confirming need for multi-platform optimization. Key insight: 'Your AI narrative is becoming the decisive entry point to your customer experience — your customers are both people and AI agents.'
