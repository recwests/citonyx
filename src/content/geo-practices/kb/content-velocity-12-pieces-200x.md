## Summary
Brandi AI (Feb 2026) found brands publishing 12+ optimized pieces per month gain AI visibility up to 200x faster than those publishing 4/month. Content velocity compounds with AI freshness preferences — more frequent publishing creates more citation opportunities and fresher content signals.

## Details
The 200x figure reflects the compounding effect of multiple factors: each new piece creates an additional citation surface area; frequent updates signal freshness, which AI engines heavily weight (content under 30 days gets ~3.2x more citations); and broader topical coverage builds entity authority. The implication is clear: sporadic or low-volume content strategies cannot compete in AI search. A consistent pipeline of 3+ pieces per week targeting specific AI queries outperforms occasional high-effort posts.
