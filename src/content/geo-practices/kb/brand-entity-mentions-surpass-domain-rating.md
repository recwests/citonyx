## Summary
First study comparing SEO vs GEO factor families on a normalized Divergence Index. Brand Entity Mentions (NIS 0.918) vs Domain Rating (NIS 0.397) show entity-based signals dominate generative search authority. Authority persists across paradigms (DI +0.136) but its form broadens from link-graph to brand/entity salience.

## Details
The SEO-to-GEO Gap paper (Dmitry Kargaev, SSRN, March 2026) builds from Aggarwal et al. (KDD 2024), Ahrefs' 75,000-brand study, Backlinko, and Semrush. Brand Search Volume scores 0.547. Content quality is important in both, but GEO rewards evidence-bearing content (statistics, citations, fluency) rather than relevance matching and length. Technical signals (HTTPS, page speed) are near-null differentiators. SEO builds infrastructure, GEO builds selection advantage on top — sequential layers, not competing paradigms.
