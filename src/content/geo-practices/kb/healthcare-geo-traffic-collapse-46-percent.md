## Summary
Health vertical hardest hit by AI Overviews: 34-46% traffic loss as 63-85% of health queries answered directly. New monetization models emerging: licensed content marketplaces, AI ads, content protection beyond blocking.

## Details
Doceree reports healthcare publishers facing existential traffic decline. 69% of all Google searches now end without a click (Similarweb). Health vertical: 54% AIO share — highest of any category. New publisher strategies: (1) AI ads — contextual display in chat interfaces; (2) Licensed Content Marketplace — tokenized content licensing contracts; (3) AI content protection beyond blocking — controlling how content is accessed and monetized. HIPAA/GDPR concerns drive different blocking patterns in health. Recommended: implement MedicalWebPage, FAQ, HowTo schema; ensure named medical authors; maintain factual accuracy for AI trust signals. Healthcare GEO requires fundamentally different strategy than other verticals.
