## Summary
Wellows analysis of 2,400 citations found 96% of AI Overview citations come from sources with strong E-E-A-T signals. The Authority Ladder: vector embedding alignment (r=0.84) → Entity KG density (r=0.76) → brand mentions (r=0.392) → brand search volume (r=0.334). Domain age of ChatGPT-cited sources averages 17 years. E-E-A-T has crossed from ranking factor to binary gatekeeper.

## Details
Only 38% of AI Overview citations now come from top-10 organic pages (down from 76%). The remaining 62% draw from deeper content with strong authority signals. AI search referrals grew 527% YoY in H1 2025. Bowens Authority Ladder framework confirms structured entity signals outweigh raw brand fame. Dataset schema for original research gives up to +43% visibility. llms.txt adoption emerging as highest-leverage config file for authority signaling. Remaining 4% of citations without strong E-E-A-T confirms the gate is nearly absolute. For brands: invest in Knowledge Graph entity density and verifiable author expertise before chasing citation volume.
