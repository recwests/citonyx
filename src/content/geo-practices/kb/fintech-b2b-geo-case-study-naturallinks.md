## Summary
Natural Links fintech B2B GEO program: 28 earned media placements, 40+ comparison pages, FinancialProduct/FAQ/HowTo schema, 2 original research reports, Wikidata entity, and structured content refresh. Results: +61% branded AI mentions, 189% comparison page traffic, AI-assisted discovery from 4% to 19% of inbound leads, 22% reduction in deal cycle.

## Details
The program combined traditional digital PR (28 earned media placements) with structured GEO content: 40+ comparison pages targeting competitor and alternative queries, FinancialProduct + FAQ + HowTo schema markup, two original research reports for data authority, and Wikidata entity establishment. The 22% deal cycle reduction suggests AI citations accelerate B2B buying decisions by providing pre-qualified information. Comparison pages emerged as the highest-ROI content type for fintech B2B.
