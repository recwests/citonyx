## Summary
Digital PR is the widest evidence-to-adoption gap in GEO: 25% of all LLM citations come from earned media, yet only 6% of practitioners use it as a primary tactic. Stacker/Scrunch (March 2026) found distributing content across third-party news sites produced a median 239% lift in AI citations.

## Details
Six PR tactics compound into AI citations: (1) original research — highest return, creates citable reference pages; (2) expert commentary via HARO/Qwoted; (3) wire syndication for distributed mention footprint; (4) executive thought leadership with Author schema; (5) reactive newsjacking for real-time retrieval feeds; (6) trade bylines for niche topical authority. 95% of AI-cited links come from non-paid sources. The gap suggests a massive first-mover opportunity for brands investing in Digital PR for GEO.
