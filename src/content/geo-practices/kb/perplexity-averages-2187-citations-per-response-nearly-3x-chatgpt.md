## Summary
Qwairy's analysis of 118,000 AI responses (Jan-Mar 2026): Perplexity averages 21.87 citations per response vs ChatGPT 7.92, Google AI Mode 8.34, Claude 5.67. Perplexity cites 32% second-tier sources (niche blogs, mid-tier pubs) vs ChatGPT 24%, Google AI Mode 19%, Claude 21%. Only 11% of cited domains overlap across engines. Content published in last 30 days has 82% citation rate on Perplexity vs ~55% on ChatGPT.

## Details
Perplexity source mix: news/publisher 42%, niche authority 35%, academic/research 12%, government/institutional 11%. 35% niche authority share is highest among all engines, making Perplexity the most accessible for specialized brands. Domain-level authority matters more than page-level. Branded mentions correlate with Perplexity citation at 0.664 Spearman. Freshness = ~40% of ranking weight. Reddit = 6.6% of all citations. Wikipedia = ~0% on Perplexity (opposite of ChatGPT's 16.3%).
