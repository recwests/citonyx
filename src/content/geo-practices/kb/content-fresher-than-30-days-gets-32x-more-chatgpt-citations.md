## Summary

GrowByData's 2026 study shows content updated within 30 days earns 3.2x more ChatGPT citations. Perplexity shows 2.6x lift for fresh content, Gemini 2.1x, Google AI Overviews 1.8x, and Claude only 1.3x. Key signals: dateModified in JSON-LD, visible 'Last updated' in body, and genuine content changes.

## Details

The freshness effect splits engines into two camps. ChatGPT and Perplexity are highly recency-sensitive (news, tech, finance queries). Claude weights authority more. 'Fake fresh' backfires — changing only the date without substantive content update lowers trust signals. Cross-reference visible date with dateModified schema to avoid mismatch penalties.
